TUBA Breast Augmentation
Jan 22, 2018
First of all a saline implant must be used. This is not as lifelike as a silicone gel implant. Any postoperative complications will usually require another additional incision and resultant scar. The technique is not as precise a procedure compared to the standard approaches.
