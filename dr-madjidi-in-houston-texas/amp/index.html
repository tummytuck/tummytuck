Apr 24, 2020
The number of cc is not the most important in saying of the implants are too big or not . It’s the diameter of the implant that needs to fit you chest width. If implants are wider that the width of you breast on each side, you will either have the breasts too close together (symmastia) or the implant will go under your arm pit! The width of you chest will tell your doctor the largest implant your body can accommodate.
