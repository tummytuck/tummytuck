Raised Breast Veins through Areola after Subglandular Breast Implant Augmentation?
Visible Breast Veins after Breast Augmentation
Jan 15, 2014
It is common for veins to become engorged after augmentation in the post-operative period of about a month. Some women are naturally more “veiny” than others and this is a common site to see them. I do not recommend having them removed as they are doing an important job. These veins are too large in caiber to sclerose. I’m sorry that there are not any great options!
3 weeks post op and feel constantly thirsty. Is this a side effect of the surgery?
Surgery Side Effects
Jan 15, 2014
Feeling thirsty in the first few days after surgery is quite common and is most likely due to dehydration or fluid shifts in your body. To still be feeling like this 3 weeks after surgery is unusual and should be evaluated by your primary medical doctor to ensure that you don’t have a new onset of diabetes or other medical problem. Best wishes!
