Tummy Tuck Should Remove Most Stretch Marks

A tummy tuck should remove the majority of your stretch marks which are at or below your umbilicus. In addition the muscle tightening should largely correct the laxity in your central abdomen.

Obtain a consultation with a BC plastic surgeon with whom you can discuss your options as well as the benefits and risks. (John Whitt, MD (retired), Louisville Plastic Surgeon)

I think you are an excellent candidate for tummy tuck surgery. This operation will serve to remove “a lot of extra skin” and reapproximate the abdominal wall muscles that may have spread with weight gain/loss and/or pregnancies.

Pictures of tummy tuck procedure

This will tighten up the abdominal wall and help narrow the waistline. The pubic area can also be addressed if necessary. The loose skin and stretch marks from the belly button down will be removed. Some of the stretch marks above the belly button and along the flank areas will remain, but will be lowered. The incision can be kept low and hidden and undergarments/swimming suit.

You are lucky in that the quality of the skin of your upper abdomen is quite nice; this skin will be “redraped” and should contribute to a very nice end result. I think you may be a little too hard on yourself; I doubt that you have 25 pounds to lose. Once you are at your long-term stable weight and have completed pregnancies (if planned), seek consultation with well experienced board-certified plastic surgeons. Make sure you see lots of examples of the work while you do your due diligence. (Tom J. Pousti, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

Tummy tuck will remove your loose skin and most stretch marks

Pictures of tummy tuck surgery

Based on your photos, you appear to be a great candidate for a tummy tuck. Your stretch marks from your belly button down to your pubic area will be removed. The small amount of stretch marks just above your belly button will still be with you, however, they will be much lower after the surgery – close to your pubic area. I’m not sure you need to lose 25 pounds, but I would consult with a board certified plastic surgeon in person. (William Bruno, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)

You are a great candidate for a tummy tuck. Dont worry about losing any weight before, you look thin enough. All of the stretch marks under your belly button should be removed and with tightening the abdominal muscles, you will have a nice flat tummy. (William B. Rosenblatt, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)
