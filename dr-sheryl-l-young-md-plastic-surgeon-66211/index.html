Could a breast lift or implants fix sternum appears to be sticking out of my chest?
Breast Lift or Implants after a Breast Reduction.
Dec 8, 2019
Hi! Thank you for this question. Congratulations on your weight loss. You should return to see your plastic surgeon. Looking at you photos, without an examination, I would likely recommend an implant and possibly removing some skin. I would have a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon examine you and make you a plan to correct your breast. Best of luck!

Dr. Sheryl Young

I am getting implants and I am not sure if I want to do a lift. Do I really need one?
Augmentation and Lift
Nov 16, 2019
Hi! This is a difficult question to answer because it depends on you post op expectations and how you feel about scars. If your expecting a high round breast you probably need a lift. This can be done in 2 separate surgeries if your not sure. You should discuss this indetail with your surgeon. Good luck!
Is it normal for my implants to still look like this 6 weeks out? What can I do?
Breast Implants are High
Nov 16, 2019
It is very normal for implants to remain high a month after a breast augmetation. Please follow your surgeons protecol. You might consider serial photographs to help you follow the implants postitions. Sometimes its hard to tell they are slowly falling when you see them daily. Good luck!
What kind of local anaesthesia is usually used for doing blepharoplasty? And what kind of oral seda
Eyelid Surgery Anesthesia
Nov 16, 2019
Thank you for your question. This decision should be made in a consultation with your Board Certified in Plastic Surgery Surgeon. I like to discuss this with my patient. Depending on the complexity of the surgery, sometimes local anesthesia is appropriate. If your a person who can't stay still, I would recommend a general anesthetic. Please discuss this at your consult.
If my white cells are too elevated can I still get a breast augmentation?
Elevated White Cells and Breast Augmentation
Nov 4, 2019
Thank you for this question. I would not recommend having any elective surgery including a breast augmentation with an elevated White Blood Cell count. I want only the best out comes for my patients. I think this would put you at increased risk for a post operative infection. Please see your primary doctor and have this worked up.

Best wishes, Dr. Sheryl Young, MD

Scars after Breast Reduction
Nov 4, 2019
Hi! You have a frequently asked question that is important to address. It is normal for bundles of tissue to form when healing. In my patients I recommend massage, taping or silicone patches. I would recommend you see your surgeon for an examination and some follow up of your scar.
Best Wishes, Dr. Young
I am a very young looking 65 year old. Other than hypothyroid I am in pretty good health. Am I too
Age and tummy tucks
Nov 2, 2019
I like to think you are as young as you feel. For elective surgery you should be healthy and a non smoker. Your health is more important than your age. I would recommend you see your primary physician for a heath screening and then have a consult with a plastic surgeon.

Skin removal after weight loss

Is is possible to have surgery without steri strips or glue?
Steri Strips are not needed
Nov 1, 2019
With a good closure there is no need for steri stips or glue. I like to use it to prevent micromovement in the scar, but it is not necessary. You should discuss this with your plastic surgeon.
I hope your surgery goes well.
Dr. Young
Do I need a lift? If so, should I get implants now and a lift later since I will be doing rhinoplas
Augmentation and lift
Oct 27, 2019
Thank you for your question. I assume you are looking for a higher, rounder appearance to your breast. An augmentation and lift would most likely be the best procedure to achieve this goal. The procedures can safely be done together, although you decrease some of the risks by separating them. I encourage you to have a conversation with your surgeon about the risk and benefits of the combination procedure and combining your breast surgery with a rhinoplasty.
Bra or no bra after BA? And how long?
Bra or no Bra
Oct 24, 2019
HI! I like my patients to wear compression post op because I think it makes them more comfortable. The compression can be a surgical bra, sports bra or cami. I do not want them to wear a bra that lifts the breast because I like my implants to settle especially if they are under the muscle. You should ask you surgeon for their protocol.
Will cutting the incision smaller make your boobs higher?
Augmentation incision lenght
Oct 24, 2019
Hi! The length of the incision is not related to the placement of the implant. In general, a silicone gel implant requires a slightly longer incision than a saline filled implant. You should have a discussion with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss all the factors that influence what the implants will look like after an augmentation. Thanks for your question.
Wanting a new body. Will a tummy tuck and liposuction help after VSG?
Tummy Tuck after weight loss surgery
Oct 24, 2019
Thank you for your question. I feel you are a good candidate for a tummy tuck with liposuction. I like my patients to be at their idea or stable weight for about 6 months prior to the procedure. If you loose weight after the procedure your result will not be as good. I like to look at the harmony of the abdomen with the back and mons pubis to determine the best scar location. I think you are ready for a consultation so make an appointment.
Breast augmentation - medium or high profile in round implants considering my anatomy?
Choosing an implant for augmentation
Oct 20, 2019
Thank you for asking this question. Implants come in a variety of sizes and shapes. I feel your surgeon should guide your decision. Implants should be chosen based on your post operative size goal and chest wall width. You also have choices as to the type, size and brand of the implant. I feel my primary roll as a surgeon is to discuss this with you at your consultation.
