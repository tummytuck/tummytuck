Exercise after tummy tuck

Before and after tummy tuck in San Jose

You can discharged from the hospital in a day after a tummy tuck in San Jose. Going to work depends on your state of health and usually occurs after 2 weeks. But from the physical activity is better to abstain, because it can slow down the healing process. After 1.5-2 months, the patient is almost is restored and can lead an active lifestyle and do exercise.

For better preserve results of tummy tuck, doctors recommend control your weight. As a result of pregnancy after abdominoplasty, tummy can stretch again, and negate the efforts of the surgeon, so that if a woman is planning to become pregnant, it is best to postpone surgery for a more favorable time.
