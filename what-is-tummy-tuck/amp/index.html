What is tummy tuck surgery

Tummy tuck is one of the most popular plastic surgery. Alas, it can be necessary even to young women, for example, if the body cope with the last pregnancy is not as easy as we would like. Today tummy tuck is one of the most common surgeries among men who want to get rid of excess weight. Plastic surgeon uses an individual approach to each patient by assigning one or another kind correction of abdomen.

Stretched skin tummy tuck

Abdominoplasty – a short way to a beautiful belly. Let’s start with the fact that tummy tuck called abdominoplasty and there are several kinds of surgery.

Classical tummy tuck is considered the most complete. This surgery is performed under general anesthesia and takes between 2 to 4 hours depending on the complexity of the situation. During the procedure, the plastic surgeon performs a long incision from one hip to the other (that the seam from the surgery was less noticeable. It hided in the so-called “bikini line”). Next removed the extra skin and fat, and the abdominal muscles are sewn in the new position.

Belly button after tummy tuck

Feature of classical tummy tuck consider the removal of excess tissue and fat from the upper and lower abdomen and navel transfer to a new position. The fact that after crosslinking of walls of the stomach after correction, navel usually changes its location, and the physician is surgically returns it to the usual place.

Partial tummy tuck should be considered more gentle. It performed under local anesthesia and is often performed in conjunction with other plastic surgery procedures such as liposuction. Partial tummy tuck involves only the lower abdomen, and the seam of the surgery is slightly shorter than in the classical version of the surgery. One of the disadvantages of partial tummy tuck may assume unnatural location of umbilicus after stitching of tissue edges.

After both surgeries plastic surgeon recommends wearing a compression garment and in case of abdominal pain, take painkillers. Often with a tummy tuck is performed circular face lift or rhinoplasty, to save time and money of the patient. It is very important for a long time to keep the effect of a tummy tuck. For that there are several ways and rules, by following which you can significantly increase the duration of the obtained effect . First of all, during the recovery period is necessary to strike out alcohol and smoking from daily life, because it affects the blood supply, healing processes and tissue regeneration.

Additionally, in the postoperative period, you should observe a balanced diet, and after its end, always do a special set of exercises to maintain muscle tone and skin of the abdomen.

For best effect during rehabilitation after abdominoplasty, you should not be exposed to direct sunlight, which can cause unwanted pigmentation and accelerate the aging process of the skin.

Within a few weeks after the surgery requires a reduction in physical activity to save the result. In the future, it is recommended to wear comfortable underwear.

In addition to general recommendations for the conservation of the great condition of the skin, it is desirable to always use creams based on glycolic acid and retinol, after discussing this with your beautician.
