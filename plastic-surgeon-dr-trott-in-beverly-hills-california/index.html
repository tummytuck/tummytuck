Breast Lift Through Fat Transfer to Breasts?
Can you lift breasts with fat transfer to the breasts?
Jan 12, 2017
It is possible for a fat transfer to give the appearance of a breast lift, the same way that an implant can. An implant cannot lift the breast but it can lift the nipple in certain situations, by filling out the lower pole (bottom part). The same can be done with fat transfer. However, most likely with your breast size and weight loss, this would not be possible with your breasts. If you were a small C cup, with just a little ptosis (sagging) it could work.
