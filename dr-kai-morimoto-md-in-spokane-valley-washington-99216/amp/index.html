Surgical vs Non-Surgical
Apr 6, 2018
There are many options for you today. If you are most interested in a surgical approach, then liposuction or a mini-tuck might work for you. If non-surgical is something you would prefer, you should look into SculpSure – a non-invasive procedure that takes 25 minutes for a 4 pac configuration that looks about right for you.
