The indications for this operation is the presence of skin and fat “apron” on the stomach, which can occur when sudden weight loss or after pregnancy. The essence of the surgery is to remove slack or sagging skin with fatty tissue and restore the original shape of the abdomen.

Tummy tuck is performed under general anesthesia and lasts 2-2.5 hours.

Usually, patients are asked the following issues at the consultation:

Where is the scar, and whether it will be noticeable after the tummy tuck surgery?
The scar is in the pubic area on the hairline that allows you to wear a bikini.

Do I need to lose weight before surgery?
This is not required, but if you decide to lose weight, it should be done prior to surgery.

How does childbirth affect the result of the Tummy tuck operation?
During pregnancy, there is a deformation of the skin on the abdomen, so the operation is better do if you are not going become a mother in future.

What are the contraindications exist for tummy tuck?
The presence of serious diseases, including cancer.

Summary

Age: 18 years old.
Pre-event:
– Do not eat to-day operations
– 6 weeks prior surgery stop taking contraceptives, as well as drugs that violate the blood clotting.
Anesthesia: General.
Duration of operation: 2-2.5 hours.
Hospital stay: 1-2 days.
The bandage: within a few hours
Removal of sutures: 7-10 day.
Return to the normal rhythm of life: in 10-15 days.

Postoperative precautions:
Refrain from vigorous activity during the month
Avoid use of the sauna for a period of healing

Painful sensations: no
