Can I massage or do stretches to help stiffness and sore muscles after tummy tuck?
Post operative care after a tummy tuck
Feb 11, 2019
It is important to walk bent over at the hips for 5-7 days after surgery to reduce tension on the suture lines. Sleep with your hips in a flexed position and your head elevated on 2-3 pillows and keep 2 pillows under your knees. Following these instructions will help with the quality of your scar as well. Walking helps circulation in the whole body. Remember to move your feet back and forth for 2 weeks post surgery to help with blood circulation and to prevent blood clots…
