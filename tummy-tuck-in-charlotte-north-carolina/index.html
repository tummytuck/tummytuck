Tummy tuck in Charlotte

Tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) in Charlotte is one of the most popular plastic surgery. Both women and men resort to it. Moreover, the number of men is grow.

People resort for various reasons to tummy tuck. Changing the shape of the abdomen after childbirth, resulting in rapid weight loss, due to genetic features, but most of all, the tummy tuck carried out due to excessive weight.

Types of tummy tuck

Tummy tuck in Charlotte is very popular. If only a few years ago, abdominal plasty could afford only wealthy people, but now it can not afford to virtually anyone.

Tummy tuck is performed at fairly affordable prices. Especially when you consider the aesthetic effect, because the skin becomes elastic and beauty, and the lost pounds of fat tissue is not back just the way it happens even after the most trendy diets.

Tummy tuck carried out for women and men of all ages from 18 years. Charlotte plastic surgeons are fluent in the methods of complete and partial abdominoplasty.

Charlotte experts recommend tummy tuck in such cases:

Tummy tuck in Charlotte, NC

  • with impossibility of realization of liposuction
  • in the presence of excess subcutaneous tissue and skin of the anterior abdominal wall, which can not be removed by other methods and techniques
  • with an excess of abdominal skin, or there are significant stretch marks
  • at a total tension and discrepancy of the abdominal muscles
  • overhang at the front of the abdominal wall
  • skin and fat apron
  • rough postoperative scars in the lower abdomen

Tummy tuck is an operation, which is a reduction and a tummy tuck by removing excess skin and subcutaneous fat layer at the bottom and middle of the abdomen, as well as the contraction of the abdominal muscles. Tummy tuck is a major surgical procedure that requires extreme care and precision.

Tummy tuck in Charlotte performed only by highly experienced plastic surgeons. If necessary, we will arrange for you consultation the leading plastic surgeons, who will answer any questions and will plan the operation with you.

Tummy tuck in Charlotte, as well as any other plastic surgery, carried out not everyone who wants to, because it has contraindications. Most of them are relative, so consultation of plastic surgeon is required!

With tummy tuck in Charlotte, it is possible:

Tummy tuck Charlotte, NC

  • strengthen the musculo-aponeurotic layer
  • get rid of the sagging of the anterior abdominal wall
  • give a good shape of the anterior abdominal wall and remove its laxity
  • remove postoperative scars of anterior abdominal wall

Contraindications to carrying out tummy tuck in Charlotte, NC:

  • marked obesity
  • uncompensated heart failure and pulmonary
  • post-surgical scars, located above the navel
  • diabetes decompensated
  • planned in the near future pregnancy

If there is no contraindications plastic surgeon will select the best method of operation.

Tummy Tucks demands from the plastic surgeon utmost of care and caution. Realizing the seriousness of the issue, we suggest doing of abdominoplasty with leading plastic surgeons of Charlotte. All of them have extensive experience in this type of activity, and are fluent in all types of tummy tuck.

Tummy tuck Charlotte, North Carolina

Tummy tuck in Charlotte performed by various methods. The method chosen depends on several factors:

  • skin condition
  • stretch marks
  • postoperative scarring
  • thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer
  • the presence of hernias
  • requirements of the patient

Today in Charlotte successfully practiced such kinds tummy tuck:

  • Standard tummy tuck. During the surgery removes excess folds of skin and subcutaneous fat of the anterior abdominal wall. Sutures are imposed at the level of the pubis.
  • Mini tummy tuck (partial). During surgery removes excess skin from the lower part of the abdominal wall (below the navel). Thus umbilicus is not moved. Liposuction can be performed at the same time to reduce the level of fat in the abdominal wall.
  • Extensive tummy tuck. During the operation removed the excess fat and skin in the abdominal area and fatty tissue on the thighs or back. This procedure will include two sections – one above the pubis, and the second – around the navel. The length of the cut depends on the number of removed skin.
  • Endoscopic tummy tuck.This surgery is performed to tighten and make the best form the muscles of the abdominal wall. Performed using several incisions in the pubic area. Typically, the skin is not removed.

Tummy tuck in Charlotte, North Carolina

Tummy tuck in Charlotte performed under general anesthesia. The procedure lasts from 2 to 4 hours. The patient would have to stay in the hospital for several days (usually about 2-3).

Note that tummy tuck, unlike some other plastic surgery procedures has lasting results. After the operation is virtually invisible seam. Rehabilitation period lasts from 4 to 12 months.
