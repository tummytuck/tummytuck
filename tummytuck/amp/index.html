Tummytuck

Liposuction won’t help with excess skin.

Liposuction can be a great option for men and women who have already reached a healthy target weight, but notice one or two stubborn problem spots like the inner thighs or the love handles.

While lipo can be a very effective body contouring tool, it can’t help with loose skin or separated abdominal muscles that’s what a tummytuck is for. Some surgeons will combine the two procedures, or include liposuction in the areas around the tummy to ensure a smooth transition following abdominoplasty.

You’ll see the best results if you wait until you’ve reached your weight loss goals before scheduling any type of body contouring procedures. (David L. Kaufman, MD, Folsom)
