Would dermapen be a good first step in acne scar revision?
Deep acne scars and a dermapen
Dec 28, 2015
Some of the scars are not so deep and in that case a Dermapen might provide some help. However ,many scars are quite deep, hence the term 'ice-pick' scars. These really need under-grfating with dermis from perhaps an old surgical scar you have or from a skin graft from behind the ear. One newer choice is Belafill, a collagen coated plastic particle which can be injected under the scar. This requires collagen skin testing in advance.
