Is liposuction (either Smart Lipo, tumescent, liposculpture etc) good for someone with a protrudin
pinch test
Sep 15, 2019
There is a simple way to determine whether liposuction of your abdomen and waist will result in a significant improvement. It is the pinch test. If you can pinch a layer of skin and fat on your belly that is at least 1 inch thick, liposuction will result in an improvement. The thicker the tissue in the pinch, the more improvement liposuction will produce. If the pinch is less than an inch, your fat is inside your belly wrapped around your large intestine and it can’t be removed.
