Types of tummy tuck procedures in Cincinnati

Tummy tuck Cincinnati

  • Classical tummy tuck: an incision is made in the bikini area, and then the fat is removed, excess skin is excised, the tissue layers are sutured with the formation the navel
  • Minimal abdominoplasty in Cincinnati: during the procedure happens less traumatic of tissues, skin and fat apron is removed without moving the navel

  • Stress-vertical tummy tuck: during surgery tightened tissues, located not only horizontally, but also vertically
  • Extended abdominoplasty in Cincinnati: indicated in the presence of pronounced wrinkles and fat not only on the stomach, but also in the back
  • Counter tummy tuck: carried out in the case where the excess fat tissue located not only below the navel but above it.

Post-operative care

Tummy tuck in Cincinnati OH

Recovery after surgery takes 3-6 months. In the first weeks can be observed mild soreness seams, swelling of tissues. To remove the postoperative discomfort specialists in Cincinnati use modern medicines. For the first time after a tummy tuck in Cincinnati, patients are advised to limit physical activity, use of a corset, bandage. Sport activities are prohibited, as it may interfere with the healing process and lead to the development of complications.

Sparing regimen continued during the first two months. Patients can be within 3-5 days  in the clinic. If the health status permits, they go home earlier. Throughout the recovery period (6-12 months), the patient should visit specialists to assess the quality of tissue healing. A tummy tuck in Cincinnati is one of the complex operations, so during rehabilitation is very important to follow medical advice and does not violate the prescribed regimen.
