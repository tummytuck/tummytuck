What is the difference between chin lipo and neck lipo?
Chin vs neck liposuction
Nov 26, 2014
There is no difference between liposuction of the neck and chin. Liposuction of this area can improve the contour of the neck and jawline. A chin implant alone will not have any effect on the neck area, so if you find this area a concern, liposuction may be a great plan. Please be sure the physician performing the surgery is a board certified plastic surgeon who has experience in chin implants and liposuction and is using a chin implant that is the correct size and shape. Best wishes!

What can I do about pre and post breast lift care?
Preparing for breast lift
Nov 6, 2014
Smoking can increase the risk of having complications with wound healing, so do not smoke for 6 weeks prior to and after surgery. Also, ask your plastic surgeon about supplements that can affect bleeding and anesthesia and avoid those. You should not be tanning at all due to skin cancer risk, but your scars can darken for up to one year post-op if exposed to sun. Please contact your plastic surgeon for his/her pre and post op instructions!

When patients present to my practice with this problem, I approach it with a multi-faceted approach.
