UltraShape and number of treatment sessions

The typical protocol for UltaShape  is three sessions performed every other week.  We have had many patients whom that were so happy with the three treatments that they underwent additional treatments to further reduce fat.

UltraShape for reducing arm fat
Jul 10, 2016
UltraShape can be used to destroy unwanted fat in many areas of the body as long as there is approximately 2cm of fat thickness.  We have treated arms, thighs, love handles, abdomen and male chests.

Is UltraShape Right for Me?
Jul 10, 2016
Nearly everyone can benefit from UltraShape to destroy areas of unwanted fat.  UltraShape uses nonthermal ultrasound to destroy fat without pain.   It is not recommended during pregnancy or treating an area that has an underlying hernia.  The area to be treated needs have approximately 2cm fat.

Maximizing UltraShape Results
Jul 10, 2016
Unlike all the other non-invasive technologies to destroy fat, UltraShape is nonthermal so it does not freeze or use heat to reduce areas of unwanted fat.  In addition, the treatments can be customized for each patient.  As with all technologies, it is recommended to live a healthy lifestyle with dieting and exercising to maximize your results.  There is no discomfort, swelling or bruising with UltraShape so exercise can be continued.
Ultra shape or coolsculpting?
UltraShape is Non-Thermal
Mar 16, 2016
UltraShape is the only fat destroying technology that is uses non-thermal, pulsed, focused ultrasound to selectively destroy fat.  Since UltraShape is non-thermal, it is the most comfortable option as compared to freezing or heating the fat.
Is Ultrashape safe for someone with a copper IUD?
UltraShape Safety
Mar 16, 2016
UltraShape is safe because it uses nonthermal, pulsed, focused ultrasound to selectively destroy fat.  Surrounding tissues, such as nerves, blood vessels, connective tissue, muscle, etc are not injured.
Can Ultrashape of the abdomen be performed in the presence of a small umbilical hernia?
UltraShape and Hernias
Mar 16, 2016
UltraShape and the other fat destroying systems should not be performed over hernias.  Unlike many of the other systems, UltraShape treatments can be customized to avoid treating regions of known hernias.

Mar 16, 2016
UltraShape is unique as compared to other non-surgical fat reduction systems because unlike the others which are freezing or heating the fat, UltraShape uses non-thermal pulsed, focused ultrasound to selectively destroy fat.
