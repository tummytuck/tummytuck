Is it necessary to have massages after a tummy tuck and lipo 360 (PAL)?
No.
Oct 6, 2020
Hi Bentley. It is not necessary to have massages after tummy tuck and lipo 360. However, I do strongly recommend them. I always remind my patients that I can do the best job technically with their surgery, but the healing is up to them. Therefore, the final results are a “team effort”. Massages can help with the healing process. They help to manage swelling, promote symmetry, and avoid scar tissue from forming into “lumps” – a problem that is very difficult to fix after the fact….

tummy tuck recovery
