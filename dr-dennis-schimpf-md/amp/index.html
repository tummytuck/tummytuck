Can Reverse Abdominoplasty be done with Kocher Gallbladder scar without concern for blood supply?
Blood supply
May 10, 2015
There is concern for blood supply to the distal abdominoplasty flap in the setting of a previous kocher incision. Ideally we would like to remove the scar with the procedure. But this is not always feasible. In addition the time period between the kocher incision and the abdominoplasty is important. The longer the time period the better re-established the blood flow will be. I agree it is doable but you should definitely seek the opinion of a board certified plastic…
