How to prevent asymmetry in facelift?
Asymmetry After Facelift
Feb 5, 2017
Asymmetry after facelift surgery, particularly if seen after the early phase of surgical swelling and bruising has passed, is distinctly uncommon. More often, there is asymmetry present before surgery that many patients do not recognize. The facelift may be able to assist in correcting pre-op asymmetry by using facial implants, transferring fat for volume and contouring, adjusting tissue and skin tension, as well as other techniques.
