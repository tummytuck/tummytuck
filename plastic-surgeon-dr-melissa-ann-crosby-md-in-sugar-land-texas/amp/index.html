Can my breast be fixed or reconstructed having 3rd degree burns ?
Options for breast reconstruction after burns
Jan 7, 2017
I am sorry to hear about your trauma and thank you for your question. Yes, there may be multiple options for breast reconstruction which may include using tissue from another part of your body to repair the severely burned skin/tissue or implant based with or without your own tissue to assist with volume asymmetries and tissue defects. It is important to choose a plastic surgeon who has experience with reconstructive plastic surgery. Again, thank you for your question.
Is it a bad idea to travel outside of my state for Breast Reconstruction Surgery/Mastectomy?
Out of state reconstruction for breast cancer
Jan 3, 2017
I have many patients that may travel from out of state for a portion of their breast cancer treatment especially reconstruction. It is important to understand that there is often some follow-up required and if a tissue expander is used you may need to return for expansions and an additional surgery for the implant placement. I would discuss with your plastic surgeon what type of follow-up and after care may be required at the time of your consultation.
