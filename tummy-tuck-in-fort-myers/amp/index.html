Fort Myers tummy tuck

Tummy tuck in Fort Myers – a surgical procedure that will ensure:

  • Improvement of shape and waist;
  • Strengthen the muscular frame of the abdominal wall;
  • Elimination of the so-called skin and fat “apron”;
  • Improving and tightening the skin condition;
  • Elimination of sagging skin, stretch marks (striae), hernias.

Tummy tuck procedure in Fort Myers

Abdominoplasty Fort Myers

Surgery can be performed under local or general anesthesia (depending on the qualifications of the doctor and anesthesiologist). As a local anesthetic is applied epidural (spinal) anesthesia. Most extensive surgery is performed by so-called “anchor” technology, when the cuts form a anchor. We explain in the course of the surgery is performed moving the navel, pulling the skin down and horizontal scar formation, and formed waist – it is carried out vertical incision.

This is the classic version, but there are other techniques. Sometimes the weakness of the skin allows you to make surgery with the transfer of the navel without vertical section (if in the waist area everything good). In this case, the seam will be just around the navel and horizontal running along the bikini line. Mini-abdominoplasty in Fort Myers suggests only horizontal scar, navel is not affected.

Tummy tuck scar Fort Myers

Sometimes it is necessary to sew muscular frame (also in order to create a waistline). With this volume of surgery within 3-4 months should limit physical activity on abdominal. In the classical surgery a muscular frame is not affected, and a month and a half, it is already possible to train.

During the surgery in Fort Myers always taken into consideration a vector of stretching of soft tissues. For example, during pregnancy occurs stretching of tissues in the front direction. Tummy tuck after giving birth, in this case, assessed against these changes.

Under weight loss occurs stretching of soft tissues in a circular vector, it must also be taken into account when surgical intervention in order to achieve the best effect during the tummy tuck.

Seam after tummy tuck Fort Myers

As a result of surgery varies blood flow in the flap, and there is redistribution of skin and adipose tissue. That is why before surgery in Fort Myers, patient must carefully examine your overall health. It is important to understand than the more responsible and thorough in preparation for surgery, the easier it will be the process of recovery.

Mesh implants during tummy tuck used in the event that there is laxity of the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall, then they serve to seal the muscles. Muscle weakness is not always related to the lack of physical activity, sometimes after childbirth there is dilution of the muscles. Implants of this kind are usually not resorbed.

The period of rehabilitation after tummy tuck surgery:

After the surgery, the patient remains in the hospital of Fort Myers for 3-5 days. At this time, he can stand up, but is not recommended walking or remain long in the vertical position. Compression slimming underwear puts on the patient after the surgery, which must be worn for a month after surgery to protect the surgery area. The recovery period takes from 1.5 to 2 months. At this time the patient to limit heavy lifting or any physical activity that provokes tension of the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall. Suture becomes invisible after about 8 months, and the first 6 months occurs forming in the thin thread. And only after 12 months, you can evaluate the results of the work of the surgeon and, if desired, to make a tattoo of scar.
