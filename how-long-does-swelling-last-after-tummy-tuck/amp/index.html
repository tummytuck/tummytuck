Swelling after abdominoplasty: how to accelerate the healing?

When the swelling will subside after abdominoplasty? The answer to this question largely depends on the behavior of patients during the recovery period.

Sleep. Great importance is the position of the trunk during sleep and rest. Experts explain that in a horizontal position, lymph is easier to circulate in the region of the incision. Therefore, in the morning, swelling after abdominoplasty is much less than in the evening. Recovery will accelerate if you sleep on an orthopedic mattress and pillow after surgery. The ideal posture – lying on the back with arms outstretched along the trunk and a roller under the knees.

Food. Women should carefully monitor the volume of liquid drunk. Clean water is extremely useful, but even its consumption should be limited. Remember, that thirst intensifies after salty and spicy dishes, and also after sweet drinks. Swelling after abdominoplasty can not be treated with diuretics, as they provide an unnecessary burden on the kidneys and make it difficult to diagnose complications.

Surgical corset. This medical attribute performs many functions, including a gentle massage of the abdominal cavity, improving lympho- and blood flow. Swelling and numbness after abdominoplasty will fall much faster with the correct choice of corset. It can look like a wide elastic belt or high panties with a fixed upper part. After a month of continuous wearing it can be replaced with comfortable compression garments.

Restrictions. Contraindications and prohibitions include any operation on the abdominal cavity, including abdominoplasty, swelling worries longer than people who smoke, as well as those who consume alcoholic beverages. In the first month after surgery, it is forbidden to take hot baths, visit beaches, saunas, solariums. Limit exercise and postpone long trips.
