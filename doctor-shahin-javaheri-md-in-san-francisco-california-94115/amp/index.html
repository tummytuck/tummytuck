How long is recovery for fat transfer breast augmentation?
Breast Fat Graft Recovery
Aug 26, 2014
Recovery from fat grafting to the breast will vary from one person to another, but generally, it is not long. The bruising will last 1-3 weeks, and the over all appearance should be complete by 2-3 months. Usually, the liposuctioned areas have the most discomfort.
What is the best way to communicate to your doctor what results you’re looking for?
Communication
Aug 22, 2014
Be Honest! I believe that the most common cause of unhappy patients is the lack of communication. The doctor should be the source of information about a particular procedure, but it is the patient’s task to provide exactly what it is that they want. At this point the doctor should be able to determine whether the request is either realistic, or whether it can be performed, based on their experience.
How can a fat graft to face and lips be removed or reversed?
fat grafting reversal
Aug 22, 2014
Removal of fat following grafting is certainly possible, but it does depend on the location placed. This will require “localized” liposuction of the grafted fat. Fat grafting is a technical procedure and requires skill as well as experience.
