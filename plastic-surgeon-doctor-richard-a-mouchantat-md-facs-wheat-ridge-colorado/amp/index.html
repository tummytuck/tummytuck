size choice
Nov 25, 2015
I recommend using implants that fit your chest wall the best and deciding how you want your breast to be shaped. Almost any implant will make you look bigger, the right dimensions of the implant will make you look beautiful. Your "wish boobs" are full and round so it is likely that you will be happy with a high profile implant. I don't recommend going bigger so that you won't feel like you aren't big enough, after all you will be 400cc larger! The fit is the most important factor. Good luck.

Weight loss! Should I consider an arm lift?

See a board certified plastic surgeon to have a consultation.
