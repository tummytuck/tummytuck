Implants and Cup size

Choosing the right implant is more involved then asking for a certain cup size because different stores have different sizes, a C cup at one place may be a D cup at another place. I would schedule a consultation and try on different sizes, different profiles as well as doing 3D computer simulations with different implant profiles and sizes.
