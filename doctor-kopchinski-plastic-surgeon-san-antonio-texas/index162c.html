Tummy tuck or lipo?
Tummy Tuck
May 9, 2018
Thank you for your question. Based on your photos and that you have had children, I would recommend a Tummy Tuck with Liposuction of the Flanks. This will yield the best results and be the most long lasting. Liposuction addresses fat whereas Tummy Tuck removes excess skin and fixes the separation of your abdominal muscles which is common with childbirth. Seeking an in-person evaluation with a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon would be best! Good Luck!

Is lumpiness, unevenness normal at 2 weeks?
Lumpiness post liposuction

Hi! Yes, lumpiness and unevenness is common at only 2 weeks. In my experience the skin contraction can cause these uneven contours that usually resolve at 1-3 months.
