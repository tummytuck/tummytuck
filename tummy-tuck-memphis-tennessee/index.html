Tummy tuck Memphis, Tennessee

After tummy tuck in Memphis TN

Tummy tuck in Memphis is a surgical procedure that lies in the fact to save the patient from violating the right proportions of the anterior abdominal wall and the body as a result of distension of the muscles and increasing the thickness of skinfold abdomen.

Despite the longevity of a series of plastic surgery, tummy tuck in Memphis is one of the most difficult in all respects. In 1999, plastic surgery celebrates the 100th anniversary of the tummy tuck. At present, this surgery has gained the greatest popularity.

Indications for tummy tuck in Memphis are:

After tummy tuck in Memphis

  • Excess skin and subcutaneous tissue of the anterior abdominal wall, which can not be removed by other means and methods (diet, exercise, liposuction, a combination of these methods).
  • The presence of pronounced stretch marks (striae pale) with an excess of abdominal skin.
  • Rough postoperative scars in the lower abdomen.

It is not recommended to combine abdominoplasty with other abdominal operations.

Contraindications to abdominoplasty  in Memphis

  • Uncompensated heart and lung failure.
  • Decompensated diabetes.
  • Obese.
  • Planned within the next year pregnancy.

Abdominoplasty Memphis

Tummy tuck Memphis – is surgery not for the treatment of obesity and weight loss. So, before you resort to it, you need to find out the causes of obesity, use of all methods and thus to reduce your weight. If surgery is performed at the maximum weight, then the result will be spoiled after weight loss, because again may be formed excess skin.

Features of tummy tuck in Memphis

Basically, abdominoplasty in Memphis is performed by moving the navel because it is impossible to tighten the upper abdomen without this technique. Also is made plastic of muscles of the anterior abdominal wall, without which it is impossible to change the profile of the abdomen. After this surgery remains a long scar above the pubis (30-50 cm.) And the scar around the belly button moved (after a time almost imperceptible).

Postoperative tummy tuck Memphis

Tummy tuck Memphis

Scars remain on the anterior abdominal wall (horizontal and sometimes vertical); exact characteristics of these scars can not be clearly defined prior to surgery due to the individual characteristics of the process of scarring. Some areas of the abdominal wall may lose normal sensitivity temporarily, and in some cases – permanently. Swelling of the tissues of the abdomen and bleeding (bruising) are stored for 3-4 weeks.

After surgery in Memphis remains a circular scar around the navel; form of navel may change. After surgery may accumulate tissue fluid or blood under the skin of the abdomen, that may require drainage performances or removal of fluid with a syringe, sometimes reoperations.

Tummy tuck Memphis TN

If on the anterior abdominal wall already performed tummy tuck surgery, and there are post-operative scars, in some cases increases the risk of malnutrition edges of the skin wound; this may require an additional skin plastics and other reoperations.

With considerable thickness of subcutaneous fat in the upper abdomen, moving them with sutures may lead to thickening (roller) above the suture line. To eliminate it may require additional surgery.
