Persistent Hematomas After Tummy Tuck and Lipo
Hematoma 2 months after a tummy tuck
Jul 30, 2017
What you think is a hematoma is most likely a seroma. Seromas can be drained percutaneously in the office. You may have to do it several times before it resolves. If it's still persistent, you will have to surgically have the seroma capsule excised.

Hardness after arm lift

Hello there, although it is difficult to tell based on the photos, it is not normal to have a large area of "hardness" after an arm lift 2 months post op. Purpose of Binder After Tummy Tuck?
Binder after a tummy tuck
May 24, 2017
There are no scientific studies to show that usage of a binder after a tummy tuck is beneficial in any way. Having said that, patients feel more comfortable wearing a binder. I place a 4-panel velcro binder on my patients for about 4 weeks. I noticed that it helps with swelling and potentially minimizing seroma complications. Of course, I experience is anecdotal only.
How Soon After Surgery Can I Start Scar Treatment?
Scar management after a tummy tuck
May 24, 2017
Scar massage - Start at 3 weeks after surgery 4 times day
Silicone sheeting - Start at 3 weeks and continue for 4-6 months
Avoid sun exposure during scar maturation phase
Remember scars take 1 to 2 years to remodel into their final state.
How to Convince Insurance Company to Cover Tummy Tuck?
Tummy tuck insurance coverage
May 24, 2017
I have never seen a Tummy Tuck or Abdominoplasty procedure being covered by insurance as it's considered cosmetic. A panniculectomy, which is a very different kind of procedure that addresses excess hanging lower abdominal skin may be covered as a medically necessary procedure with adequate documentation of the size of the pannus, and rash or recurrent infections within the folds.
How Long Should I Leave my Dissolvable Stitch Alone?
Suture spitting after tummy tuck
May 24, 2017
Suture spitting is not uncommon. Sometimes sutures can find their way out of the incision and can be quite irritating to the patient. The best way to deal with them is to see your plastic surgeon to have them removed. When my patients complain of sutures sticking out, I try to take them out as soon as possible.
How Much Does a Tummy Tuck Cost?
Cost of a Tummy Tuck
May 24, 2017
A tummy tuck can cost anywhere from $5000 to $15000 depending on geographic location. The costs typically include surgeon's fee, facility fee, and anesthesia fee. The national average surgeon's fee is around $5000.
Will Breasts Sag if I Decide to Explant?
Will Breasts Sag if I decide to explant?
May 24, 2017
It depends on a number of factors. But long story short, if you had the implants for a long period of time and if are of advanced age, there is good chance your breasts will sag after implant removal. Some patients are OK with that look while others may request for a lift procedure with or without reimplantation.
How Much Does Breast Explantation Cost?
Breast Implant Explantation
May 24, 2017
Ruptured breast implants or capsular contracture-related implant issues are typically covered by insurance for implant removal. This is the case even if the original implants has been placed for cosmetic reasons. They obviously will not cover re-implantation for cosmetic reasons.
