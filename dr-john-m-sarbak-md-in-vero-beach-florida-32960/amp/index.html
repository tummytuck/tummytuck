4 weeks, eyelids
Oct 13, 2017
First take a deep breath. Your body is still healing from surgery. It is not uncommon to have swelling for six weeks or even a little bit longer after any surgery. You will notice it more in the eyelids because that area is a focal point. While your body is healing you can help the process by avoiding foods with salt in them. That will reduce your swelling somewhat. Be patient, you haven’t seen your final result yet. Best wishes.
