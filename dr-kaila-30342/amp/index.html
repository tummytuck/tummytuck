Is a successful breast augmentation possible without a lift?
Lift needed?
Jul 21, 2014
Your question is a very common one. Some people try to get around a lift by putting in a larger implant. The problem will be the results will be short lived. The weight of a larger implant all eventually cause sagging and will eventually put you back into the same position except with a larger more sagging breast. Doing implants for now will give you a good outcome and buys you the time to decide to do a lift later. Remember you can always add lift incisions you can’t take them away….

Does fat transfer to hips really last?
Fat transfer
Jul 4, 2014
Yes fat transfer lasts 3 months post procedure all fat that did not survive the transfer is resorbed and you are left with the surviving fat. In my hands I find approximately 70% of the fat that is TRANSFERED survives the transfer and remains, 30% resorbs. Fat can be TRANSFERED almost anywhere there is a vascular bed for the fat to survive.
