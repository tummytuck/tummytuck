Implants can’t be repaired

Implants that are damaged cannot be repaired. Since you have Saline implants, if your implant had a hole in it, it should deflate and the body would re-absorb the sailine. When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
Your swelling will get better
Mar 6, 2017
After breast augmentation, the swelling you see will be at its maximum for the first one to seven days. You should see a slow decrease in the swelling over the first one to two months. When I see my patients at their two month follow-up, they are usually at their final size. At this point they can safely be fitted for a bra. Just remember, cup sizes can vary significantly between bra manufacturers.
Breast Augmentation Recovery: What Can I Expect?
Recovery from augmentation
Mar 6, 2017
Recovery and return to work or based on what type of work you do and how much lifting is involved. Most patients can do most daily activities within 1 to 3 days after surgery and can usually return to work within three days, providing they do not do heavy lifting. Your scars will start fading as soon as they are healed and are minimally noticeable by 1 to 2 months after surgery. Completion of the healing process may take up to a year.

Can lipomas come back after lipoma removal surgery?
Lipoma recurrence
Jun 9, 2009
After lipoma removal, most lipomas will not come back. If the lipoma was removed by excision then the chances of it coming back are very small. Some surgeons prefer to remove lipomas using liposuction and these are lipomas have a higher incidence of recurrence.
