5 mo post TT, muscle repair, Lipo. Fluid drained early wks. Is this seroma or pseudobursa?
Abdomen
Jun 7, 2017
It is hard to diagnose your condition from pictures. If you had your muscles repaired, typical swelling can last 6 months to 1 year post surgery. This should get better slowly. I recommend you consult with your plastic surgeon to confirm you had your diastasis repaired. A pseudobursa can cause permanent swelling or a fuller contour. You may request an ultrasound or CT to diagnose this.
