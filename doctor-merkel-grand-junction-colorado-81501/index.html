I Am 18 and I Want to Get Botox in my Top Lip, is That too Young?
Boyox is a paralyzer; not a filler
Nov 8, 2011
Don't get confused and think botox is a filler and can augment your lip! It is a paralyzer of muscle and one has to be very careful as to where it is injected Your concern for the smaller upper lip is not new. The volume ratio for upper to lower is 40% upper to 60% lower. If your proportion is different than that then the fillers can augment the disproportionate lip very nicely. Pick your 'injector' carefully
