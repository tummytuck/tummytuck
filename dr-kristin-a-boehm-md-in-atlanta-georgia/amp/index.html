Lipo vs. Tummy Tuck
May 9, 2019
Typically after a significant weight loss like yours a tummy tuck is the better option because it removes loose skin as well as subcutaneous fat. Liposuction can be combined with the tummy tuck to contour areas such as the flanks or pubic region. Is Liposuction Included?
Apr 22, 2019
In most cases liposuction of the flank or "love handle" area enhances the final result of a tummy tuck. It typically helps to define curves by accentuating an hour glass shape. Simultaneous Fillers
Apr 19, 2019
I often use combination fillers to get immediate results from some of the hyaluronic acid fillers and the more long term collagen stimulation from fillers like Sculptra.
