Best skin tightening treatment for post-pregnancy belly?
Probably a mini-abdominoplasy or a full abdominoplasty
Jan 27, 2009
There really is no best way to do this. It just depends on the condition of the various components of the abdominal wall: muscle, fat and skin. Also, the proportion of intra-abdominal fat needs to be assessed, which, of course, is not approached by abdominoplasty. Smart Lipo can only improve minimal skin laxity.

Can SmartLipo tighten the upper arms?
Yes, but it depends on the extent of the laxity
Jan 27, 2009
Smart Lipo can definitely tighten skin, but not if "bat wings" are present. Finally, a combination of liposuction, Smart Lipo and skin resection can all be performed simultaneously as indicated.

Will breast lift have any effect on pregnancy?
Breast lift won’t effect pregnancy, but pregnancy will effect breast lift results
Jan 27, 2009
If you’re starting out with breast ptosis, you will probably get more ptotic with pregnancy, lactation, and nursing. No mastopexy or breast lift procedure can withstand these changes. I have had a number of patients who have gotten pregnant, delivered and did NOT nurse and no significant change in their breasts occurred. There is so much individual variation, so it’s very difficult to predict.
Will bleph effect dry eyes problem?

Jan 27, 2009
Blepharoplasty will certainly not make your dry eye better. However, you may have to increase your use of artifical tears for several weeks; your ultimate need for the tears may not significantly change once you’ve recovered from surgery. I’ve done several patients with this problem and all have returned to their pre-op use of the tears.
