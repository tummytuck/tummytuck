Would a mini tummy tuck be enough, or should I go straight for a full tummy tuck?
Mini tummy tuck equals mini results
Dec 29, 2014
It appears that you have laxity of your abdominal wall above your belly button. A mini tummy tuck only tightens the lower abdomen and could potentially make a weak upper abdomen bulge even more. A full tummy tuck tightens both the upper and lower abdominal wall allowing better contouring along with removal of more redundant skin. the scar can be hidden below your bikini line and is often only inches longer than a mini tummy tuck scar.
