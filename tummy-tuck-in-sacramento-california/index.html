What is a tummy tuck in Sacramento?

Abdominoplasty in Sacramento is a lifting of abdomen. This is plastic surgery, which is carried on the front of the abdominal wall and is designed to remove excess fat and skin of the abdomen, to make the navel more attractive. The shape and dimensions of the human abdominal wall are affected by several key factors: age, natural complexion and, of course, sex. Defects in the anterior wall of the abdominal cavity are fairly common, they represent a skin – fat folds and is most often caused by a sedentary life, and the consequences of pregnancy and childbirth.

Indications for a tummy tuck in Sacramento are:

Tummy tuck in Sacramento, CA

  1. Excess skin and subcutaneous tissue (fat) of the anterior abdominal wall, which can not be removed by other means and methods (diet, exercise, liposuction, a combination of these methods).
  2. Inability to perform liposuction (lifting) due to the sharp decrease in Collagen-elastic properties of the skin (risk to get the effect of “baked apple” on the skin of the abdomen after liposuction).
  3. The presence of pronounced stretch marks (pale striae) with an excess of abdominal skin.
  4. The presence of skin and fat apron and ptosis (drooping) of the anterior abdominal wall.
  5. Discrepancy recti and general stretching of the abdominal muscles.
  6. Rough postoperative scars in the lower abdomen.

 Contraindications for a tummy tuck in Sacramento

  1. Uncompensated heart and lung failure.
  2. Decompensated diabetes.
  3. Diseases of the blood.
  4. Lack weight.
  5. planned within the next year pregnancy.

Tummy tuck in Sacramento – cosmetic surgery is not for the treatment of obesity and weight loss.

So, before you resort to it, you need to find out the causes of obesity, use of all methods and thus achieve weight loss. When surgery is done at maximum weight, the result after the weight loss will be spoiled because again may be formed excess skin. For patients who have undergone a tummy tuck, is available a consultation of clinical nutritionist.
