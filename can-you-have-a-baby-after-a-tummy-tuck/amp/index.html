Can you have a baby after a tummy tuck

Of course, I would not recommend planning on having a tummy tuck until you are finished having babies but sometimes babies arrive unpredictably! There is no danger to the baby and you will have a normal pregnancy. However the skin and muscle will stretch again and you may need another abdominoplasty.

Pregnancy after tummy tuck is not a problem, however it is difficult to predict how the result will hold up. It depends on your genetics, skin elasticity, body shape, previous baby weight, number of children and amount of weight gained with pregnancy.

I would wait at least six months to a year after tummy tuck surgery. You may have some resultant laxity and may need a skin tightening or even further muscle tightening.

Can you have a baby after a tummy tuck surgery

Of course, you may have a baby after a tummy tuck!

Yes it is possible to have babies after a tummy tuck. However your abdomen may not look as good after the baby as it did after the tummy tuck.

In a tummy tuck we do 3 things

  • Remove excess fat
  • Repair the muscles
  • Remove excess skin.

Having a baby may reverse some of this correction you achieved after your tummy tuck but never completely. Even if you have a baby after tummy tuck, your tummy will always look better than if you never had the operation.

In most cases a tummy tuck is used to correct the looseness around the front of the belly caused by stretching during pregnancy. If you know you are planning on having more children, I would suggest waiting to have a tummy tuck. That being said, it is perfectly safe to have another child after having a tummy tuck. It is important to give the incision enough time to heal so that it won’t be compromised from the stretching.

As you can read on this forum, most board certified plastic surgeons agree it is best to wait until you are done. I have patients occasionally who have life circumstances that make them want to have the surgery done now, such as a divorce or separation, but who are not certain on wether they will have kids in a future relationship.

From a surgical persective, a tummy tuck won’t prevent you from having more kids, but you will likely restretch the results. This is a personal decision, and you must weight the pros and cons for yourself.

Of course its safe to have a baby after a Tummy Tuck. However, you could potentially need a revision or another TT. In my practice I try to discourage women from undergoing a TT if they are planning more pregnancies. However, this is just to save them the cost and down time from possible revisionary surgery. But, if they have the means and support – why not? If this is the case, then I advise women to refrain from future pregnancies for a good solid year post TT.
