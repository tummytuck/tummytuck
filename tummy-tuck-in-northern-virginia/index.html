Tummy tuck in Northern Virginia

Northern Virginia tummy tuck

Tummy tuck of Northern Virginia is contraindicated in diabetes, diseases of the blood and inappropriate clotting. Not recommended the correction of the abdomen when planning pregnancy in the next year.

Abdominoplasty is not a means for weight loss, this is a forced measure and subsequently after the surgery you should to keep the weight at a constant level.

Techniques for the correction of belly is used different. It depends on the intended volume of the upcoming surgery. Regular tummy tuck surgery in Northern Virginia consists in removing excess folds of skin and fat. Area of surgery – the lower part of the abdomen.

Abdominoplasty Northern Virginia

During surgery it is possible tightening of the muscles and the removal of an umbilical hernia or white line of the abdomen. Liposuction is done if necessary.

As a result remains two scars on the stomach – one at the umbilicus, and the other in the suprapubic region. Extensive abdominoplasty in Northern Virginia is essentially the same as standard surgery, but its volume is expanded by the removal of fat from the back and hips with a side seam.

Tummy tuck surgery Northern Virginia

Small tummy tuck is done in Northern Virginia, when there is a need of resection of a small area of ​​skin. Zone of surgery – the area below the navel without involving it into process. Lipo tummy tuck in Northern Virginia – a variant of the mini-abdominoplasty. It performed in patients in whom is necessary to remove a piece of skin and fat that hang over the pubis. In this case, the correction of muscles not carried out.

After abdominoplasty in Northern Virginia

The most non-traumatic way of tummy tuck in Northern Virginia is an endoscopic abdominoplasty, which allows you to create the perfect waist and remove the bulge below the navel. However, no traces of surgery remains. This surgery is performed using an endoscope, which is inserted through a puncture in the navel and on the inner side sutured ligamentous apparatus of the anterior abdominal wall.

Tummy tuck with the removal of skin and fat apron requires careful examination and preparation. To do this, first performed examination at the therapist to rule out contraindications to this surgery and only after that, the surgeon determines the scope and technique of surgery.

Belly before tummy tuck in Northern Virginia

For anesthesia in Northern Virginia used general anesthetic. Surgery is performed only in a hospital. Surgery is time consuming and can take up to 5 hours. After surgery, patients remain under observation in the hospital for about one week.

In the postoperative period the patient may experience pain that stoped by analgesics. Perhaps the feeling of numbness in the area of tight skin. This symptom will pass on their own. Hemorrhage and swelling of the tissues of the anterior abdominal wall is preserved up to a month, and then, when everything heals, disappears.

After the surgery within two months is not recommended sauna, bath, gyms, swimming pools. Contraindicated physiotherapy and massage. Air travel can not be carried out for three weeks after surgery.
