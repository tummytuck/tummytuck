Tummy tuck in Jacksonville, Florida

Jacksonville Tummy tuck

Tummy tuck is one of the ways to correct figure in Jacksonville. This need arises in the the case of extension of the abdominal muscles after pregnancy that in conjunction with weight loss leads to sagging. This operation leads to good results, but keep in mind that the scar remains on the abdomen after abdominoplasty, which, depending on the severity, may have a different size.

Who makes abdominoplasty in Jacksonville most often.

Patients with indications for tummy tuck are the people (mostly women) who can not achieve the the desired effect through diet and exercise. Abdominoplasty is shown women who have had multiple pregnancies, which led to a stretching of the abdominal muscles and skin. Plastic surgeons of Jacksonville can recommend a tummy tuck at the loss of elasticity of the skin that usually occurs with the acquisition of excess weight or age.
