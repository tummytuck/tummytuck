Tummy tuck and liposuction

Belly button after tummy tuck in Newport Beach

You still have not decided whether you need a tummy tuck and correction of belly? In some cases, liposuction may well be alternative. Especially intense lifting effect is observed after laser nano-liposuction.

To get the most expressive results, tummy tuck performed together with liposuction of the back and other areas. However, liposuction of the abdomen and tummy tuck are not performed simultaneously, since it violates the blood supply to the stomach and requires long-term rehabilitation.

It should be noted that liposuction or a tummy tuck are not weight loss methods. And the best results on the abdomen have been reported in patients with moderate excess body weight.
