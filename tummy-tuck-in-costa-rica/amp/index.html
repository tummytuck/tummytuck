Tummy tuck in Costa Rica

Tummy tuck in Costa Rica

Tummy Tuck in Costa Rica is a surgical procedure to reduce the stomach.

Part of plastic surgeries aimed at improving the quality status of the female body after childbirth or to fight obesity. The two main surgical procedures that carry such appointment are abdominoplasty and liposuction.

With the help of both procedures, you can get rid of excess fat and skin. However, the appointment of abdominoplasty is more specific. This surgery is aimed at qualitative reduction of the abdomen (the area between the pubic bone, navel, lower back and sides). This procedure is sufficiently complex and requires skilled training of a plastic surgeon who practices it. In addition, the clinic doctors isolated a number of serious contraindications for abdominoplasty in Costa Rica.

In the presence of at least one of the following contraindications, the surgeon can refuse a patient in surgery. This is due to the fact that the procedure to reduce the stomach – is a major surgical procedure, the fulfillment of which the presence of contraindications endangers the patient’s life.

Belly button after tummy tuck in Costa Rica

Contraindications for tummy tuck in Costa Rica:

  • Congestive heart failure;
  • Pulmonary insufficiency;
  • Diabetes mellitus;
  • An immunodeficiency syndrome (AIDS);
  • Malignant neoplasms;
  • Pregnancy (including planned in the next year);
  • The presence of traces of surgical interventions on the abdominal cavity.
  • The latest degree of obesity.

Abdominoplasty in Costa Rica: the types and nature of surgery.

Tummy tuck scar Costa Rica

In the clinic carried out all existing types of plastic surgeries to reduce stomach. Abdominoplasty is divided into two main types:
  • Mini-tummy tuck;
  • Extensive tummy tuck.

Both species have a common indications. Experts identify a number of key evidence, which, in the absence of contraindications, may cause surgery to reduce stomach.

The indications for surgery in Costa Rica:

Before and after tummy tuck in Costa Rica

  • Excess skin and subcutaneous fat in the abdomen (from the pubic bone to the navel and below);
  • Striae in the treated area;
  • Loss of tone the abdominal muscles;
  • Postnatal increase in abdominal wall.

However, it should be noted that extensive tummy tuck should not be combined with other surgical procedures. This can cause complications, which in some cases may threaten the patient’s life. Even if otoplasty or breast enlargement are planned after the surgery, they should be postponed until the end of the rehabilitation period.

Mini tummy tuck in Costa Rica

Tummy tuck Costa Rica

This procedure removes the excess subcutaneous fat with the front wall of the abdomen, located between the pubic bone and the navel. During the procedure, the surgeon makes an incision along the hairy part of the pubic bone. Mini-tummy tuck is not accompanied by the movement of the navel. Usually it is performed in parallel with liposuction.

Besides the main purpose of this procedure, the mini-tummy tuck can serve as an excellent option when you remove the inguinal or umbilical hernia. After surgery along the line of the pubis, the patient has little noticeable scar with a total length of about 20 cm.

Extensive tummy tuck in Costa Rica.

Extensive tummy tuck in Costa Rica

Extensive tummy tuck – this surgery is more complex than its analogue. This is due to the fact that it runs across the abdominal wall and, in some cases, the adipose tissue may be removed from the sides as well from the back, and even hips .

This surgery is performed to reduce the stomach with physical movement of the navel. This is due to the fact that the surgeon making a few cuts and removing excess fat, can not pull up the weakened muscles without moving the navel. With extensive tummy tuck incision is made in the pubic area and around the navel. After this intervention remains two fairly noticeable scars.
