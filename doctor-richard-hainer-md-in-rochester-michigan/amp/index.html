How many times does a seroma need to be drained before it goes away?
Fluid collection after facelift
Aug 21, 2018
The key to having a seroma resolve is by aspirating it every other day to decompress the cavity and allow the cavity to obliterate. My only concern is that the cheek fluid collection could also represent a leak of salivary fluid. It should be checked for amylase to make sure that you do not have a salivary gland injury. Otherwise I think frequent aspiration is the key to getting the seroma to resolve in conjunction with a good compression garment. Hope this helps. Good luck.
Can you get a fat graft for both a BBL and your breasts in one surgery?
BBL and fat graft to breasts
Jun 23, 2018
Yes it is absolutely possible to have both a Brazilian butt lift and fat grafting to the breasts done at the same time. The issue will be whether you have enough donor fat. The use of the Revolve fat grafting system greatly accelerates the time to harvest and therefore it can be done within a reasonable amount of time. Hope this helps. Good luck.
5 year post tummy tuck, should I revise belly button or laser?
Belly button scar after tummy tuck
May 24, 2018
Since you are 5 years post tummy tuck, your scars are mature and really will not be very responsive to laser treatments. Your best option is to have the scar revised under local. This could easily be done in an office-type setting. That way the scar could also be moved slightly more internally so it is not so noticeable. Hope this helps. Good luck.
How long after a tummy tuck can I get Brazilian butt lift?
Timing between surgery BBL and tummy tuck
May 24, 2018
Typically I would recommend at least 3 months between major operations like a Brazilian butt lift and a tummy tuck. This will allow for the tummy tuck to heal and for you to recover. It will also allow additional liposuction contouring of the abdominal region to harvest additional fat for your Brazilian butt lift. Hope this helps. Good luck.
