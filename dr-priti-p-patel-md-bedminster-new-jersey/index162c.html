How Long After Breast Reduction Surgery Are Breasts Swollen?
Swelling after breast reduction
Oct 28, 2018
General breasts will be swollen for 6-8 weeks after breast reduction surgery. The majority will improve in the first few weeks, however we recommend waiting to get fitted for a bra for about 6 weeks.
