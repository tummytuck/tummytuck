weight loss before tummy tuck
Oct 23, 2014
It is optimal to be at a weight that is comfortably maintained for best results after tummy tuck. a recent history of massive weight loss puts one at risk of healing problems. Weight gain after, of course, undoes some of the cosmetic improvement. I ask my patients to try to achieve a weight that can reasonably be maintained prior to this surgery. Most patients will lose weight after surgery as the feel full sooner, and are more physically able to exercise once fully healed.
