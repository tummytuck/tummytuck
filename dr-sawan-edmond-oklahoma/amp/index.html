Is it scar tissue that I feel under the skin of my abs and hips after vaser lipo?
Vaser lipo and scar tissue
Aug 28, 2020
Vaser works by producing significant Ultra sonic energy that helps break down fibers especially in the back area to facilitate liposuction and sculpting , however it can leave scar tissue in certain areas. The best option would be to seek consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon that uses the vaser in your area and discuss possible treatment with vaser again to break down the scar tissue , however early lymphatic massage after vaser will be needed .
