Sydney Tummy Tuck Prices

Abdominoplasty in Sydney is a type of cosmetic surgery that involves tightening and flattening of the abdominal area.

Also known as tummy tuck, abdominoplasty is quite popular for people who have lost excessive weight, women who’ve had their last child and want to look fit without the flab, as well as those who want to have proportionate bodies sans the beer belly.

If you are considering undergoing abdominoplasty, bear in mind that this procedure is not a total weight loss treatment, but rather it is an enhancement or a corrective procedure for people who already loss massive weights.

The loose fat and skin are removed during abdominoplasty to give the tummy a flatter, leaner and healthier appearance.

For those who are considering weight loss through abdominoplasty, it is best to lose the pounds first before undergoing this procedure. Usually the results are permanent, unless the individual gained weight over time.

Abdominoplasty can cost from $4,000 to $20,000 in Sydney AU. The cost varies from one country to the next. The patient’s requirement, age, weight and health are also considered in the pricing. Additional fees should also be expected such as the pre and post visits to the plastic surgeon’s clinic.

What to expect after an abdominoplasty procedure

  • Long recovery time is needed for patients who have undergone abdominoplasty. It takes time to heal the sutures and it is best to take some time-off fromwork. Usually it takes up to 4 weeks to recover from abdominoplasty.
  • Wearing of compression bandage is recommended to lessen the swelling and pain during recovery.

  • Avoiding strenuous activities and lifting heavy loads can hasten recovery and healing.
  • Taking the prescribed medications to help the healing process is also crucial.
  • Bed rest during the first few days is essential.
