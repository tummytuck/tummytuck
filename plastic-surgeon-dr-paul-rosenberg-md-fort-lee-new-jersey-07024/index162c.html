While it is possible that a superficial skin suture opened up, the deep layer is most likely fine. I have a cyst in my right breast can I still get a Breast Augmentation?
Breast Cyst

Depending upon the location of the cyst, it might even be possible for your surgeon to remove it at the same time as he inserts your implants.
