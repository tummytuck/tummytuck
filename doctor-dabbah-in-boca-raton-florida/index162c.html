Is my belly button too high?
Belly Button High
Mar 21, 2017
It appears your belly button may be a bit high but it would be interesting to look at your before photo.  We are all shaped differently.  During most tummy tucks the belly button is detached from your skin but not from your abdomen.  Although plastic surgeons can influence the site of the belly button slightly depending on the length of the stalk, we usually cannot move it drastically.

I was going into upward facing dog and felt as if the flesh under my incision split, can this happen?
Swelling and Pain Post TT
Mar 17, 2017
If your belly is continuing to swell and you are more uncomfortable, you should see your surgeon again.  Although this is unusual, things can happen.  If stitches were used to tighten the abdominal muscles, there may have been some pulling or tearing of the tissue during your upward facing dog.  Usually observation is indicated unless you have or develop a hematoma.

Surgeons may do things differently
Mar 30, 2016
Some surgeons perform liposuction before doing an arm lift either at the time of the lift or sometimes months before.  Some don't do it at all.  It really depends on the preference of the surgeon.  Don't be as concerned about the liposuction as to the final result.  As long as the surgeon understands what you want to accomplish and as long as your expectations are realistic, you'll be happy.  Don't hesitate to ask the surgeon what he/she plans on doing and why.

Some hyperpigmentation is due to over production of melanin which usually responds to tretinoins and hydroquinone. However hematomas usually leave behind hemosiderin deposits which do not respond to these products....
