When Will This Necrosis After Tummy Tuck Heal?
Necrosis after Abdominoplasty
Apr 21, 2011
Healing will take about 3 months, when managed properly.

It will take several weeks until the extent of the necrosis has defined itself.

Debridement should be done only on clearly necrotic tissue.

Frequent visits with your plastic surgeon are key.

Any revisional surgery, if needed, should wait at least 1 -2 years.

Should I be Moving Around Immediately After Breast Augmentation?
Breast Augmentation Recovery
Apr 21, 2011
Instructions vary with surgeons and patients, but should be focused on the prevention of early healing complications, such as blood clots, bleeding (hematoma) and infection.

Excessive resting in bed may promote blood clots.

Excessive activity might start up bleeding.

The middle ground, taking it easy and resting the first week is prudent – the body deserves time to heal.
