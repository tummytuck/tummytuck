Tummy tuck gives you the opportunity back the beauty of your figure. In Cleveland plastic surgeons do the following types of abdominoplasty: standard, extensive, endoscopic. The choice of method depends on many factors, such as the degree of stretching of the abdominal skin, the presence of stretch marks on the skin, scarring, depending on the thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer, the individual characteristics of the patient. Surgery is performed under general anesthesia and lasts from 2 to 4 hours.

Surgery can be performed by both women and men over 18 years. Surgery is contraindicated in patients with severe obesity, with lung or heart failure, suffering from diabetes before a planned pregnancy, or if the patient has a postoperative scars above the navel.
