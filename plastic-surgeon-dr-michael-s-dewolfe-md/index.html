What are the exact reasons SculpSure is recommended only for people at or near their target weig
No weight loss expectations
Feb 27, 2016
In the studies, weight loss was a minimum of at all, while inches were decreased for the treatment area. Just as liposuction should not be as a weight loss tool, SculpSure is similar.  The recommendation is for treatment at and under a BMI of 30.  However, debulking of problem areas can be effective. 
How can this laser go down deep enough to melt fat away and not burn you?
Alternating hearing and cooling
Feb 27, 2016
The SculpSure 1060nm diode laser heats tissues as all lasers require for effectiveness. As with all layers, cooling the tissues is required. SculpSure alternates a cycle of heating and cooling to avoid skin burn. The wavelengths are able to penetrate into the subcutaneous fat in a cone shape configuration. Fat cells that are sensitive to this heat will be destroyed. Certainly those closer to the activation plate will be more affected than much deeper tissues. 
How does SureSculpt compare to CoolSculpting? Can either technique be used to treat the buttocks
Versatility is the Key
Feb 27, 2016
SculpSure has the advantage of not requiring fat to be sucked into a vacuum to improve a particular area. Coolsculpting and SculpSure have equivalent efficacy of reducing 24% of fat in a given area. However, SculpSure has the advantage of not needing to distort the tissue to be effective.  Therefore, I have found SculpSure to be much more versatile to tailoring the fat reduction by placing the applicators in a patient specific configuration.

Feb 27, 2016

This is the scenario optimal for SculpSure. Placed on the lower abdomen or love handles, patients will experience a 24% reduction in fat from the area of treatment after 12 weeks.
