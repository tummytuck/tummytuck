Loose skin. Would a full tummy tuck do the job?
Tummy tuck vs lipo
Dec 16, 2020
Thank you for your question. Looking at your photos I would suggest for the best results a tummy tuck. That will allow your surgeon to tighten the abdominal wall muscles and remove the extra skin. When you are seen in person your surgeon may recommend a laser assisted liposuction. This helps with the loose skin but may not improve it 100%. I would suggest seeing a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon in person to evaluate you and discuss your options. Best of luck to you.

What are my options for getting a slim waist and flat belly?
liposuction vs tt
Dec 15, 2020
Thank you for your question. When working on the belly we talk about liposuction vs tummy tuck. Liposuction will slim your midsection down by inches reducing the pinchable fat and getting some skin retraction. Whereas a tummy tuck will allow you to tighten up the abdominal wall muscles if need and allows your surgeon to remove the extra skin. I would suggest seeing a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon in person to evaluate you and discuss your goals and options. Best of luck to you.
