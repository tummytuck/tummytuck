What is the difference between cosmetic and functional Eyelid surgery?
Functional vs Cosmetic
Jul 25, 2014
A cosmetic repair of the eyelids implies that the main focus on the surgery is to achieve a more aesthetically pleasing shape to the patient's eye. Functional eyelid surgery is aimed at correcting problems, not appearance, with the eyelid. For example, a patient experiencing diminished vision due to eyelid ptosis (sagging) may require surgery in order to improve their range of sight.

Ideally, patients should lose as much weight as possible before surgery. As for a specific number, this is often determined by you insurance company, not the physician.
