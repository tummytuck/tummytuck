Mini tummy tuck is ideal for young women with not so pronounced disadvantage.

Complete tummy tuck is performed in Bakersfield with transverse cuts at the level of the pubis and the navel. Through these incisions is eliminated stretching of the abdominal muscles, tightens the skin and removes excess fat cells.
