Which incision is best for breast augmentation?
incision
May 4, 2019
Great question! My preference is an inframammary incision for silicone implants (I use periareolar incisions for saline implants if the patient prefers). In general, both these scars heal very well with minimal visibility. The risk of permanent loss of nipple sensation or ability to breastfeed with these scars is minimal , though if you are very concerned then the only way to totally avoid the risk is to have the augmentation after you are done having children. Good luck! Dr. Tamy
