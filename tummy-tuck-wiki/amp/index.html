Medical reasons for tummy tuck on wiki

Tummy tuck wiki before and after

Patients with indications for tummy tuck – the people (mostly women) who can not achieve the desired effect through diet and exercise. Abdominoplasty is shown women who have had multiple pregnancies, which led to a stretching of the abdominal muscles and skin. Also it may be recommended with loss of skin elasticity, which usually occurs with acquisition of excess weight or age.

Tummy tuck is contraindicated in women who plan future pregnancies, because vertical muscles in the abdomen that are tightened with the help of surgery, can stretch again during pregnancy.

Abdominoplasty not recommended for people who are going to lose weight, since in this case the effect, achieved by using surgery, may be greatly spoiled.

Testimonies for tummy tuck wiki:

  • pendulous abdomen, which arose as a result of weight loss, age-related changes or pregnancy;
  • laxity and stretching of the abdominal muscles, leading to prolapse of internal organs;
  • scar stretching of skin, especially around the navel and below;
  • presence on the belly of unsightly scars after other surgical interventions.

What i need for a successful tummy tuck?

Wiki tummy tuck

  • absence of any acute illness at time of surgery (e.g. common cold);
  • on 10 days before surgery should stop taking medications that contain aspirin;
  • categorically prohibits smoking within 10 days prior to surgery and for 3-4 weeks after surgery;
  • during the last 1-2 days before surgery (on the orders a physician) is recommended only drink liquids;
  • previous evening should take a bath;
  • surgery is performed on an empty stomach.

Features of surgery

A tummy tuck wiki

  • horizontal or vertical (or both) incisions are made on the anterior abdominal wall;
  • excess tissue below the belly button is removed along with scar stretch marks;
  • in the presence of scars after other surgical interventions a surgeon if possible removes them;
  • divergent muscles of the anterior abdominal wall during pregnancy are pulled together to restoration their normal position and tone;
  • at the end of surgery imposed cosmetic seams, drains are installed, applied dressings and bandage.

The postoperative period tummy tuck wiki

  • Bed rest is recommended for the first night. Get out of bed usually permitted the day after surgery;

  • Is necessary constant wearing of bandages for 4-6 weeks (and sometimes, on the orders a physician, up to 2-3 months);
  • In the early days can be observed malaise and mild fever. The development of postoperative complications is associated with the thickness of fat, weight of the patient, presence of comorbidities, smoking;
  • During the first 2-3 weeks perhaps malaise, swelling of tissues, and pain during movement. They are gradually disappearing completely after 2-3 months after surgery;
  • During the first 1-3 months there is a decrease of the sensitivity of seams. Therefore you can not use a hot water bottle with hot water, too hot shower or bath. It is recommended to avoid direct sunlight.

At full or partial tummy tuck wiki is achieved very good results in patients with weakened abdominal muscles or excess skin. In most cases the effect is long-lasting, if you follow a balanced diet.

The final result of the surgery depends not only on the skill of the surgeon, but also on the individual patient and the observance of all the doctor’s recommendations.
