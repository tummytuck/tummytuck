Tummy tuck alternative

Flabby, sagging skin of the abdomen is a frequent consequence of pregnancy or rapid weight loss. Until recently there was no alternative to tummy tuck in the solution of this problem. However, the surgery – a very serious interference with a long recovery period and high cost.

Changes in the skin which lead to loss of elasticity

The collagen fibers form a so-called carcass of the skin, which constitute about 70% of the dermis, and form therein a three dimensional network, which serves for supporting the various cells and structures. The main function of collagen fibers is to provide a strength tissues, they are low-extensible and very strong in tensile.

SkinTyte tummy tuck

Over time, and under the influence of solar radiation, or by prolonged excessive stretching of tissue (pregnancy, weight gain), collagen fibers lose their normal structure, thicken and stretch. Elastic fibers, proteoglycans, and the aqueous composition of the skin are also changed.

All this leads to the fact that the skin is stretched, loses its elasticity, firmness, becomes thin and dry.

The mechanism of action

  • Denaturation and contraction of collagen fibers;

  • Activation of repair (restoration) processes.

Thus, the skin of the abdomen is tightened, reduced, it becomes more elastic and healthy, stretch marks become less noticeable.
