What is the difference between lipoabdominoplasty and a standard tummy tuck?
Lipoabdominoplasty adds liposuction to a simple tummy tuck
Sep 26, 2020
Traditional tummy tuck was simply elevation of the abdominal skin and removal of excess skin with possible muscle tightening. Many North American surgeons used to avoid adding liposuction to a tummy tuck out of a fear that it can increase risk of complications. That notion has now been disproved and adding liposuction to a tummy tuck is becoming more widespread. Would a BBL or mommy makeover be my best option?
Tummy Tuck
May 29, 2020
Hello! Thank you for your question. Based on your photos you appear to have a lot of loose skin. A BBL will leave you with more loose skin as liposuction does not address skin laxity it only removes fat. A Tummy Tuck removes excess fat, tightens the abdominal skin, and repairs separated abdominal muscles. Be sure to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss your options.
