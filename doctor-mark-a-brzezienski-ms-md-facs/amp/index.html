Asymmetry after Implant removal
Feb 5, 2017
My go to technique for these asymmetries is fat grafting.
In this technique, fat is removed from the abdomen or hips, washed, and injected into a topographical map placed before surgery. Fat grafting allows correction to very specific areas which is not possible with implants. Occasionally and in significant deformities, more than one session is required.
Do I have tubular breast? Do I need surgery? What's wrong with my breast

Feb 5, 2017

If you consult with a boarded plastic surgeon, techniques available to you would include fat grafting and breast implants.
