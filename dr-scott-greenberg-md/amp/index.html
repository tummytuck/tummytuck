Is it a bad sign if patient coordinators keep calling you after a consult?
Persistent calls
Jan 23, 2019
Every office handles the consultation process differently. Choosing a board-certified plastic surgeon, getting a personal consultation with the surgeon and not necessarily an intermediary and feeling comfortable with how the office handles you are all important determiners of which surgeon you choose. If persistent calls bother you, then that is not the right office for you.
Best way to help scar fade away after Breast Augmentations?
Breast scars
Jan 23, 2019
There really is no one best scar cream. The best things for scars are compression massage, a silicone-based cream to provide the vehicle for the massage and time. The redness is something that time will help. If it persists, there may be other things going on with the scar athat may need to be addressed that you should discuss with your plastic surgeon.
What are the best lasers/topical creams i can use to diminish the redness of strech marks on breast
Redness After Breast augmentation
Jan 23, 2019
It is not unusual to develop stretch marks after breast augmentation. It is something that you really should just observe for an additional several months as the appearance is likely to improve. If the redness persists, this is best treated with IPL, BBL or vascular laser treatments. There really is no good, consistent or reliable treatment for the stretch marks themselves. You should discuss your options with your plastic surgeon.
Will my breasts ever be even after breast augmentation 1 month ago?
Breast Asymmetry
Jan 23, 2019
It is not unusual to have some asymmetry 1 month after breast augmentation surgery. Final results are usually not seen for at least 2-3 months allowing for all of the swelling to settle, the skin to stretch and muscle relaxation to occur. You should discuss this with your surgeon.
Do I have tuberous breasts and will saline, under the muscle give me good results?
Tuberous breasts
Jan 23, 2019
It does appear from your photo that your breasts are somewhat tuberous. This is a combination of small breasts, narrow breast base diameter (making them look triangular) and sometimes nipple/areolar pseudoherniation. This usually requires an augmentation mammoplasty as well as some type of breast lift (mastopexy) to get the best results. These are things you should discuss in detail with your plastic surgeon and make sure he/she has experience dealing with this...

Do you gain weight after breast augmentation?
Breast Augmentation
Nov 30, 2018
There may be a temporary weight gain from the intravenous fluid given during surgery but this should resolve 2-4 days after the procedure. There is the actual weight of the implants but this should be no more than 1-2 pounds.

Do I have tuberous breasts?
Tuberous Breasts?
Oct 24, 2018
From the photos, it doesn't appear that your breasts are tuberous. Tuberous breasts usually involve 3 findings: small breasts, a high, tight inframammary crease and a short breast base diameter. You appear to be an excellent candidate for a breast augmentation and possibly a breast lift as well depending on your goals for the procedure. These are details and alternatives you should discuss with your surgeon.
Am I a candidate for breast augmentation?
Breast Augmentation
Oct 24, 2018
From the photos that you submitted, you appear to be an excellent candidate for a breast augmentation using implants (saline or silicone) or fat transfer. Thread lifts are really not effective for maintaining long term results. As an alternative, if you do not want breast implants or fat transfer, you could consider a breast lift. These are alternatives you can discuss in detail with your plastic surgeon.
