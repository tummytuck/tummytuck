Mini tummy tuck skin only – How soon after Liposuction can you get skin removed?
Mini Tummy Tuck after Liposuction
Sep 18, 2019
It is best to wait until the swelling from your liposuction procedure has resolved . This typically takes anywhere from 3 to 6 months. If you have no skin laxity or minimal skin laxity the scar may be the same length as a C section scar. Larger amounts of skin laxity typically require a longer scar.
