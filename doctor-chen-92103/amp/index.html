Wrinkles under my eyes. What can I do?
Wrinkles Under Eyes
Jun 20, 2018
Wrinkles under eyes can be treated in several ways, ranging from less invasive procedures such as laser treatments to surgical procedures such as blepharoplasty. Lasers can be effective in improving fine lines. For more significant laxity, lower lid blepharoplasty can make a dramatic difference. This procedure involves removing the excess skin in your lower eyelid and tightening it, thereby removing the wrinkle lines.
