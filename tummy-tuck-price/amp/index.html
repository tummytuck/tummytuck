There are 3 types of tummy tuck’s. Mini-tummy tuck, Regular and Extended. The cost of a tummy tuck depending on which type of tummy tuck you will need.

Duration of surgery: 2-4 hours depending on the amount of liposuction. In my technique the scar is placed extremely low, approximately 7 cm from the vaginal crease within the bikini line. The belly botton scar is “inny”.

Muscles are tightened in midline, liposuction of the lower lateral aspect of the abdomen, along with the upper abdomen and flanks to improve the contour. A pain pump in inserted to control the pain for the first 48 hours and reduce the amount of narcotics taken.

Anesthesia: General by a board certified anesthesiologist in an accredited surgical facility or hospital setting

Recovery after Tummy Tuck:

  • about 3 hours in the recovery room
  • 2-3 days in hunched over position. Surgical discomfort is controlled with pain pump and pain meds.
  • 10-14 days back to the activities of daily living
  • 4-6 weeks normal exercise

My tummy tuck price

Tummy tuck prices vary greatly from State to State and Surgeon to Surgeon as do the types of tummy tuck procedures and the types of results you can obtain. There are standard tummy tucks, modified tummy tucks, mini tummy tucks, reverse tummy tucks, lipo-abdominoplasties, etc. The scope of work can vary greatly, as do results. In many patients, a tummy tuck without the proper liposculpture will not produce satisfactory results. Each patients requirements are different and the procedure or combination of procedures has to be tailored to them which will, of course, affect the cost of surgery.

More important than the price of surgery, is the quality of the surgery and the results. If you were to get a tummy tuck for free and were very unhappy with the results, what was the value ? Understand also, that great results in cosmetic surgery generally depend on taking the time to get those results. Select your surgeon based on quality, results and attention to detail to obtain your best value. 

A tummy tuck price

When discussing cost of a procedure like a tummy tuck you have to bear in mind not just the surgeons fee but the total fee which would include surgeon, facility, anesthesia,follow up visits. My surgeon fee is around $4000 but the total fee is around $6000. Fees will vary depending on location, but one should be vary wary of really ultra low quotes. Be sure to have a long discussion with your surgeon, see plenty of photos, and if possible talk to previous patients.
