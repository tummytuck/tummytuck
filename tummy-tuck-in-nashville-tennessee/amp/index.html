Tummy tuck in Nashville, Tennessee

Tummy tuck in Nashville

Tummy tuck or abdominoplasty in Nashville is surgery of plastic surgery, which consists in excision of excess tissue in the anterior abdominal wall. Modern fashion trend, or simply a woman’s desire to look perfect, dictates the need to have a beautiful and flat stomach. The problem is relevant also for a large number of the male population. The pace of modern life does not always allow you to mobile lifestyle, devote much time to their appearance, always eat right. All this may lead to loss of aesthetic appearance of the abdomen. In this case, there is always the possibility to resort to tummy tuck in Nashville, which will allow you to once again feel confident and irresistible even on the beach.

Indications for tummy tuck in Nashville

Tummy tuck can cope with a lot of shortcomings in this area, such as:

  • divergence of the abdominal muscles, which has the name – diastasis, and occurs most often as a result of pregnancy;
  • excess accumulation of adipose tissue in the abdomen;
  • marked reduction in skin tone with the formation of “apron”, which occurs, for example, due to loss of a large mass of the body;
  • pronounced stretch marks.

Tummy tuck Nashville

Defects of this type are formed in patients in their lifetime as a result of pregnancy and childbirth, pronounced weight gain and in case of sharp losses. It is not always possible to deal with them by means of exercise or diets, so with a strong desire to get a flat belly and beautiful can help only plastic surgeon in Nashville. Modern methods of a tummy tuck in Nashville make it possible to get rid of any imperfections in just a couple of days. Before the procedure will require give a blood sample and implementation of the necessary studies to assess the general condition of the patient. The detection of any serious illnesses before abdominoplasty require their correction.

Implementation of abdominoplasty in Nashville is not advisable at the maximum body weight of the patient. During heavy weight loss after the tummy tuck procedure, the result is reduced to nothing, as the body tissues much sag again. Therefore, for patients with severe overweight is advised to lose weight as much as possible before the procedure. Then surgery effectively eliminates the effects of drastic weight loss and figure flaws, provides long-term outcome.

Tummy tuck procedure in Nashville

Tummy tuck Nashville, Tennessee

Despite its apparent simplicity, tummy tuck in Nashville is a complex surgical procedure,  you should only trust an experienced plastic surgeons its implementation. During this surgery, which lasts up to 3 hours,  Nashville professionals spend an enormous amount of work on the body of the patient. Abdominoplasty is performed under general anesthesia and requires a hospital stay of 1-2 days. During the surgery, performed excision of excess skin and fat, eliminates the discrepancy rectus abdominis.

Volume of tummy tuck in Nashville determined by the characteristics of each patient. Thus, for example, may be performed mini abdominoplasty, which requires a small number of incisions and is minimally invasive. Such surgery indicated for patients with the need to correct only one zone on the anterior abdominal wall, that does not require stitch the abdominal muscles. Endoscopic mini abdominoplasty in Nashville allows you to tighten the skin underbelly. The incision of skin tissues is small, and instruments are inserted through small punctures on the anterior abdominal wall. So after such surgery scars become invisible, shortens the period of rehabilitation, and intervention in the body is very limited.

If you want to eliminate a pronounced stretching of the anterior abdominal muscles, eliminate a large amount of excess skin and fatty tissue, tissue incision is made ​​in the lower abdomen. Additional sections also allow to address deficiencies in the back, hips. This is called extensive tummy tuck in Nashville and has a pronounced effect on the formation of a slim figure.
