Would change in weigth, up or down, affect reconstructed breasts with anatomical silicone implants?
Weight changes with implant reconstruction
Jan 3, 2016
Unlike soft-tissue breast reconstruction, weight changes have very little impact on implant-based reconstructions. You will likely see no changes in your breast size with weight loss, however your breasts may become more prominent if you lose inches in your chest and stomach areas. Likewise, significant weight gain can result in implants “getting lost” or decreasing in projection in relation to increased chest and abdomen fat.

Capsulectomy and volume loss
Sep 14, 2014
Hi,It is very uncommon to lose breast volume with a capsulectomy as the capsule is typically a thin sheet of tissue. You may, however, perceive a loss of volume with a breast lift, because skin is removed in this procedure which can make your breasts look smaller. The breast volume is usually the same, it is just “packaged” into a smaller skin envelope.Good luck!
