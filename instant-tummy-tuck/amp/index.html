Generally speaking, the “ideal” patient for instant tummy tuck surgery is one who has completed pregnancies, is psycho socially/emotionally/financially stable, has an excellent social support system surrounding him/her, is capable of arranging enough recovery time and who has reached a long-term stable weight. When the time is right, consider consultation with board certified plastic surgeons who can demonstrate many patients who have achieved the type of results you are looking for.
