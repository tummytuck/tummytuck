How is tummy tuck surgery performed in Omaha

Abdominoplasty Omaha

The duration of a standard tummy tuck ranges from two to four hours. The operation is performed only under general anesthesia, local anesthesia is unacceptable. Directly surgery is performed on the anterior abdominal wall in various ways: by horizontal or vertical lines, in some cases – by both. The rehabilitation period after a tummy tuck

After tummy tuck in Omaha

After surgery, the patient certainly remains in the hospital during 1 – 3 days. Compression garment must be worn for at least 3-4 weeks. In addition, prohibited any physical activity for a month, and some exercises (eg, abdominal exercises) can not be made about 2-3 months. Also, after tummy tuck in Omaha is not recommended to sunbathe in direct sunlight or in the solarium. Hot shower, bath, sauna prohibited until healing postoperative suture.

In general, the recovery period lasts for six to eight months. After this time, you will see the final form of the “new” stomach. Thanks tummy tuck, you will be able to achieve pronounced and long-term effect. But remember that you have to keep your normal weight and prevent its sharp fluctuations.

Tummy tuck contra

  • explicit obesity;
  • pregnancy and lactation;
  • pregnancy, planned in the next year or two after surgery;
  • presence of postoperative scars above the navel;
  • inflammatory or acute infectious process in the zone of surgery;
  • diabetes;
  • uncompensated cardiac or pulmonary insufficiency.
