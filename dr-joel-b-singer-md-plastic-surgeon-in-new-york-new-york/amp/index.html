Unforatunately visceral fat can only be removed by diet and exercise; Liposuction can’t reach under the muscles where the visceral fat lies. Liposuction can only reach the sub-cutaneous fat that is pinchable.Good luck.

8 months ago I had laser lipo done on my abdominal upper and lower. I still have burning and hardnes
Laser Liposuction Post-surgery hardness
Aug 13, 2016
First of all, I’d ask a quick question of you: Are you a smoker? Nicotine can significantly impact healing and I’ve seen patients who are smokers have nodularity and hardness for a very long time after liposuction. In non-smokers the nodularity and hardness usually resolves at around 6 months (or sooner). Pain is unusual at 8 months post-op so you may want to see your surgeon. I recommend lymphatic massage at a center that specializes in this kind of massage.

I smoke. Mini tummy tuck for loose skin?
Smoking risks
Jul 12, 2015
Smoking clamps down on blood vessels and puts you at risk for skin “necrosis” which means some of the tissue can die due to not having good enough blood circulation. Surgeons have to risk this when they operate emergently as you describe in your question, but surgeons prefer to avoid this risk to you (and to the doctor operating).Good luck quitting; I wish you the best.
