Can botox work to fix rippling caused by breast implant surgery? 2 months post op.
Botox and Rippling

Rippling is caused by a combination of factors including implant type and the quality of soft tissue coverage over the implant. A more cohesive, highly filled smooth round implant like the Sientra style 107 or the Natrelle Inspira is least likely to cause rippling.
