I had breast implants done yesterday. Why did they not do nipples?
Nipple Reconstruction
Jul 5, 2015
Nipple reconstruction is usually performed in delayed fashion a few months after final implant exchange. Exact timing is surgeon dependent, but the purpose for delaying is to allow time for your implants to settle into their final position, so nipple reconstruction can be performed more precisely and accurately.
