Breast Lift Without Implants?
Breast lift without implants
Oct 23, 2017
You can absolutely get a breast lift without implants. The lift will give you a more perky look to the breast by removing the excess skin and tightening the skin envelope. If a patient wants to have significant upper pole fullness of the breast, I often recommend a small implant as well. One thing to remember is that you can always have an implant placed later if you are unhappy with the volume after a lift.
