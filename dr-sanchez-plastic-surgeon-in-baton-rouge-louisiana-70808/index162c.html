Is my swelling normal 9 1/2 months after a tummy tuck? My surgeon says everything looks fine
Post operative swelling
Jan 15, 2019
Intermittent long term swelling can be common after an abdominoplasty. This is because the lymphatics, the specialized tissue responsible for alleviating swelling, takes awhile to completely heal. This swelling can be seen on and off for up to a year after surgery. The other possibility is that the abdominoplasty flap (area right above incision) is thicker than the area below. If that is the issue it can be easy corrected with liposuction.
