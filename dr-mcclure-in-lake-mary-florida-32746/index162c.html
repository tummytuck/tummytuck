Stitches Did Not Dissolve After Breast Reduction?
Breast Reduction
Aug 8, 2017
This is common in a breast reduction. There are hundreds of stitches placed in a breast reduction. Sometimes before they dissolve, your body can push them to the surface of the skin. If this occurs, they can be pulled out. Best of luck.
