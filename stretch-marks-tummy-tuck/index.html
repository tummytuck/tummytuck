Will tummy tuck help with my Stretch Marks?

The abdominoplasty, or tummy tuck, procedure will usually elliminate most of the stretch marks that are present below your belly button depending on the amount of excess skin that you have. Often, the surgery is performed below prior c-section scars or right through these old scars, so you don’t have to worry about any new scars down there.

It is difficult to say exactly from the picture you have posted, but it looks from this view that you could potentially have a very nice result from a full tummy tuck with muscle tightening and some additional flank liposuction. Depending on your location, prices for abdominoplasties will vary ($6000-$8000 where I am in Jacksonville, FL) but the procedure is essentially the same no matter where you have it performed. However, make sure your surgeon is a board certified plastic surgeon before making your decision. (Erez Sternberg, MD, Jacksonville Plastic Surgeon)
