Tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) in Mexico

The main objective of abdominoplasty in Mexico is the creation of normal proportions of the abdomen. There are many kinds of defects of the anterior abdominal wall, various combinations with each other, as well as varying degrees of severity, which requires the use of different surgical techniques and an individual approach to each case.

Increase in the abdomen is not only an aesthetic problem! Saggy skin fold contributes to the emergence diaper rash under the fold. Overweight provokes redistribution of the load on the spine, causing pain in the lower back. Blood stagnation occurs in the lower half of the body, and as a consequence – varicose expansion veins.

You can achieve maximum results with minimal risk of postoperative complications with right selecting of surgery. Tummy Tucks in Mexico have various kinds, which differ from each other length of cuts and volume of surgery.

Mexico Abdominoplasty

When there is a need to correct the aesthetic defect in the anterior abdominal wall?

  • If there is fat in the abdomen and in the side walls;
  • At postnatal change of recti (stretched muscles with increased spacing, a bulging belly);
  • With an excess of skin,   arising after childbirth or rapid weight loss.

With strict observance of the surgical technique, regardless of the length and location of the cut, scar is not noticeable afterwards. As a rule, it is located in the bikini area and after some time turns into a white narrow strip.
