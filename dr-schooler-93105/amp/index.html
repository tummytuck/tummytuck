Thank you for your question. If your original surgeon is a board certified plastic surgeon, you definitely should seek their advice on revision surgery, and should expect some consideration of a discount to improve your symmetry. What is bulging from this hole in my tummy tuck incision 7 1/2 months post op?
suture abscess (dissolving)
Jul 2, 2018
There are deep sutures placed during a tummy tuck that usually are long lasting but dissolvable sutures. As they dissolve, it can create inflammation and purulent drainage that looks like an infected pimple. You should discuss it with your surgeon, as you could also have infected mesh or permanent sutures causing the sinus tract.

Hi. Sorry about your frustration. It's not easy to see from your photo, but you might require both new larger implants and a revision of your lift to further tighten the skin envelope. If you just add extremely large implants alone, you might quickly sag even further.

Than you for your question. There are several approaches to your anatomy. If you have a previous midline scar, it makes sense to do the fleur-de-lis. Want more than one opinion on how my breasts look almost 3 weeks after anchor lift and augmentat
wait 6 months
May 4, 2017
The combined augmentation with lift procedure is difficult and revision rates are very high. You appear to have a good early result, although you have visible asymmetry, this will change over time so I would not judge your result for 3-6 months. You should have frequent visits with your surgeon as you go through the next several months of the healing process. Asymmetry can indicate early contracture on one side or bottoming out on the other.
Do I need a lift? Getting implants, but don't want to move my nipple.
no
Feb 20, 2017
You don't need a lift. You have a tight lower pole which can also occur also with tuberous breasts. There may have been some confusion over the lift suggestion due to that possibility. Sometimes the lower breast needs to have the fold adjusted and the constriction released to allow the implant to drop, and sometimes herniation of breast tissue can worsen at the areola after placement of an implant, prompting the need for tightening of the areola.

I am 3 1/2 weeks post op tummy tuck Is this an open tummy or just something that will heal with
mild flap necrosis
Jul 24, 2016
You have a mildly ischemia abdominal flap. You should see your surgeon to follow it. This can happen with tight closures, aggressive liposuction during the procedure, or rarely with infection. Your surgeon will be the best to advise you on wound care to allow it to heal. It should not impact your long term result.
