Will I lose my size?
Still early for final look
Apr 19, 2017
Still early to see the final shape. You still have some residual swelling around the implants and your implants are still somewhat high. They will start settling down and fill the lower aspect over the next few months. Once the swelling around the implants subsides they will look rounder and less boxy and should not look smaller proportionately.

5 days of having a wrong garment is not going to affect the final result or shape of your fat transfer unless the garment was much smaller and causing to much compression affecting the circulation to the fat cells which I doubt.
