I'm 14 yrs old and have droopy nose. Am I too young for a nose job?
Young rhinoplasty
Jun 26, 2014
Rhinoplasty can be performed safely on a person when they are physically mature. For females this is usually between 14 to 16 years of age. Also in younger patients a good support system is necessary to help in the post operative care.
7 weeks post op of Liposuction, now I have loose skin. Will this go away?
Liposuction 7 weeks post op
Jun 26, 2014
It is too early to tell what your final result will be. You need to wait at least 6 months to be able to get an accurate assessment of your outcome. Skin retraction is variable and I am sure you still have some swelling. I am sure you are anxious to get your results but be patient and this should improve greatly with time.
Can my rhinoplasty be done without osteotomy?
Rhinoplasty without osteotomy.
Jun 26, 2014
After reviewing your pictures I would recommend osteotomies after dorsal hump removal. If you had just the hump removed without osteotomies than the upper portion of your nose would be to wide. I do not believe that would give you a good cosmetic result.
