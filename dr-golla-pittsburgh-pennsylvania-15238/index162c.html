Is it safe for a diabetic to get liposuction and a tummy tuck at the same time?
Diabetes
Nov 12, 2015
I think being a diabetic increases your risk profile for many things especially healing. I think that you and your plastic surgeon should discuss how this will be impacting your complications after surgery.He may discuss limitations due to your diabetes or potential complications that you both can discuss together to decide what is best in your specific example.
