Tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) Kansas city

Tummy tuck in Kansas City – plastic surgery for correction of the abdominal wall. This surgery is recommended in the case of the formation of excess subcutaneous fat and skin tissue that occurs due to one or more pregnancies or massive weight loss and which are difficult to get rid of with the help of exercise, massage or diet.

During the surgical operation in Kansas City, which lasts typically from 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on its volume, made a long horizontal incision in the lower abdomen, just above the pubis in conjunction with a circular incision around the navel, in order to separate it from the skin. Thereafter, a large area of the skin of the abdominal wall is lifted upward toward the ribs in order to gain access to the muscle tissues. Then straight abdominal muscles are stretched toward the middle part and fixed in the new position.

Before tummy tuck in Kansas city

Then, mobilized area of ​​skin is pulled downward, parallel to that carried out the removal of excess skin and fat tissue. Navel is now temporarily under the skin of a new toned abdominal wall. The next stage – is performed modeling of a new abdominal wall so that the new hole for navel, and its contours are placed in such a way that almost never seen surgery seams. When the tummy tuck finishes, superimposed cosmetic stitches and a bandage.

Tummy tuck in Kansas City is usually performed under general anesthesia. Before surgery carried out a survey and discussion with an anesthetist in order to eliminate all possible risks associated with anesthesia. Anesthesiologist is present during the entire surgical procedure, supervise the process of anesthesia and monitors the health of the patient. Alternative to general anesthesia is a local anesthetic, carried out by subcutaneous injection. Under a local anesthetic, sedatives administered intravenously to a patient prior to surgery.

Kansas city tummy tuck

The postoperative period proceeds in Kansas City usually without complications. The patient can return in a few days to a normal life. Bandage, that imposed after surgery, is removed during the first three days. In the postoperative period, in order to avoid the formation of edema and hematoma, are advised to avoid heavy physical exertion; for 3 – 6 weeks is recommended to wear a special compression underwear.

Postoperative scar in the beginning is reddened, after a few months is white, and gradually becomes almost imperceptible. The first 6 months you should avoid getting sun on the scar.

After approximately 6 – 8 weeks after surgery in coordination with your doctor, you can again resume sports. Doctors recommend that in the postoperative period to minimize the consumption of alcoholic beverages and smoking. Also sauna and steam bath not recommended for several months after tummy tuck surgery.

These treatments, as well as other treatments in Kansas City have established themselves as highly effective therapies, that can heal almost all kinds of diseases.
