Candidate for TT surgery

The tummy tuck scar is usually lower and longer, but can be concealed as well. Choose a board certified plastic surgeon who understands what you want and he can give you options. (Eric Weiss, MD, FACS, Orange Park Plastic Surgeon)

Can I Get A Tummy Tuck?

A thorough in person exam is needed, but it appears that you would benefit from an abdominal procedure of some kind. This may be a full abdominoplasty, mini-abdominoplasty with umbilicoplasty or umbilical float.

A rectus plication is needed as well. Your surgeon will be able to tell you if your C-section scar can be removed, but it looks to me that it can be.

TT surgery scar

Please make sure you discuss all your concerns with your board certified plastic surgeon and discuss all options clearly. (Douglas L. Gervais, MD, Minneapolis Plastic Surgeon)

I suspect you will have a nice outcome from a TT surgery. The stretch marks don’t really show on the photos. Often, most or all the stretch marks in the lower abdomen can be removed. The incision can be at the same level as the c-section scar, though I cannot tell based on the photos posted if the incision that would be made around the navel would come off with the excess, or if you might have to have a small low vertical incision as well. Best answer will come during a consultation. (Jourdan Gottlieb, MD, Seattle Plastic Surgeon)
