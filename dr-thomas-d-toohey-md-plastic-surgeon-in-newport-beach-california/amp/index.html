Does Sucking and Pressing the Breast Increase the Size?
Any effects would be temporary
May 29, 2018
No sucking and pressing of the breast would lead to a long term permanent result to increase the size of your breast. There is a system called BRAVA that causes swelling in the breast tissue but without a fat transfer this result is temporary. If you are looking for an increase in cup size, a breast augmentation would probably be your best bet. As always, consult with your board certified plastic surgeon.
Signs of Capsular Contracture After Breast Augmentation?
Capsular contracture
May 29, 2018
When Capsular Contracture happens on an implant there is usually a displacement where the implant can appear higher on the chest. There is a hardening of the tissue around the implant and sometimes, pain or asymmetry. This can be diagnosed in person with your plastic surgeon, however the degree of capsular contracture that is occurring wouldn't be able to be truly diagnosed until the implant is removed and the implant pocket is inspected.

Will Electric Stimulation Machines Help Firm Facial Muscles?
No, in fact it might make your wrinkles worse.
May 29, 2018
Any use of an at home electric stimulation will give you temporary effects if any at all. It would not be effective towards the effects of "lifting" your face. In fact, it might even make your wrinkles worse, because when you flex your muscles then you make the skin move and you create a crack. For example if you never smile then you never get smile lines. If you never frown then you never get frown lines. Also, affecting the muscle will not help you with any loose skin.
