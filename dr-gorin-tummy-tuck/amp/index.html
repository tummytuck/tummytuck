What is the most effective oral pain medication after Tummy Tuck/Mommy Makeover with Breast Augmenta
Pain control after mommy makeover varies
Nov 17, 2014
Thanks for the question. Each surgeon will have their own specific protocol for postop pain management. Narcotics, like percoset, are common place. I Rx these for my patients. However, I also like to use a muscle relaxant (like soma or flexeril), Neurontin (gabapentin), and Celebrex.
