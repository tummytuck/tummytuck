Trying to decide how many cc’s to get.. I am 4’11 100lbs currently a 36b… I want to be a DD.
Determining implant size
Aug 4, 2014
The primary determinant of the implant size you can use is the width of your breast. In general the implant should not be wider than your breast. In order to be a double D cup size you’re probably going to need a high-profile implant in the 450 cc range. Based on your height and chest with a medium profile implant will be too wide for you. Bring in pictures with you to your consultation of breasts that you like their size and shape. Good luck
