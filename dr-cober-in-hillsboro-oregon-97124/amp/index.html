Is it normal the actual implants are smaller than the tissue expanders?
expander to implant exchange
Apr 26, 2017
I find that permanent implants placed for the same volume in expanders look about 10-20% smaller, so I strive to get my patients to look 10-20% larger with their final expanded look. It also takes several months for the expanded tissues to relax and "settle", so early on things often look flattened without much projection. Try to be patient!
