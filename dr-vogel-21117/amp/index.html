Hair transplant to trauma region: can it be done?
Transplant to scar area on the scalp
Aug 27, 2018
Hair can be transplanted into a scarred area on the scalp. You should be evaluated by an experienced hair transplant surgeon to determine if this is the best option based on the scar characteristics. If the blood supply to the area is good then grafts will do well in the scar.
10 days after a hair transplant, but I still have lots of blank areas, does this mean my HT was a failu
Hair Transplant Growth
Aug 27, 2018
10 days after Hair Transplant is very early to see growth. Typically, hairs that are transplanted are visible immediately following surgery. However, by 2 weeks without PRP treatment most hairs will no longer be visible at the surface. New follicles will start to grow around 4-5 months. It can take eight months to see the full result of a hair transplant.
What is the upkeep for PRP therapy?
PRP Protocol
Aug 27, 2018
Protocols for injections and maintenance vary from provider to provider. We generally recommend three treatments in the first year with maintenance after that. Maintenance is a collaborative discussion based on the patients results and is either every six months or yearly. As always, make sure your provider is experience in PRP specific to hair loss.
No PRP results after 3 months? I’m 23, on finasteride.
Results with PRP and Finasteride
Aug 27, 2018
PRP works to thicken hair diameters. Many patients notice hair thickening and looking better around month four following treatment. So it may be too soon to measure results. It is always a good idea to make sure your provider is very well versed in PRP. All PRP is not the same. It should be injected in the region of the hair follicles and mixed appropriately.

Am I expecting results too soon? Is there a possibility that the grafts didn't take but fell out or wer
Hair Transplant Results
Jul 12, 2018
At four months, it is still early to see results. Generally, PRP can help with an early and more robust growth of the transplant but this is not the case for everyone. It can take 8-9 months to see results from the transplant. A discussion with your surgeon can always be helpful in evaluating how you are doing at 4 months post-transplant, especially when you have concerns.
Should only single hair grafts be used in the front of the hairline? 5 months post-op.
Natural Front Hairline
Jul 12, 2018
In order to make a hair transplant look natural in the front, 1-2 hair grafts can be used and the design should be slightly uneven. This is because no one has a straight line when you look at the hairline. A follow-up with your surgeon to discuss your concerns is warranted as they will know what hairs were placed in the design of the hairline.
