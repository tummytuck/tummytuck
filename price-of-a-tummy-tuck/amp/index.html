Price of a tummy tuck

Tummy tuck prices vary by location and surgeon. You have to be able to compare total costs. Check to see if the fees includes all expenses such as, surgeon fee, anesthesia fee, operating room fee, garments if needed, lab fees. Some offices even add extra fees not seen in the original quote.

Check to see if liposuction of the abdomen is included or not. There is usually additional fees for liposuction of other areas but abdominal lipo should be included int the TT fee. Also Mini abdominoplasty costs less than full abdominoplasty. Our office gives a global fee for the procedure which means there are no additional fees.
