Smoking before and after tummy tuck?
Reduce complications
Apr 11, 2017
All types of smoking, including marijuana and vapor products are pulmonary and lung irritants that significantly affect your anesthetic risks. Many types of smoke affect surgical risks also. You have stopped early enough for you May surgery.
No physician is going to tell you it is ever OK to resume smoking until you appear to be completely healed. As for marijuana that depends upon which state you live in.
Do I need a breast lift or could I do a nipple lift with implants?
Can't always avoid scars
Apr 11, 2017
Women usually avoid breast lifts because of the scars on the breasts. With the appearance of your breast shape and nipple position you are going to need a breast lift. Most patients choose an implant if they wish a size increase or additional upper fullness that cannot be created by a breast lift alone.
This is a difficult procedure and you should look for a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon, a diplomat of The American Board of Plastic Surgery.
