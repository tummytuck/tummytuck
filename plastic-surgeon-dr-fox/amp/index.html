Do them at the same time

Talk to your surgeon about combing the procedures at the same time. You plastic surgeon can elevate the flap. Then your hysterectomy can be completed and closure with the abdominoplasty completes your surgery.
Do mini tummy tucks and full tummy tucks carry the same risks?
Abdominoplasty
Feb 6, 2020
All surgery has some risks but both a mini and a full tummy tuck are generally safe procedures. The full tummy tuck has some increased risk of delayed healing but the benefits usually out weigh the risks.
Do tummy tucks take care of and get rid of lumps and loose skin due to liposuction?
Abdominoplasty
Feb 6, 2020
There is a greater scar trade off with a tummy tuck than with liposuction. Skin excess and irregularities may exist after liposuction depending on skin elasticity. A variation on a tummy tuck can improve or tighten excess skin.
Do I need an extended tummy tuck or CTT?
Abdominoplasty
Feb 6, 2020
Congratulations on your weight loss! You should get a nice improvement with and Abdominoplasty addressing the anterior abdominal wall without a circumferential excision or a corset approach.
How long do I need to take off work after a tummy tuck?
Time off
Feb 6, 2020
How long you take off from work will depend on how physically demanding your job is. However, most patients will be tired for 2 weeks following surgery and physical activity is limited for 4-6 weeks. Your incision design is something you should definitely discuss with your surgeon.

Abdominoplasty
