Is is normal that I am almost six weeks post op still experiencing pain and I'm swollen?
Healing
Sep 5, 2018
One of the hardest things to predict after surgery is how much pain a patient will have.  Even with the same operation patients will have a wide variation in pain level.  Stay positive you are in the early stages of healing--after surgery the body is in the acute phase of healing with the most swelling and discomfort for 6 weeks after that things continue to improve for a full year.  So you are just starting the phase where things get better and better every week.
