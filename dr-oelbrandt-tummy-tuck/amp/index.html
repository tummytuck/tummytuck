loose skin after liposuction
Nov 25, 2016
There seems to be an important amount of loose skin + possibly weak muscles.

This is only solved by a mini( lower ) tummy tuck, or a full tummy tuck (upper and lower ) .

Liposuction is only treating the subcutaneous fat layer , and not the visceral fat. Liposuction may cause deflation of the skin, just like weight loss may do.

Therefore the right treatment is :

– loose weight

– start working out and strengthen your muscles

– tummy tuck , possibly with muscle repair
