How Much Do Breast Implants Cost?
total cost will vary
May 15, 2015
The total costs will vary based on your surgeon, the geographical location, type of implants, and hospital/ facility/anesthesia fees. In additional, the complexity of your case may sometimes increase the fees as it necessitates longer operative times, ie: if you need a lift or for a revision. Post gynocamastia surgery swelling?
Post-operative swelling
Aug 25, 2014
It is very common to have swelling and bruising right after surgery. Gynecomastia surgery typically entails either liposuction alone or with direct excision of the breast tissue or skin. All of these factors can have an impact on the amount of swelling you have and your ultimate result. My patients are placed in a compressive garment after surgery but this is dependent on surgeon's preference. Please check in with your surgeon about your concerns.
