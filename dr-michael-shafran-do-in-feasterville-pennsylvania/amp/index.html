Is Sculpsure supposed to be done with gel?
Is Sculpsure supposed to be done with gel?
Dec 3, 2017
Very good question. The technology behind the treatment therapy of Sculpsure is based on laser wavelengths heating and disrupting the fat cells beneath the dermis, without damaging your external skin. Gels may need to be applied for other types of treatments, such as ultrasound based technologies, but not with Sculpsure. A Lux Lotion may be sprayed on the applicators, which appears as a fine mist, but is not a gel-like substance. Hope this was helpful.

I had Smarlipo and running fever should I go back to finish other area?
Low Grade Fever
Dec 3, 2017
A low grade fever (below 100.4) is a fairly common occurrence following any surgical intervention within the first 24 hours. Continue to monitor it as it should gradually resolve as your body recovers from your operation. Have your surgeon re-evaluate you prior to moving forward with your next area of liposuction, as it is an elective procedure and you should be in optimal health prior to your next surgery.
