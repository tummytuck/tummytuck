Tummy tuck in Denver

Tummy tuck Denver before and after

A tummy tuck in Denver – is complex and extensive operation, during which the surgeon not only removes excess skin and subcutaneous fat, but eliminates the intermuscular diastasis, forms a new umbilical opening, fixes aponeurosis. Tummy tuck usually lasts 2-4 hours. Abdominoplasty can conduct only if there are indications for this intervention, so each patient of clinic in Denver undergoing tests before tummy tuck. Tummy tuck can be small and large.
  • Small tummy tuck If the changes in the abdominal area are not essential, the plastic surgeon in Denver performs a small abdominoplasty for the patient. The doctor makes a horizontal incision above the pubic bone, turns away and fixes the skin and tissue, sews the rectus abdominis. After that removes excess skin and subcutaneous fat, the edges of the surgical wound are pulled together and stitched.
  • Large tummy tuck in Denver. During a large tummy tuck doctor conducts incision not only on the bikini line, but also around the navel. Plastic surgeon connects the muscles of the abdomen, forms belly button at the place where it should be. After excision of excess fat and skin, the incisions are sutured. Results of the surgery are obvious – after a short time the patient’s abdomen becomes taut and elastic.

Postoperative and rehabilitation period after tummy tuck in Denver

Tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) in Denver, CO

Since tummy tuck in Denver is a long open surgery, which is performed under general anesthesia, the patient after its completion will be remain in the hospital for 2-3 days. During this time, there may be swelling in the area of ​​the scar, the accumulation of fluid in the postoperative wound (liquid, spotting). In order to facilitate the outflow, doctors during suturing of incision is left gum drain or tube. Planned bandaging are made within 10 days after discharge from the hospital. For quick recovery after surgery the patient should follow these recommendations:

  • Eliminate physical activity for some time;
  • Wear a compression garment.

In 1 month after hospital discharge, the patient should visit a surgeon who is visually estimate the speed of the process of tissue regeneration scar.

Tummy tuck procedure in Denver

Currently, there are five basic techniques for tummy tuck in clinics in Denver:

  • The standard tummy tuck – remove the excess skin, suturing the abdominal muscles and the formation of a new navel through an incision in the bikini area;
  • Mini tummy tuck in Denver – is characterized in that during surgery the number and size of sections is minimized. This considerably reduces the risk of complications, as well as the term of the rehabilitation period;
  • Extended tummy tuck is used in cases where there is a strong sagging skin on the abdomen, hips, thighs and upper regions of the back;
  • Endoscopic tummy tuck in Denver – useful if you need to tighten the abdominal muscles and does not involve the removal of excess skin. This method is considered to be less traumatic and often combined with liposuction.

Most often tummy tuck in Denver is performed with general anesthesia, but there are cases where more appropriate to use local anesthesia. This plastic surgery involves the performance of the two cuts in the abdomen and navel. After that, the part of the skin of the abdomen rises up and the muscles are sewn to strengthen the abdominal wall. After that excess skin is removed and belly button again moved to the center of the abdomen. Depending on the method and the state of health of the patient surgery can last from two to five hours.

After completion of the tummy tuck, surgeons are left two tubes in the patient’s groin by which performed outlet of fluid from the operated area. Also superimposed compression bandage on his stomach after tummy tuck.

Recovering from a tummy tuck in Denver takes about a week, during which the patient must be in the hospital. In addition, over two months after the tummy tuck it is necessary to wear a special compression garment and to refrain from intense physical stress.

In some cases with a tummy tuck in Denver also performed ​​the removal of a hernia at the front of the abdominal wall. However, surgeons do not recommend to combine this type of plastic surgery with other operations.

The presence or absence of a noticeable scar on the abdomen depends on the skills of surgeons. In Denver employs true professionals, highly owning all the subtleties of overlay cosmetic seams and using only the best high quality sutures. That is why through certain time after abdominoplasty scars are barely visible and almost do not stand out on the skin.
