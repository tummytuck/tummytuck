Advice for Exercise After Breast Augmentation?
Exercise after Breast Augmentation
May 8, 2018
Strenuous or athletic exercise is strongly discouraged for a few weeks after breast augmentation for a few reason. First of all, exercise raises heart rate and blood pressure. This can result in bleeding from any surgical area. Second, it requires strain of muscles in the area (even if you are just on an exercise bike). You are spending a lot of money and time on enhancing your breast contour. Why take the chance by exercising and possibly complications?
