Nov 15, 2019
Hello and thank you for your question. In order to have optimal results after a tummy tuck it is best that you have reached your goal weight. I recommend calling the surgeon you have in mind asking for their BMI requirements this will give you an idea as to where your weight should be. After a tummy tuck it is best to wait at least 6 weeks prior to getting back into the gym, it is recommended to ease back into your old routine.
