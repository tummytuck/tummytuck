Is a rhinoplasty appropriate for hump removal

You do have excessive projection of your nose from your face and this could be improved as well as the bump removed with reduction of the bony and cartilaginous dorsum of your nose. Tummy tuck versus diet and exercise following 2 c-sections
Jun 9, 2014
You definitely need a tummy tuck. Your panniculus is too large and your stretch marks are too severe to fix with diet and exercise. My nose is deviated to the right and is asymmetrical. What procedure would you recommend?
Options to correct significan nasal asymmetry
Jun 9, 2014
You have deviation of the long and cartilaginous dorsum of your nose and a large dorsal lump. This could be corrected with reduction of the bone and cartilage of your nose and in-fracturing of the bone to get alignment. You also have lack of supra tip definition and a bulky and amorphous nasal tip. These tip problems could be corrected nicely through on open rhinoplasty approach.
Breast REDUCTION vs. Breast LIFT….what is the better option to relieve pain of heavy breast? (ph
Breast reduction versus breast lift
Jun 9, 2014
Since you are symptomatic with pain then reduction of your breasts is the best approach. You also have very large areolas that would be reduced at the same time. I would use the vertical reduction approach which will eliminate the long scar in the inframammary crease area that is present with the old standard breast reduction procedures.
I got saline implants in August 2001. I’m noticing ripples under my breast. Is that normal?
Are ripples normal with saline implants?
Jun 9, 2014
One of the issues with saline implants besides the high incidence of deflations is the unnatural look and feel in some patients. Ripples either palpated or visual on direct view are a real problem. We frequently do implant exchanges and swap out the old saline implants with the new cohesive gel silicone implants. These new silicone implants have a much more natural look and feel.
