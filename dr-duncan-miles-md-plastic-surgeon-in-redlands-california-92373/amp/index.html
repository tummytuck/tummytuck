What is the best procedure to treat misshapen and sagging breasts without implants?
Lift and fat grafting
May 17, 2014
Thank you for the helpful photos. I think your assessment is correct. I would recommend a 2 stage approach. Have your lift first, then in a second stage use the BRAVA system before fat grafting. It will give the most consistent results.
Is there anyway for the patient to prevent muscle flex deformity? Is the patient to blame if this occu
Animation deformity
Apr 7, 2014
Hello. The flexion or animation deformity you are describing is very common with submuscluar implants. It is no one's fault. In some woman it can be very noticeable. A position change to a subglandular placement will fix the problem. If you are very thin, fat grafting to the upper pole of the breast can help camouflage the movement. You will need to discuss the options and fees with your surgeon .
