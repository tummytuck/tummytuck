Can I get a good result with breast augmentation alone (no lift)?
Lift ?
Mar 3, 2015
You do not need a lift to accomplish a really nice result. Your nipples are well above your inframammary fold and I think you could undergo just a standard breast augmentation and achieve a very natural look. One recommendation I would give you would be to consider an anatomically shaped cohesive gel implant, or what is commonly referred to as a gummy bear type implant. This will give you a very nice shape and I think a wonderful result after breast augmentation without a lift.
