The price range for tummy tuck is very wide – the difference between the minimum price and the maximum value can be up to 20,000 thousand dollars. You should understand that the market is free in price formation.

This means that the surgeon and the hospital can offer a price for tummy tuck surgery that it deems necessary. However, the logic in the pricing is still there. Although not at all.

The fee for a tummy tuck includes the surgeon’s fee, anaesthesia, facility, garments/pain pumps, and at times the cost of an overnight stay. The fee will also vary depending on whether you are having a full or mini tummy tuck.

The costs can range from about $5500 for a mini to over $9000 for a full.

This is a complex and serious procedure – make sure your surgeon is appropriately board certified and has hospital privileges in plastic surgery at a local respected hospital. Be careful of bargains or steep discounts.

Find your surgeon first then discuss fees. Doing it the other way around increases your chances of having an undesirable outcome.

The price for a tummy tuck in Orange County California will include:

  1. The surgeon’s fee ($5,000 to $7,000)
