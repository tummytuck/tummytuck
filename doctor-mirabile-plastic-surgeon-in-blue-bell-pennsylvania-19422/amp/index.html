How Common After a Full Tummy Tuck is Liposuction?
Liposuction after tummy tucks
Aug 27, 2013
It is common to perform liposuction after tummy tuck surgery to treat the thick flap of abdominal tissue that is pulled down and sewn to the bottom flap. This upper flap can not be treated with aggressive liposuction at the time of surgery because it is possible to compromise its blood supply and negatively effect healing. After healing is complete, liposuction is a great way to thin out that flap.
