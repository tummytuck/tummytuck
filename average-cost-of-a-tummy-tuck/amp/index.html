Average cost of a tummy tuck

First, since I do not perform “cookie-cutter” operations, my prices vary based on the patient’s needs and the extent of surgery required. Surgery can range from a mini-tuck to a full abdominoplasty with liposuction. Abdominoplasty in women who have had multiple births (I have operated on several who had triplets after in vitro fertilization) is a more extensive and expensive procedure.

Anesthesia/facility charges for the abdominoplasty start at about $1500 for the smallest operation and can go up to $2350. My surgical fee for a full abdominoplasty begins at $6500 and goes up to a maximum of $10,000. I do not hold to the paradigm that the higher the fee, the better the surgeon.

Distinctions in overhead expenses in various parts of the country, particularly malpractice insurance and staffing, accounts for the disparity between prices. Additionally, some physicians try to get as much as they can from the patient. I try to establish a longterm relationship and, if that means that my fees are lower than those in Manhattan or Long Island, so be it. I believe that, as plastic surgeons, we have to be responsive to the demographic of the communities we serve.

Average cost of abdominoplasty

Average cost of a tummy tuck usually vary by geographic area. The surgical fees vary from $3500-4000 for a “mini” or lower abdominoplasty to $5000-7000 for a more standard abdominoplasty with some liposuction. The “mini” abdominoplasty is usually performed in our fully certified office OR under “twilight” anesthesia which adds $1250-1450 for the OR/Anesthesia costs. Average cost of a tummy tuck surgery

There are 3 costs that go into every procedure: surgeon fee, facility fee, and anesthesiology fee. Most surgeons cannot adjust the facility/anesthesiology fee. Regarding the surgeon’s fee, there are three main variable that will determine this cost: geography, extent of the surgery, surgeon experience. In a major metropolitan city the costs can be very different. Outside of Washington DC where I practice, the average cost all inclusive ranges from ~$7500-9500.

Average cost of a tummy tuck how much

There is no set price for tummy tuck. Depending on the size of the excess skin and fat that need to be removed, operative time, and facility/anesthesia fee, the price of the tummy tuck can vary greatly. It can range from $6000-$12000. If one needs liposuction of the flanks or hips, the cost may be higher.

Surgical fees will vary from region to region and by surgeon. We are always happy to give a ballpark quote to a patient over the phone, but a tummy tuck is tailored to what the patients need. Some need a full abdominoplasty, some only need a mini, sometimes liposuction is needed, sometimes tightening the muscle is not necessary. Each of these procedures requires a different amount of time in the operating room and at a different cost. Make sure you see a surgeon certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Your consultation is when you will learn about the specific choices you have and why each is recommended or not given your situation. After the consultation, I am able to give a patient a more specific quote for the best surgery to meet her needs.

Abdominoplasty average cost

An abdominoplasty can range from $4000 to $14,000 depending on patient size, address of facility, surgeon, etc. Malpractice costs and overhead vary from place to place. These costs are passed on to patients. I would suggest looking at before and after pictures. Try to obtain a personal referral. And shoot for somewhere in the middle of the price range.
