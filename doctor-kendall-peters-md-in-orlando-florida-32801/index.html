Would a breast lift give me rounder breasts with lots of upper pole?
Breast lift
Apr 13, 2020
Although a breast lift improves the overall shape and appearance of breasts that are droopy or saggy by removing excess stretched out skin, it does not add volume or upper pole fullness to the breasts like an implant does. A breast augmentation with implants is really the most ideal way to obtain fuller, rounder breasts with upper pole fullness and enhanced cleavage.
The surgeon at my consultation said I can't get a BBL because of the way I'm built?
BBL surgical candidate
Apr 8, 2020
In order to be an ideal surgical candidate for a Brazilian butt lift, there must be a sufficient amount of fatty tissue in the abdominal region. This is because the fat is liposuctioned from this area and transferred back into the buttocks. If there is not enough fat present in this region, it will not yield enough fat for the transfer process.

Can I get some upper pole improvement by doing an implant vs. a lift?
Upper pole fullness
Apr 6, 2020
Although the overall shape and contour of the breast will be improved by performing a lift, the only way to enhance the upper pole fullness of the breasts is with placement of an implant. This is why a "mastopexy augmentation" is oftentimes the best way to enhance the overall shape and contour of the breasts, especially following chilbirth and/or breast feeding.
Does it matter how much cc of fat is taken out of the body? The more the better or is it really base
Fat reduction
Apr 6, 2020
Fat reduction in the form of liposuction typically involves the removal of no more than 5 liters worth of fat. Once this amount is exceeded, the risk for electrolyte imbalances, excessive bleeding, heart arrhythmias, and tissue/nerve damage begin to increase beyond an acceptable amount.
I've always hated my chin and jaw, how can I reshape my lower face?
Lower face contouring
Apr 6, 2020
Depending on each individual's aesthetic goals, the lower face can be contoured with a combination of injectable fillers along the jawline and within the chin in order to define these areas, as well as treatment of the submental region with a fat reduction procedure such as Kybella or Coolsculpting.
Should I be concerned about this leg mole and how can I get rid of it?
Suspicious Mole
Apr 6, 2020
Hello and thank you for your question. The best way to manage a suspicious mole is to have it biopsied by a board certified dermatologist. This is really the only way to confirm a diagnosis. If the biopsy results indicate the need for removal of the mole, a plastic surgeon referral may sometimes be necessary.
How Long Does Tummy Tuck Surgery Swelling Last?
Tummy Tuck recovery
Sep 12, 2019
Swelling after a tummy tuck typically takes about 2-3 months to completely resolve, however, most of the swelling has improved by 4-6 weeks. I let my patients resume cardio activities after 4 weeks, but restrict their abdominal activity for 3-6 months to allow adequate time for the muscle plication to completely heal.
How Long Should You Wear Your Binder/Garment for After a Tummy Tuck?
Compression garments
Sep 12, 2019
I recommend wearing a compression garment for at least 6 weeks following a tummy tuck and/or liposuction. This will help with swelling and skin contracture, but it will also help the skin adhere back down to the abdominal muscles and minimize fluid collection.
