When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
Post-surgical swelling will take months to completely resolve
Nov 4, 2013
Swelling occurs with almost every surgery. It usually peaks after 3 days and then starts to subside. Compression can help minimize the overall degree of swelling you obtain. The swelling will start to go down in the first 3 weeks. It will take months, however, for the swelling to completely resolve. Although everyone is different, a general rule of thumb is that you will be 90% done by 3 months and 100% done by 6 months. Most people know their final bra size within that 3-6 month window.
How Long Does Tummy Tuck Surgery Swelling Last?
Swelling after a tummy tuck
Aug 29, 2013
Swelling after a tummy tuck lasts for several months. During the procedure, the skin is elevated off of the muscle layer. This divides some of the lymphatic vessels. Because of this, it takes a while for all of the swelling to resolve. For most patients, about 90% of the swelling has resolved by 3 months, and 100% by 6 months. Keep in mind, therefore, that if you are already happy with your results after one month, you will be ecstatic after 6 months!
