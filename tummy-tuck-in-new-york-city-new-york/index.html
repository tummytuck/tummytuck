Tummy tuck surgery in NYC

What is a tummy tuck in New York ? Behind this name hides surgery, which aims to improve the shape of the abdomen. It’s quite extensive and difficult surgery, allowing the patient to rid of unaesthetic figure, sagging belly and a variety of complexes. Currently, two basic types of tummy tuck in New York: abdominoplasty with the transfer of the navel and the same operation without moving the navel.

New York City plastic surgeons perform this operation for over twenty years, allowing them to speak with confidence about the great experience not only in operations, but also in care of patients. Very important to carefully prepare the patient before surgery. A full clinical examination allows the fullest assess the condition of the patient, to minimize the risk to his health. In New York City a tummy tuck is performed on the background of high-quality and safe anesthesia.

Tummy tuck in New York City, NY

In the preparation stage tummy tuck requires advance planning lines of cuts. In most cases, tummy tuck involves an incision in the bikini area. After the operation remains barely noticeable thin scar in the natural skin crease, which can be successfully hidden under even the most elegant lingerie.

Tummy tuck is performed in New York City in women and men. As a rule, these people are the middle and older age groups, often after forty years. Women can become owners of unaesthetic anterior abdominal wall after childbirth or dramatic weight loss. In such situations, the structure of the skin and subcutaneous abdominal stretch, become weakened, stretch marks appear. In addition, the tummy tuck is recommended for the correction of discrepancies rectus abdominis.
