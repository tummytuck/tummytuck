Why are patients bent over after Tummy Tuck? Is it the muscle repair or incision?
Why bent over
Nov 3, 2015
Patients are told to keep flexed or bent over for the first two weeks to reduce tension on the skin incision and the muscle tightening. Tension on the skin can cause a widened scar. Everyone heals a little differently so the way the scar heals depends on many factors but it’s important with any surgery to follow your doctor’s post op instructions to achieve the best result.
