Guarantees

First, there are no guarantees in medicine or surgery (or in life for that matter.) Remember, cosmetic surgery is surgery. With any surgery, there are risks and limitations. There are variables that you can control such as selecting a board certified plastic surgeon who is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Make sure that your procedure is done in a fully accredited surgical facility. Review many before and after photos tummy tuck.

Great tummy tuck results image

But in cosmetic surgery, there are several variables that you or your PS can’t control including but not limited to your healing potential, your past medical history and overall health status (Are you overweight, a diabetic, a former smoker?). This is surgery and ALL surgeries carry small risks just like with anything in medicine or surgery. Its important that if you opt for cosmetic surgery, you can accept these risks.

Fortunately, the risks are very very low, but they can happen – even to the most experienced and ethical surgeon. The best you can do is to do your homework and the odds are overwhelmingly in your favor you will have a great result if you are indeed a good candidate for a tummy tuck. Part of selecting a board certified PS is to select a professional who will help you to get to a healed state god forbid in the very rare instance there is a problem. (C.
