How many days are you in bed after a tummy tuck?
Dangers of bed rest
May 11, 2017
As other answers have indicated, lying in bed after tummy tuck, puts you at risk for serious complications.
I know a woman whose daughters brought her meals in bed thinking they were doing their mother a favor. She ended up in the ICU with a blood clot from her leg that had broken off and gone to her lung.
