Early postop after Tummy Tuck
Dec 18, 2016
It is normal to have swelling from the trauma of any surgery. This is especially true in the first several months after tummy tuck. The other possible problem to consider is a seroma. In your pictures, you seem more swollen below the belly button, this is a typical area to have swelling if you have a seroma. You should see your surgeon, to be examined to make sure you do not have a seroma. Your surgeon can usually drain this in the office, if you do have a seroma....
