Tummy tuck in Baltimore, Maryland

Tummy tuck in Baltimore, Maryland

Tummy tuck in Baltimore performed for all patients who are dreaming to become owners of a beautiful and toned stomach. Tummy tuck in Baltimore recommended for patients who have excess skin and subcutaneous adipose tissue of the anterior abdominal wall, with the presence of stretch marks or discrepancies of recti.

Tummy tuck is performed for patients which have contraindications to liposuction. Hernia surgery can be combined with a tummy tuck for patients who have umbilical, inguinal, postoperative hernia. Tummy tuck in Baltimore performed under general anesthesia. Excess subcutaneous fat and skin is removed, overly stretched muscle layer is sutured. Formed a new umbilical opening into which the is fixed umbilical ring. Wounds are sutured with considerable tension in layers. Duration of the surgery in Baltimore is 60-120 min.

Tummy tuck in Baltimore is a surgery to excision of excess skin, fat deposits. This surgery is quite voluminous, lasts 2 hours. Before surgery the patient should get tested. tummy tuck can be small or large in volume of surgery intervention.

Tummy tuck Baltimore, Maryland

  • Large tummy tuck. Performed an incision around the navel on the lower bound (on bikini line), the upper limit depends on the volume of excised tissue . Incision resembles a leaf of flower. With the skin is removed subcutaneous fat, stapled divergent rectus abdominis, formed a new hole for the navel. Edges of a wound are pulled together and stitched. Thus, during surgery removes excess skin and fat belly gets proportionality and aesthetics.
  • Small tummy tuck in Baltimore. The principle of surgery is the same as a big tummy tuck, but surgery is less voluminous. Fatty tissue is excised, the skin flap is excised to the navel, navel is not moved, the wound is sutured to the bikini line. Small tummy tuck indicated for patients with small changes.

Tummy tuck Baltimore
