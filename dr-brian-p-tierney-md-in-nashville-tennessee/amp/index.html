Are Inspira moderate implant sizes comparable to mentor moderate plus sizes?
Inspira implants
Apr 10, 2016

In general the inspira moderate implants(SRM) will have more projection per base width compared to the Mentors.  In other words, you get more projection with a smaller base width with the Inspira implants. Abdominoplasty and liposuction

What's the Average Recovery Time of Breast Reconstruction for a Mastectomy?
Recovery time after mastectomy for reconstruction
Nov 17, 2015
The most popular type of reconstruction after mastectomy is a implant based reconstruction. A tissue expander is placed at the time of mastectomy. A second stage surgery is usually performed 2 to 3 months later. This is when the permanent implant is placed. The initial recovery after mastectomy and reconstruction is about six weeks.
