Why am I not flat after a full TT?!
Still Swelling
Feb 11, 2017
This early post op you still have routine surgical swelling. I have found there are two phases of swelling with tummy tucks. The first phase is the post op swelling and possible a mild fluid collection that can last for 6 to 12 weeks. Then, around 3 to 5 months out there is another subtle period of swelling that I believe has to do with lymph fluid collection in the tissues.
Give it time.
Gordon Telepun, MD

Risky To Be Aggressive With Liposuction In The Front

When doing a full abdominoplasty it is very disruptive to the blood supply to the front skin flap. Therefore, it is dangerous to do liposuction to the front skin flap and compromise the blood supply even more. It could lead to poor wound healing and skin necrosis. I think your surgeon did the right thing. Perhaps communicating this problem with you ahead of time would have helped you understand this issue better.
