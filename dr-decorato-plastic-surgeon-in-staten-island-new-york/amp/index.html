Am I a good candidate for a doughnut lift as I don't like the lollipop scar?
Best breast lift procedure
Mar 3, 2020
Based upon the photos provided, a circumareolar mastopexy or "donut lift" would not provide the result I believe you desire. The circumareolar lift will only provide a maximum of 2 cm (less than an inch) of lift. Some type of vertical scar must be added to obtain the best result. Fortunately, the vertical scar can heal very well when performed correctly.
