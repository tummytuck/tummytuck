What are symptoms for a trapped nerve in abdomen following Tummy tuck?
Pain in abdomen 7 months post op
Jan 21, 2016
Pain to the side of the belly button months after surgery is either muscle pinching or nerve pinching from the muscle tightening sutures. It usually improves spontaneously. The first treatment you might consider is a steroid injection at the site of the pain. If 2 of these a few weeks apart don't improve the condition, then cutting the muscle tightening suture may help - this can be done in the office usually through the periumbilical scar line.Jonathan Freed, MD
