Tummy tuck in Indianapolis, Indiana

Tummy tuck in Indianapolis

Tummy tuck in Indianapolis is a surgery for reducing and tightening of the abdominal muscles by removing excess skin and fat from the middle and lower abdomen. In the rhythm of modern life, women and men are not always able to devote much time to appearance, lead an active lifestyle, eat right. In addition, various types of defects occur due to pregnancy and childbirth, or in the case of large weight gain and rapid weight loss.

Tummy tuck in Indianapolis will provide fit and beautiful belly. This operation is one of the most common in clinics of Indianapolis. This surgery is done by women and men of all ages from 18 years.

Indications for plastic belly Indianapolis:

  • excess skin and subcutaneous adipose tissue in the anterior abdominal wall with the divergence of the rectus muscles of the abdomen, or the presence of stretch marks;
  • contraindications to liposuction;
  • umbilical, inguinal, postoperative hernia, when combined with a tummy tuck.

Tummy tuck Indianapolis

Contraindications to tummy tuck in Indianapolis:

  • scarring from previous operations above the level of the navel;
  • diabetes;
  • cardiac or pulmonary insufficiency;
  • obese;
  • planning to become pregnant within the next year after abdominoplasty.

Tummy tuck is not a treatment for obesity in Indianapolis and is rather a measure of last resort when other methods do not help or impossible. For this reason, before establishing the cause of obesity, apply any weight loss methods to achieve the desired result. In the case when a tummy tuck is performed with the maximum weight of the patient, then the result after weight loss can be spoiled due to the new formation of excess skin.

Diagnosis of the patient precedes to surgery, during which doctors will assess health and choose the most suitable technique tummy tuck in Indianapolis, depending on the number of removed tissue, the presence or absence of stretch marks, scars from previous operations, the thickness of the subcutaneous adipose tissue, hernias, individual patient characteristics .

Technique of tummy tuck in Indianapolis:

Before tummy tuck Indianapolis

  • Partial tummy tuck – the operation involves the least amount of cuts that will eliminate the complications and reduce the rehabilitation period. Excess skin was removed from the bottom of the abdominal wall, parallel to liposuction.
  •  Standard tummy tuck – the operation, during which the incision will have be made in the bikini area, will be removed unnecessary layers of the skin, subcutaneous fat, muscles of the abdominal wall are sutured, a new form of navel is formed.
  • Extended tummy tuck – operation, which is an indication for a strong sagging skin in the abdomen, on the sides, back, upper thigh area. Performed two cuts in bikini and navel area.
  • Endoscopic tummy tuck – the least traumatic way of abdominoplasty in Indianapolis. This type of surgery involves only the muscles tightening without removing the excess skin. Typically, endoscopic plastic combined with liposuction.

Tummy tuck in Indianapolis, IN

Tummy tuck in Indianapolis performed with general anesthesia or epidural anesthesia. During surgery, the surgeon removes excess of subcutaneous fat and skin, sutured stretched muscle layer, formed a new umbilical opening in which is fixed belly button ring. Wound sutured in layers with considerable tension. Abdominoplasty in Indianapolis can last from 60 minutes to five hours.

Hospitalization period is 1-3 days. There are regular bandaging after the surgery for two weeks. During the month, you must wear a special compression garment or support bandage that ensures even scarring of the skin, as well as you need avoid heavy physical activity.

A month later, the patient may already be back to normal, everyday life, getting rid of the “fatty apron”. The final result is evaluated after six – twelve months. Cosmetic effect of abdominoplasty is stored for a long time, if supported by a balanced diet and exercise programs.
