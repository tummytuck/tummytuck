How Much Does a Tummy Tuck Cost?
Cost of Tummy Tuck
Jun 30, 2020
The average cost of a complete tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) is about $10,000 and includes liposuction of the mid trunk, lab tests, anesthesiologist, an overnight stay in a hospital setting and three girdles (one before surgery and two after surgery).

I'm 11 days post op, mommy makeover, I still have my drains, and I've developed a Seroma.
TT drains
Jun 26, 2016
Hello and thank you for your question. Creating the vacuum in the drains collectors frequently (every 30-60 min) during the day is important. Your drains could be removed soon depending on the amount of fluid collected each day. Seroma can infrequently develop even in the presence of drains. An examination of your abdomen will shed light as to whether there is a seroma or not. Suggest to call your surgeon.
