Tattoo after tummy tuck

Tattoo removal after tummy tuck

If you are going to have a tummy tuck anyhow for contouring of the abdomen, I would have that done first and wait a few months to see how everything looks.

There is a high likelihood that a significant amount of your tattoo would simply be removed with the tummy tuck, making laser of that much of the tattoo unnecessary, and what remains might be small enough and low enough below the bikini line that it isn’t a problem for you.

If it is, then you can have laser tattoo removal on that reduced portion of the tattoo after the skin heals at about 3 months postop. (Joseph L. Grzeskiewicz, MD, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

Best Option Is To Have Abdominoplasty Followed By Laser Treatments To Remove Any Residual Tattoo

Tattoo after tummy tuck on scar

In your situation, the best option is abdominoplasty followed by laser treatments to remove any residual tattoo. The vast majority of your tattoo would be removed by an abdominoplasty.

It would be pointless to perform laser tattoo removal on tissue that would be resected during this procedure. This approach has the added advantages of significant cost savings by avoiding unnecessary laser treatments. (Richard J. Bruneteau, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)

Laser tattoo removal takes multiple treatments

Tattoo after tummy tuck operation

Yes, but keep in mind that laser tattoo removal does require multiple treatments, as much as ten or more, once per month or six weeks apart, so this could delay your Tummy Tuck. (Ronald Shelton, MD, Manhattan Dermatologist)

Your tattoo can be removed with a modified tummy tuck.

Tattoo can be removed with a tummy tuck. In order to do that, the tummy tuck scar would probably have to be placed HIGHER than normal. A higher scar does not look as good. But for you, this is the best compromise, because laser tattoo removal does not work that well. (George J. Beraka, MD (retired), Manhattan Plastic Surgeon)
