Am I a good candidate for a bbl? 24-years old.
BBL
Apr 20, 2017
Based on your photographs, you will be a candidate for a tummy tuck with liposuction of the abdomen, flanks and back with fat transfer to you buttocks (BBL).
If you are 24 and otherwise healthy, you will likely be a good candidate for this procedure with low risk from surgery and anesthesia.
Discuss your specific goals and concerns with your plastic surgeon.
Today makes my 37th day after my tummy tuck (no lipo performed). I still have my drain in.
Drain removal after tummy tuck
Apr 19, 2017
Most patients are able to have their drains removed after a tummy tuck prior to 37 days post op. I have seen patients that have had large amounts of weight loss require drains to stay in for a long period of time secondary to low levels of protein. I recommend scheduling an appointment discussing your concerns with your plastic surgeon.
I had a BBL a month ago and I just want to loose 15lbs I’m afraid I will loose my butt will I?
BBL and weight loss
Apr 18, 2017
Weight loss after fat transfer may change your results. Unfortunately you cannot selectively choose where this weight loss will occur.
Your best results in plastic surgery will occur when you are as close to your goal weight as possible.

Is it better to work out before or after surgery?
Work out before or after?
Mar 22, 2017
Your best results from plastic surgery will be achieved when you are at a stable weight that is close to you goal weight. Significant weight loss after surgery may lead to alteration of your results.
Working out to achieve good muscle tone will always be of benefit, although significant weight loss can lead to loose skin that is difficult to treat without surgical intervention.
