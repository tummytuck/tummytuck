Facelift than Cosmetic fillers
Jul 14, 2017
Dermal fillers alone will likely not yield the lift that you desire. A lower SMAS face and neck lift is the optimal way to lift and tighten loose skin, followed by laser resurfacing for texture, and lastly, fillers to address your volume loss. Facelift results typically last 10 years while most cosmetic dermal fillers last up to one year.

What causes ringing in the ears after having facelift surgery?
Ringing in the Ears Post Facelift
Jul 11, 2017
The report of ringing in the ears (tinnitus) post Facelift surgery is a rare occurrence. This ringing is from congestion of the inner ear caused by intubation of the oral airway from the anesthesiologist. The tinnitus is resolved by decongestant or saline flush. The nerves are not effected, the tinnitus is a transit side effect.
Best of Luck With Your Surgery!
