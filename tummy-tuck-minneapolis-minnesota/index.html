Tummy tuck Minneapolis, Minnesota

Tummy tuck in Minneapolis

Tummy tuck in Minneapolis is a surgical procedure designed to ensure that relieve the patient from violating the right proportions of the anterior abdominal wall and the body as a result of distension of the muscles and increasing the thickness of skinfold abdomen. aimed at the (so-called “lifting of muscles”) and the removal of excess fat and skin.

Tummy tuck in Minneapolis designed for toning the abdominal muscles, remove excess fat folds to make the droops belly flat. During the surgery, which is done under general anesthesia, the muscle layer is sutured, removed the excess skin of the anterior abdominal wall and excess subcutaneous fat.

At present, this surgery is very popular, both in America and in other countries.

Tummy tuck testimonials in Minneapolis:

Before tummy tuck in Minneapolis

  • Excess skin and subcutaneous tissue of the anterior abdominal wall, which can not be removed by other methods – diet, exercise, liposuction (correction of figure by removing excess fat with the help of vacuum), a combination of these methods.
  • Inability to perform liposuction due to the sharp decrease in Collagen-elastic properties of the skin, which threatens to give effect “baked apple” after liposuction on the skin of the abdomen.
  • The presence of pronounced stretch marks (striae pale) with an excess of abdominal skin.
  • The presence of skin and fat apron and ptosis (drooping) of the anterior abdominal wall.
  • The discrepancy between the rectus abdominis and general stretching of the abdominal muscles.
  • Rough postoperative scars in the lower abdomen.

Along with the tummy tuck in Minneapolis, you can get rid of the existing hernias of the anterior abdominal wall (umbilical, inguinal, postoperative). For those who want to emphasize the waist is possible to remove the lower rib cage.

Contraindications to abdominoplasty in Minneapolis are:

Tummy tuck Minneapolis

  • Uncompensated heart and lung failure.
  • Decompensated diabetes.
  • Obese.
  • The presence of postoperative scar above the navel.
  • Planned within the next year pregnancy.

Hospitalization in clinics of Minneapolis after tummy tuck takes about four days, due to the possible accumulation of tissue fluid or blood under the skin of the abdomen, requiring the use of drains.

In the first 2-3 weeks of possible weakness, slight fever, swelling of tissues, pain that gradually disappear and disappear completely in 2-3 months after surgery.

The incidence of postoperative complications directly related to the thickness of fat and weight of the patient, the presence of comorbidities.

For residents of other countries who decided for tummy tuck in Minneapolis, it is possible to carry out the rehabilitation period under constant medical supervision, in conditions of peace and comfort.
