Weird shape of nipples 2 months post op gynecomastia surgery
Weird shape of nipples
Jan 20, 2019
Looking at your photo, I have to wonder if your areolae were very protrusive and stretched out pre-operatively. In some patients, when this is the case pre-op, after the tissue is removed and the areolae shrink down, the skin doesn’t always tighten fully, and you see creases and a lax appearance to the skin. Most of the time, this improves over the first year post-operatively, but if it doesn’t, sometimes peri-areolar tightening can help to improve the appearance.
