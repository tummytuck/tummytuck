How soon can I apply silicone strips to my TT scar?
Scar care
Mar 30, 2018
There isn’t any real scientific data on when to start silicone strips. I generally recommend starting after the incision is completely closed; i.e. there are no scabs or open areas left. Usually this is at 1-3 weeks.
Is it normal for breast to look boxy after a breast lift only – 1 day post-op.
Boxy breasts
Mar 26, 2018
I agree that one day, or even one week after surgery is too early to assess the results. You surgeon likely over-corrected your breasts during the lift, since gravity always affects the breasts as they settle into a normal shape and appearance. Be patient and make sure to follow up with your surgeon.
53 years old, determining need for brachioplasty. Looking for advice on how long to wait and what to try
Arm lift?
Mar 26, 2018
I’m afraid your problem isn’t so much excess skin as a loss of elasticity. Procures to contour you arm tend to fix a 3D problem (the contour) with a 2D problem (the scar). In your case, the scars will probably be more noticeable than the relaxed skin. In other words, your problem isn’t bad enough to warrant surgery. Unfortunately, non invasive treatments are not likely to be of help either.
I have tear drop silicone implants since 2000, so 18 years old. How can I tell if one ruptured?
Implants
Mar 21, 2018
Your best bet is to see a plastic surgeon to asses what is going on. If you have developed significant scar tissue around the implant, or you are unhappy with your appearance, you may want to consider surgery to revise your result and possibly change out your implants. If you wish to avoid further surgery and are looking for peace of mind, you may want to consider an MRI, which is the only way to definitively check for implant rupture. Either way, you should probably follow up.

I have 800cc overfilled saline implants. I’m 5’8 and about 160lbs. Will downsizing implants be right for
Downsizing
Oct 2, 2017
Without photos it is hard to be sure, but downsizing from a DDD to a C will almost certainly require a significant breast lift using scars along the bottom of your breast, up the middle, and around the areola. The risks of downsizing are mainly related to the lift and include asymmetry, sensation changes, unfavorable scars, problems with healing. Because of this, make sure you find an experienced breast surgeon. Best wishes.
I’m at 4 weeks post op, tummy tuck, breast reduction. Lipo also. Extreme sensitivity in abdominal area,
Sensitivity
Oct 2, 2017
Sensitivity is very common after surgery. This usually results from nerve irritation, which can cause all sorts of unpleasant sensations, such as pins and needles, chafing, hot/cold, and the like. This will generally improve with time. Swelling is not uncommon, either, and may take several months to resolve. Best wishes.
