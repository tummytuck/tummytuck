When can I have a revision if my implants don't drop?
breast revision
Mar 21, 2015
After surgery, changes continue for up to 6 months. The time to consider a revision depends on may factors, including the degree of the problem, if improvement is ongoing, and physician routine. In general, revisional surgery should not be considered until 3 months have passed. If the problem is improving, one can wait longer.

hematoma?
Mar 21, 2015
It does not appear you have a hematoma, based on the picture. Hematomas present as one breast being significantly larger than the other and having excessive bruising and swelling. You should check with your surgeon, who can examine you and confirm this.

Did my implant bottom out?
Implants bottoming out
Mar 4, 2015
It is common to have some residual concerns following breast augmentation. There are still changes related to healing for up to 3 months after surgery. At that time, you should discuss any adjustments you feel would improve the appearance. It may be something simple like a small peri-areolar lift on one side. Most surgeons are happy to discuss this with their patients.
Help with SINGLE frown line, not a number 11. It is 1 inch long. Plastic surgeon said it was a split mu
single frown line
Mar 4, 2015
This line can be improved with a combination of Botox and fillers. The muscle can be weakened with surgery as well. The surgery is usually done in conjunction with an endoscopic brow lift. Several treatments of Botox is necessary to weaken the muscle enough to improve the wrinkle. It took many years to develop the line, so a single application of Botox is unlikely to fix everything.
Can I get a cosmetic surgery after having a post partum hemorraghe with my last two kids?
surgery following post-partum hemorrhage
Mar 4, 2015
There can be many reasons for post-partum hemorrhage. If it is due to pregnancy related factors, then it should not affect future surgeries. However, if it is due to a problem in you blood with clotting, then surgery should be avoided. You should check with your regular physician to see if you can have elective surgery.
Mastopexy and implants -2 separate ops?
Lift and/or implant
Mar 4, 2015
The best aesthetic results are from an augmentation and breast lift. I usually do both procedures at the same time, but some surgeons feel more comfortable staging the procedures. Alternatively, if you are unsure, you can have an augmentation done first, and then see the results to decide if you want a lift as well. Adding implants improves upper pole fullness but also increases overall size of the breasts.
Tummy Tuck and Lipo in four weeks
swelling after abdominoplasty/liposuction
Mar 4, 2015
It is normal to feel "swollen" or different after this procedure. Not only is the skin and fat affected, but the nerves under the skin also need time to recover. I allow my patients to undergo massages 4 weeks after surgery but each surgeon has a different regimen. You should return to discuss this with your surgeon if it bothers you.

Is it normal to feel a gap in the abdominal muscle post tummy tuck?
Midline gap in muscles after abdominoplasty
Mar 3, 2015
It is common to feel some separation in the abdominal muscles following abdominoplasty. The area of separation, the linea alba, differs in width among different people. Even with permanent sutures, you can still have a small gap. A large gap would be concerning but not a small gap.
