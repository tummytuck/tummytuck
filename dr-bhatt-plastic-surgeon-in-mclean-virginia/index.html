Can Liposuction and Tummy Tuck Be Done at the Same Time?
Liposuction at the same time as tummy tuck?
Jan 15, 2019
Liposuction can be combined with a tummy tuck. Usually liposuction of the waist and flanks can be done safely with a tummy tuck, and this helps to smooth out any love handles. However, liposuction centrally at the time of the tummy tuck could potentially result in healing issues. It is best to have an in-office consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to get personalized recommendations.

At What Age Do Your Breast Stop Developing?
Apr 29, 2018
Thank you for your question. Generally, breast development begins some time after the first menstrual cycle and ceases between ages 17-22 years. However, the breast size and shape never stop evolving during a woman’s life and are affected by such things as pregnancy, breast feeding, weightgain/loss, and menopause.
