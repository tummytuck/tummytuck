Tummy tuck Greensboro, North Carolina

Abdominoplasty Greensboro

Tummy tuck in Greensboro, NC is a method of cosmetic surgery, which allows to make the stomach more firm and flat. The method consists of surgical removal of the excess amount of fat and skin in the middle and lower abdomen for the purpose of flattening the muscles and fascia of the abdomen.

Usually a tummy tuck used by women with excess fatty tissue in the abdomen after pregnancy or after significant weight loss. Quite often in Greensboro tummy tuck is combined with liposuction.

Depending on the extent of surgery, exists a complete and a partial tummy tuck. Duration of full tummy tuck in Greensboro is about 2 – 5 hours. Partial (mini) tummy tuck may last between 1 and 2 hours.

Tummy tuck Greensboro

Tummy tuck is a relatively volumetric surgery, and rehabilitation period after it is relatively long. For many patients in Greensboro are required from one to three weeks to return to the previous physical activity. The recovery period depends on the amount of surgical operations, technology, and other factors. At pains appointed painkillers.

Quite often, there may be numbness in the abdominal wall, which can last up to six months and more. Bandages are usually removed on the fourth or fifth day. Sutures are removed on the seventh day. In order to prevent accumulation of fluid – the so-called seroma – in the postoperative period, in the wound is left special drainages. They are removed in terms from 2 days to 2 weeks. Usually, the patient may proceed directly to his work if it is not associated with physical activity (weight lifting, etc.). You can start physical exercise only after month, otherwise there may be complications such as postoperative hernia.

For patients suffering from obesity, we can recommend such modification of abdominoplasty in Greensboro as liposuction tummy tuck.

Belly button after tummy tuck in Greensboro

Liposuction tummy tuck – plastic surgery aimed at excision of hanging skin. Its name comes from the name of skin and fat “apron”, which is sometimes found in obese people or those who have recently lost a lot of weight.

Liposuction tummy tuck can be carried out by itself or in combination with abdominoplasty – depending on the patient’s condition. But liposuction tummy tuck very different from of abdominoplasty. Abdominoplasty removes excess skin and fat, strained muscles, and when the muscles are not stretched. Liposuction tummy tuck may be used together with any other surgery after weight loss. Unlike liposuction, when only remove excess fat, and liposuction tummy tuck involves removing of fat and skin.

Tummy tuck in Greensboro, NC

Before tummy tuck in Greensboro skin and fat apron is estimated by size. The higher the score, the more difficult surgery and the possibility of complications. The evaluation system of skin and fat apron:

  • Step 1: Skin and fat apron reaches the line of growth of pubic hair, but does not touch the genitals.
  • Step 2: Skin and fat apron reaches genitals
  • Step 3: Skin and fat apron reaches the upper part of the thigh
  • Step 4: Skin and fat apron reaches mid-thigh
  • Step 5: Skin and fat apron reaches the knees
