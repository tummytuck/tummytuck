Would I qualify for a tummy tuck - have to get a breast reduction.
Generally, no
Apr 23, 2017
You will feel great after having the breast reduction. As for the tummy tuck, insurance companies do not cover this and consider it cosmetic. However, they may cover what is called a panniculectomy - a procedure to remove any excess lower abdominal skin that may be causing rashes, etc. Although it is not a tummy tuck, it can also help to somewhat improve the appearance of your lower abdomen. Good luck with your surgery!
