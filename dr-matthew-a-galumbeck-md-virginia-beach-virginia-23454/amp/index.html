Should I get lipo now or wait until I have children?
Liposuction Before Pregnancy
Jan 22, 2018
Thanks for your question. Liposuction is a safe procedure to perform and is fine to do before having kids. Liposuction permanently removes fat cells and shapes your abdomen. If you maintain your weight, you will maintain your shape. Pregnancy can change your shape as it stretches out your skin and alters your tummy muscles. While I would suggest you wait to have a tummy tuck until after kids, liposuction now is fine to do.
