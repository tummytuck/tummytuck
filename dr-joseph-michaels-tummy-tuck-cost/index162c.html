Would I benefit more from an extended tummy tuck?
Extended versus regular tummy tuck
Feb 2, 2019
Thanks for your question. By just looking at your pictures, a full tummy tuck would suffice. An extended tummy tuck or 360 body lift only helps if you have excess skin around the hip area or the outer thighs/buttocks. I perform liposuction of the abdomen and flanks in nearly all my patients. Its old school thinking that liposuction and a tummy tuck can’t be done at the same time. You may have not found the right plastic surgeon for you.
Should I get an inner thigh lift or a body lift?
inner thigh loose skin
Sep 3, 2018
A lower body lift will indirectly help your inner thigh skin. This is because your abdomen and pubic area contribute to the loose skin of the anterior and inner thigh. A lower body though will not correct all the laxity in the inner thigh and you will need an inner thigh lift to correct this. A lower body lift effects the outer thighs more. It also lift the buttocks. If the outer thighs and buttocks are not a concern then a lower body lift is too much surgery and an extended tummy tuck is…
