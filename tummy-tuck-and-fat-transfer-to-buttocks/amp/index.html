The safest thing to do is one surgery at a time so recovery is easier. Your health is a factor, and the amount of fat you want transferred.

Why are there opposing views regarding having a simultaneous tummy tuck and brazilian butt lift?

Tummy tuck operation and fat transfer to buttocks

Performing a tummy tuck and brazilian butt lift at the same time is very surgeon dependent. Surgeons with extensive experience with the brazilian butt lift will, generally, perform the procedure simultaneously with a tummy tuck. I perform these 2 procedures together very often and do not feel that there is any compromise with the result.

Certain precautions will need to be taken, but you should be able to have a very good result provided that you are a good candidate for the 2 operations. You need to consult with a Board Certified PS with extensive experience performing the brazilian butt lift, as well as combining it with the tummy tuck (George Lefkovits, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

Tummy tuck surgery and fat transfer to buttocks

The answer to this questions depends on the individual, and how extensive the surgery will be. In most cases, tummy tucks and fat grafts should be done at different settings, but there are always exceptions. This could be a minor tummy tuck, with minimal muscle repair, etc. Also, the graft surgery may be minimal. There are many factors to consider. Consult with a Board Certifed Plastic Surgeon for an exam, and to discuss your goals in detail. (Robert E. Zaworski, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)

There are no absolutes in plastic surgery and what is good for one patient will not be appropriate for another.

If fat is harvested from the anterior abdomen for use in fat grafting of the buttocks then care must be taken in the post operative period not to place excessive pressure on the buttocks area. (Jeffrey Zwiren, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)

Each surgeon has his/her own preference

Each plastic surgeon has his/her own preference and philosophy about surgery, results, and safety. I think in most cases it is safe and effective to perform a tummy tuck and buttock fat grafting at the same time. (Remus Repta, MD, Scottsdale Plastic Surgeon)
