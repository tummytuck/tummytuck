Tummy tuck in Raleigh, North Carolina

Tummy tuck in Raleigh, North Carolina

Tummy Tuck in Raleigh is an operation designed to change the shape of the abdomen. This procedure is becoming increasingly popular among a wide range of patients – both men and women. Such interference can significantly reduce waist, to prevent sagging belly and make it more fit.

The main indications for a tummy tuck in Raleigh are those cases where the standard measures (diet, exercise, massage and other procedures) does not produce the desired result. These include stretching of the skin, loss of muscle tone, sagging body fat in the abdominal cavity, the presence of “apron”, hanging folds, postoperative scars and post-natal stretch marks.

Technique of tummy tuck surgery in Raleigh

Tummy tuck in Raleigh

In the area of ​​the navel, at the hairline or on other planned place, the skin incision is performed. It depends on the situation and the patient’s physiological data. In addition, individually worked out a tummy tuck, delivering a package of actions to remove fat deposits.

The incision can be made in both horizontal and vertical direction. Thereafter specialist removes excess fat and skin. Duration of treatment is usually 2-3 hours. It ends by  overlay of the cosmetic seam using atraumatic needle.

In the classical tummy tuck in Raleigh, the surgeon works with most of the patient’s body, at the end of the intervention requires the formation of the navel at the new location. Recently, however, a mini abdominoplasty become more popular, aimed at correcting certain areas of the anterior abdominal cavity. It differs from the traditional version of the surgery in Raleigh that the scars remained very small.

In order to eliminate stretch marks, sagging skin around the navel is required umbilical mini tumy tuck. Due to the circular incision and purse-string seams on the patient’s body is virtually no visible traces.

If you have problems in the lower and middle thirds of the anterior abdominal wall is used plastic abdomen with an incision in the groin area without moving the navel.
