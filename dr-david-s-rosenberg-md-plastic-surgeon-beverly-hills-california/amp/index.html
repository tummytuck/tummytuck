What is worst than can happen with fat transfer to breast? Only one side and only a little for b
fat transfer to breasts
Oct 16, 2017
The worst things that can happen with fat transfer to the breast is either an infection in the breast or a donor site contour issue (like loose skin).   I think fat transfer to the breast is great and I recommend you speak to a board-certified plastic surgeon that is comfortable with the procedure for your consultation.  Good luck! DSR
If fat from breast fat transfer re-absorbs won't it leave a dent or weird looking skin?
fat transfer to breasts
Oct 16, 2017
The fat gets reabsorbed in a smooth and even way.  It is unlikely that there would be lumps, dents or loose skin.  With breast implants there can be ripples, hardness and rupture so there is no perfect way to enhance breast volume but I think fat is a better long-term method than breast implants. Good luck! DSR
I have gyno in my right nipple that I’ve had for the past 3 years. Any treatments?
gynecomastia
Oct 16, 2017
You could first try a course of Tamoxifen and see if that helps improve the gynecomastia.  If that is unsuccessful in improving it then surgery would be good next step.  There is no sense in waiting since life is short and you shouldn't have to suffer with this any longer than necessary. Good luck! DSR
