Tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) in Tampa

Abdominoplasty in Tampa is plastic surgery of the anterior abdominal wall. Its task is to restore the proportions of the abdomen, as close to their natural. During the operation, close attention is paid to the aesthetics of the anterior abdominal wall – excision of rough scars and stretch marks.

The need for abdominoplasty usually occurs in parous women and in patients who have got rid of the problem of overweight and obesity. In both cases, the tummy tuck in Tampa helps to eliminate the changes arising from the sagging skin of the abdomen, often covered with stretch marks and sagging in the form of an apron. With abdominoplasty possible relieve patients from unsightly scars, removing them and putting cosmetic seam on the surgical wound.

Tummy tuck Tampa Florida

Pregnancy and obesity can lead not only to problems related to the overstretching of the skin of the abdomen, but also to the weakness of the muscles of the abdominal wall. Therefore, according to testimony, in Tampa dermepenthesis can be combined with tummy tuck surgery to strengthen the muscles, to remove white line hernia abdomen with liposuction. In some cases, plastic surgeons of Tampa moves navel during surgery. Abdominoplasty can be supplemented with breast lift.

Tummy tuck is contraindicated in severe systemic diseases (heart disease, diabetes, oncological processes, and many other the human conditions); in the presence of skin scarring from previous operations above the navel. Relative contraindications are: excessive thickness of the layer of fat on the abdomen and the possibility of a subsequent pregnancy or weight changes.
