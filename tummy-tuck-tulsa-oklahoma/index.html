Tummy tuck in Tulsa

Abdomen is one of the first parts of the body, which increases in size with weight gain. shape of the abdomen suffer during pregnancy, sudden weight loss. In the future, the problem of deposition of fat cells can affect the muscles, the skin contours. It is difficult to solve the problem of ugly forms with just exercise and diet. Even the familiar method of liposuction – not a panacea, because liposuction may exacerbate sagging of skin apron. That’s why plastic surgeons recommend method of tummy tuck in Tulsa in such cases.

Tummy tuck in Tulsa is an surgery to reduce and tightening of abdomen by removing excess skin and subcutaneous fat from the middle and lower abdomen and tightening the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall. This operation is a major surgical procedure that requires the utmost precision and care. For this reason, tummy tuck in Tulsa make only experienced and qualified plastic surgeons. Each tummy tuck surgery is the formation of individual forms of the abdomen. Tummy tuck procedure in hospitals Tulsa performed under general anesthesia from 2 to 4 hours, the total hospital stay – 2-3 days. After surgery sutures dissolve completely within 2-3 months, and preferably in the period wearing firming underwear.

In Tulsa successfully practice the following types of abdominoplasty:

After tummy tuck in Tulsa

  • Standard tummy tuck. The extra folds of skin and fat from the abdominal wall are removed during the procedure. Sutures are imposed at the level of the pubis. As a result of abdominoplasty, suture remains in the lower part of the abdominal wall at the hairy part of the pubis;
  • Mini-tummy tuck. In this case, is removed the excess skin below the navel, navel itself is not affected. Is simultaneously carried out liposuction to reduce fat deposits in the abdominal wall;
  • Extensive tummy tuck. This procedure involves the removal of excess skin and fat not only in the abdomen, but also fatty tissue on the hips and back. As a result, the seams remains on the sides of the lower back;
  • Endoscopic tummy tuck in Tulsa. The procedure used to give a better shape and tightening of abdominal muscles. Is carried out with a few cuts on the hairy part of the pubic area. The skin is usually not removed, liposuction can be performed simultaneously ;
  • Average tummy tuck – a kind of mini-abdominoplasty, which is carried out in patients with a large excess of skin and fat hanging over the pubic area. As a result are removed  excess fat and skin. Seam after tummy tuck extends horizontally from one abdominal cavity to second.
