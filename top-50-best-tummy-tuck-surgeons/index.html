Why does a sagging belly appear?

Experts call it “dermal apron”, but this is not easier. So what are the reasons for its occurrence? The answer lies in stretching the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall of the abdomen.

This is caused by hormonal changes during pregnancy or significant weight changes. In the future, the stretching of the muscles can lead to the formation of hernias and health hazards – internal cavities can penetrate into the clefts that have been freed, and then their entrapment occurs.

Usually, the presence of a hernia is difficult not to notice, since abdominal and lower back pains bring discomfort. Especially noticeable discomfort after physical exertion.

Stretching muscles can lead to the appearance of hernias and other health hazards. As a rule, people start their way of getting rid of an impending abdominal with diets and sports training and wonder why these methods do not bring any result.

It’s very simple: the problem of the skin apron is not in the fat deposits, as the patients believe, but in stretching the muscles and excess skin tissue. Get rid of them is only possible with the help of plastic surgery.

Abdominoplasty and types of surgery

Abdominoplasty, or abdominal plastic surgery is a plastic surgery, during which excess skin and fat are removed, and the abdominal framework is rebuilt by tightening the muscles. In the presence of hernias during surgery, get rid of them.

This method allows you to achieve an effective result in the form of a flat and beautiful abdomen. Note that abdominoplasty belongs to the category of complex plastic surgeries because of the large scale of the intervention.

There are traditional and mini-abdominoplasty. The first involves a volumetric correction of the abdomen, during which the abdomen is restored completely. That is, the surgeon eliminates excess skin and fat tissue, hernia, tightens muscles.

Tania Medina De Garcia

With mini-abdominoplasty, there is a small correction of this part of the body, more precisely, in its lower region. During this operation, it is only possible to remove excess skin and fat in a relatively small volume.

Carrying out abdominoplasty

The operation, regardless of the traditional version or mini, is performed under anesthesia. During the intervention, the doctor makes the necessary incisions, removes excess skin, fat deposits.

Then there is a tightening of the muscles and the removal of hernias, if they are present. After the termination of these manipulations the plastic surgeon applies accurate seams which after healing will be hidden under the underwear and will be invisible to associates.

Some patients prefer to additionally make a course of laser resurfacing to make the scar more invisible.

Recovery after surgery

Because of the large amount of surgical intervention, the patient is not discharged immediately after the operation, but a day or three is observed in the clinic’s hospital. Plastic surgeons note that this period is characterized by maximum pain, but discomfort is eliminated with painkillers.

After discharge from the clinic, the patient must constantly wear compression underwear for a month. It is necessary to adhere to certain limitations so that the healing process can proceed well and without complications. So, during recovery period you can not visit saunas and swimming pools, you can not sunbathe. Failure to comply with these requirements can lead to seam divergence.

Physical activity, bad habits, smoking and drinking alcohol are forbidden. After a month and a half, you can return to the usual lifestyle, after consulting with your plastic surgeon.

Possible complications after abdominoplasty

Usually, after the operation, hematomas and swelling occur, but these phenomena are typical for postoperative period after any surgical intervention. Possible complications include seromas (fluid accumulation) and bleeding.

If they are found, contact your doctor immediately. Nevertheless, such consequences are rare, if the plastic surgeon performed his work qualitatively.
