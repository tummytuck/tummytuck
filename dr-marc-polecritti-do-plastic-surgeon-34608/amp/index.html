I have wrinkly loose skin post exchange surgery (double mastectomy). Any suggestions?
Double mastectomy concerns
Sep 17, 2015
I have found that the final implant size is typically a bit larger than the final cc’s in the expander. The expanders are hard and therefore exert pressure on the skin envelope. The implants are soft and are more likely to be compressed by your tissue. Although your loose skin may contract some, I do not think all of the laxity will go away. If your unhappy, I’d consider larger implants combined with fat transfer to improve the contour.
