How Much Rest Do I Need After a Tummy Tuck?
How much rest after a tummy tuck?
Sep 11, 2012
You should be walking immediately, but not excessively. This is to decrease the risk of developing a blood clot. I advise patients not to exercise for at least six weeks- similar to a woman that has had a C-section. It is most appropriate to have your activity questions addressed by your surgeon. He/She will be grateful that you are trying to be compliant and taking your recovery seriously.
Do I Need a Lift/Advice Benefits of Going over the Muscle?
Lift vs no lift, above vs below muscle?
Sep 10, 2012
In general, below the muscle- dual plane with silicone gel is a better overall outcome. Above the muscle with saline is likely to relate more long term cosmetic issues such as visible wrinkling . As far as the lift aspect of your question, a thorough exam would be necessary to give you all your options.

Do I Require a Breast Lift or Can I Just Get Implants?
To lift or not to lift?
Aug 14, 2012
Given your photos, it would be difficult to completely determine that as your arms are lifted, which changes the exam and your appearance. Patients frequently have an assumption that a very large implant may " lift" the breast, and avoid the needed for a breast lift.

If there is significant excess skin, a very large implant will unfortunately accelerate the sagging process. You would be best suited getting opinions of a few different plastic surgeons.

Fat Removal or Weight Loss?
Buccal fat pad removal?
Aug 5, 2012
Considering that you are 21, and our faces can thin out and lose fat as we age; I would consider optimizing your weight first prior to proceeding with any surgical procedure that is not reversible. Buccal fat pad excision is performed more frequently in patients undergoing upper jaw surgery were surgical elevation of the jaws may cause excessive fullness in the cheeks. It is much less frequently performed in other situations.
I Want my Breast Inmplants Deflated. Can I Do That Instead of Removal? Costs?
Should you remove them?
Aug 4, 2012
I have had an experience that a patient wanted the implants removed within the first 2 weeks after surgery. I suggested that she wait to allow the settling process to occur; but she instead had another surgeon honor her request- and he removed them.

She returned to me for a request for a second augmentation within 6 months. It would be wise to allow your tissues and your feelings to settle for a while before you make your final decision.

Does surgery only close the gap or does it improve muscular function too?
Diastasis and back pain
Jul 30, 2012
Generally speaking, closing a Diastasis is more of an aesthetic issue than a functional issues as it is not truly a hernia. However, I have had a few patients inform me after an abdominoplasty ( the Diastasis was repaired) that their back pain was markedly improved.

I would not recommend to patients to have an abdominoplasty solely to improve chronic back pain; if it it incidentally helps, then that is an added benefit.

It is 7/2 and I Have Abdominoplasty Scheduled 8/16, I Am a Smoker and 24 Years Old. Risks?
Quit Smoking---now.
Jul 30, 2012
Regardless of the surgery planned, smoking increases the risk of complications significantly-this has been studied in plastic surgery as well as other surgical specialties. If you are concerned about accommodating your schedule, you will likely have a complication with healing that will take much longer to recover from than scheduling your surgery in the future.
Full Tt or Mini Tt?
Full vs. Mini?
Jul 22, 2012
Very few patients benefit from a mini abdominoplasty, since most women notice some muscle weakness or laxity above their belly button. If you are one of the fortunate ladies that has tight muscles above the belly button then a " mini" may be acceptable to you.

Be sure that you completely relax your abdomen during your examination.
