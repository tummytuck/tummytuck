I've shied away from getting a Facelift and was wondering if the Myolift would help to lift my face
Myolift vs facelift
Apr 28, 2020
Aging in the face is not due to muscle sagging. Muscles stay where they are. The overlying fatty tissue and skin is what sags. Just like doing situps won't change abdominal skin laxity stimulating face muscles also won't lift fatty tissue and skin. The platyma neck muscle is different than most and can become lax. However very little to no improvement would be expected without a face or neck lift.
