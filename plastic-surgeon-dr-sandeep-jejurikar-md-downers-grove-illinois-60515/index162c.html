Will Implants alone be sufficient or Lift as well?
Augmentation Alone May Be Great
Nov 3, 2015
Based on your photos,
it doesn't look like your breasts sag significantly enough to warrant a lift in
addition to augmentation. Your nipples point forward, and there isn't a
significant amount of breast tissue below the inframammary crease. An
augmentation alone can likely give you attractive, natural-looking results
without the scarring associated with a lift. Of course, the only way to know
for sure is with a consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon. Good
luck to you!
