You will likely be best served with an abdominoplasty (tummy-tuck) to remove the loose skin, tighten the loose muscle wall and give you a tight flat abdomen.  Non-surgical procedures will leave you with a non-surgical result, despite the marketing claims of many non-surgical interventions/devices.
How to take care of scars/marks a month after liposuction?
scar care after liposuction
Jan 17, 2018
The scar creams do little to improve the appearance of scars overall.  You are best keeping the scars protected from sun exposure until they are no longer pink or red.  Otherwise, they may turn darker or browner and remain so indefinitely.

When will lipo swelling disappear to show 50% of result, how do I maintain my weight till then?
Liposuction swelling
Jan 15, 2018
The swelling from liposuction usually resolves over 3-6 months.  Ballpark – 50 % after one month, 25 % after each of the second and third months and then diet and exercise may improve it more.  The loose skin will contract to the best of its ability on its own.  This depends on the elasticity and collagen in the skin.  If you are left with loose skin, you may need an excision procedure.  Weight maintenance is based on your strict adherence to a diet and exercise regimen.

Cost and down time from liposuction
Jan 11, 2018

In regard to a BBL, would I get a firmer/smoother look (less cellulite) if the buttocks were lipo’d fi
Liposuction of buttocks before BBL?
Jan 11, 2018
I usually would not recommend liposuction of the buttocks along with a BBL, unless there is a significant collection of localized adiposity (fat).  In general, it usually does not make a lot of sense to remove and then add back.  Liposuction of the flanks and perhaps outer thighs if needed will enhance the buttock shape, but liposuction of the buttock itself, may lead to flattening of the buttock and loss of shape.

From your photos, you appear to have both bulk and laxity.  Thus, a combination of liposuction to remove the bulk and brachioplasty (arm tuck) to remove the loose/lax skin will be needed.  These can be done in one or two stages.

Breast size confusion: I’m 5’9, 57kg getting anatomical, utm, mod plus tall implants.
Size confusion
Oct 10, 2017
The best implant style to use will be based on your breast and chest dimensions and the size you wish to go.  anatomic style implants have fewer options of what will fit an individual patient’s chest/breasts.  Understand that anatomic style implants are not a guarantee of “a natural appearance”.  I’ve seen anatomic style implants end up looking totally “fake” and round implants looking totally “natural”.  Anatomic implants do tip the scale towards a more natural look, but a guarantee.
