Do I need to stop Accutane before having breast augmentation surgery?
Stop Accutane before having breast augmentation surgery?
Feb 11, 2019
Yes. It is imperative that you discontinue Accutane before any planned surgery. The risks of complications can be substantial. You would want your plastic surgeon to work closely with your dermatologist or Accutane prescriber determine the length of time you need to be off the medication. The time frame may vary from a several months to a year before it would be safe to undergo an elective surgery.

How much bruising after FaceTite?

Dec 14, 2018
You can expect some bruising after FaceTite, although the amount seems to vary from person to person. Using natural products like bromelain and/or arnica before and after treatment will help to prevent and minimize bruising. Using compression will also help to decrease the amount of bruising.
