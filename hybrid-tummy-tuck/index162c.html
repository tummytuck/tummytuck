Hybrid tummy tuck

Tummy tuck hybrid

Referring to a plastic surgeon, a man mentally prepared not only to positive changes in his appearance, but also to a very difficult and long period of recovery. Especially when it question of such major surgery as tummy tuck. But what if the desired result can be achieved by much less traumatic way? Hybrid tummy tuck is abdominoplasty at the bottom part without moving the navel. Unlike the full tummy tuck, it has a shorter duration, easier rehabilitation period, and the scar in the bikini area after it is less noticeable.

Hybrid tummy tuck may be the choice of patients who do not have such an excessive amount of hanging skin when you want a classic abdominoplasty surgery. Also, hybrid tummy tuck may include liposuction, partial excision of excess skin and tightening of relaxed abdominal muscles. Hybrid abdominoplasty is surgery, which combines a small incision and a full abdominoplasty, tightening of fascia.

What is a hybrid tummy tuck?

Hybrid tummy tuck photos

Hybrid tummy tuck is performed as usual abdominoplasty under general anesthesia. The duration of the surgery is less than in traditional surgery due to the smaller volume of intervention.

The main advantage of the hybrid tummy tuck surgery is less traumatic and better cosmetic effect of scarring, as to carry it out is usually used much smaller skin incision.

Equally obvious advantages compensated by fairly disabilities: as evidenced by the practitioners, the vast majority of patients seeking a tummy tuck, have too much skin and excess fat to  to apply this correction option.

Hybrid tummy tuck surgery

Stages of hybrid tummy tuck are: skin incision, excision of excess skin, tightening of fascia, if necessary – liposuction. After that, the incision is closed. Postoperative period with a hybrid tummy tuck is shorter and milder. The patient can quickly return to the previous work.

For what need hybrid tummy tuck ? In fact there are endoscopic abdominoplasty, which is still less traumatic. The matter is that the endoscopic abdominoplasty has no effect on hanging skin in the lower abdomen. If changes in the wall of the abdomen are not as pronounced, that there is a need to carry out a full abdominoplasty, but require excision of excess skin, then can be chosen less capacious surgery – a hybrid tummy tuck.

In addition, hybrid tummy tuck does not have much influence on the abdominal muscles, as usual abdominoplasty, as here is only affected fascia (the membrane covering the muscle). But this surgery is ideal in the case when there is no need to conduct extensive abdominoplasty.

This surgery is most effective in dealing with the results of pregnancy, when there is some muscle weakness and drooping of the stretched skin of the abdomen.

Among the complications of hybrid tummy tuck can be called all those that can occur with conventional abdominoplasty.

Hybrid tummy tuck allows to adjust one of the most problematic areas in the human body – the lower abdominal region, where appears tummy and even whole skinfold.  If it is impossible to eliminate this lack by using a tummy tuck exercise and diet, as well as in the presence of weakened muscles and skin of the abdomen, stretch marks, the omission of the abdominal wall and if you want to emphasize the waist shows the execution of this option, the reconstruction of the anterior abdominal wall. Surgery should not be performed on the eve of a planned pregnancy – it is better to postpone it for a while and turn to a plastic surgeon after birth.

Hybrid tummy tuck is, in fact, a cross between a liposuction (removal of excess fat) and standard abdominoplasty. It is a surgery in which there is no need to turn to the classic version in the absence of excessive amounts of hanging skin, and endoscopic technique is not suitable due to the fact that clean up the excess skin is still needed.

During hybrid tummy tuck doctor makes a partial liposuction, excised the excess skin and tightening of relaxed muscles of the anterior abdominal wall, but in this case affected only the fascia, which makes manipulation less traumatic.

Hybrid tummy tuck is performed under general anesthesia, the duration of it is shorter than the traditional reconstruction of the abdomen due to the lower volume of the intervention itself (no more than 2-2.5 hours).

The main advantage of this approach is considered a little traumatic and better cosmetic effect, as performed only one short cut. With a hybrid abdominoplasty a navel is not affected, all excised excess skin located between the navel and the symphysis pubis.

The only seam is at the hairy part of the pubic area, without going beyond the bikini zone and easily hiding in the future even quite frank underwear.

One of the main problems that are solved effectively by using hybrid tummy tuck, is a diastasis – divergence of the abdominal muscles, so that the belly droops and bulges. Most often this defect facing women after pregnancy and childbirth. Also, surgery may be indicated in the following cases:

  • excess fat in the abdominal wall;
  • laxity of the skin and its sagging;
  • striae (stretch marks) in the lower abdomen.

How is surgery performed?

Hybrid tummy tuck surgery scar

Before the surgery is carried out a full examination of the patient: is defined overall health, the degree of pathological changes in the abdominal cavity, the cause and possible accompanying illnesses.

For 2 weeks before surgery should stop taking drugs that affect blood clotting, do not smoke, do not drink alcohol. Also, the surgeon may prescribe a special diet to help normalize metabolism and prevent the formation of subcutaneous fat.

Hybrid tummy tuck is performed under general anesthesia. Its duration is about 3-4 hours. The length of the incision (and future joint) may vary depending on the individual patient, usually no more than 10-15 cm in length.

Quite often, hybrid tummy tuck combined with other aesthetic surgeries: liposuction of thighs, pubic area, the upper zone of the abdomen, knees, back, sides, as well as tightening or breast augmentation.

Recovery after hybrid tummy tuck

Hybrid tummy tuck scar

Recovery after such variant of abdominoplasty takes less time and proceeds a lot easier. The patient can fairly quickly return to their previous work and habitual way of life, while respecting the recommendations received from a physician concerning the limitation of physical activity and, in particular, actions carried out at the expense of the abdominals, including gets up from bed and the sex. Within a month or two should wear a supportive corset.

By the efficiency, hybrid tummy tuck is not inferior to other reconstructive techniques. The main thing that it was a reasonable choice.
