Cost for a tummy tuck

The costs depending on the amount of excess skin to be removed, time for surgery, hospital/anesthesia costs, and costs for any overnight stays if needed. The total cost for a tummy tuck (all-inclusive) by a board certified plastic surgeon (American Board of Plastic Surgery) in the Houston area is $6000-$9,000.

Tummy tuck pricing cost image

It is very common for plastic surgeons to perform adjunctive liposuction at the time of your tummy tuck procedure. This may add cost to your procedure. Overall, the cost of your procedure, although important, should not be the primary driver in selecting your surgeon. Look for proper training experience, a connection/comfort-level with your surgeon an his staff, and desirable results evidenced through a plethora of before/after photos. (C. Bob Basu, MD, FACS, Houston Plastic Surgeon)
