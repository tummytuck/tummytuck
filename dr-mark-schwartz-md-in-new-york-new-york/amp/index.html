Can steristrips be left on for too long following breast lift and augmentation?
Steri-Strips After Surgery
Mar 28, 2015
As long as the steri-strips are relatively clean and there have been no wound healing issues, keeping them in place for an extra 2 weeks is unlikely to cause a problem. For body contouring procedures I recommend that patients keep steri-strips in place for approximately 3 weeks. They are changed if they start peeling off or if they become soiled. Stretch Marks After Tummy Tuck
Feb 28, 2015
Congratulations on losing 70 pounds in one year! During a tummy tuck the skin and stretch marks are removed from the lower abdomen. If there are upper abdominal stretch marks they will be moved down to the lower abdomen. While they generally do not disappear, they may look less noticeable since the skin is on stretch. Good luck.

Feb 21, 2015
I would avoid a tanning bed for at least 1-2 months after breast surgery since the breast skin may have diminished sensation initially. Is it safe to have a baby after having a Tummy Tuck?
Pregnancy After A Tummy Tuck
Aug 27, 2014
Yes, it is still safe to become pregnant after a tummy tuck. However, the pregnancy is likely to affect the aesthetic result of the tummy tuck since the skin and muscles will be stretched for a prolonged period of time. Most patients are discouraged from having a tummy tuck if they are considering pregnancy in the near future.
How soon can I stand straight up post tummy tuck?
When To Stand Up Straight After A Tummy Tuck
Aug 27, 2014
Most patients are standing up straight by 1-3 weeks depending on how much laxity remains after skin removal and muscle tightening. for the first week, patients are allowed to remain in a position that feels comfortable for them. By 2 weeks, if a patient is still not making progress with their posture, they are encouraged to gradually stand a little straighter everyday. Good luck!
