Liposuction thighs – butt is down. How can I fix it?
Liposuction thighs – butt is down. How can I fix it?
May 7, 2018
Thank you for your question. Unfortunately, contour abnormalities are the most common complication after liposuction. If these areas are present after several months, it is not likely that they will fill out over time. Contour irregularities can be very difficult to fix. In some cases, fat can be grafted back into the areas, but you should discuss this with your surgeon to see if it is an option. Best of luck.
