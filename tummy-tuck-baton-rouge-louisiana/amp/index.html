Indications for a tummy tuck in Baton Rouge:

  • Too much accumulation of fat in the abdomen,
  • The weakness of the muscles of the anterior abdominal wall,
  • Hanging skin on the abdomen,
  • Scars on the skin of the abdomen,
  • Hernia, formed in the anterior abdominal wall.

Contraindications for tummy tuck in Baton Rouge:

  • Any acute illness;
  • Diseases of the heart and respiratory system in an acute phase;
  • Dependent diabetes mellitus (uncompensated);
  • Allergy;
  • Forthcoming in the next six months or current pregnancy;
  • Skin disorders: dermatitis, fungal infections, psoriasis;
  • Taking of certain medications.
