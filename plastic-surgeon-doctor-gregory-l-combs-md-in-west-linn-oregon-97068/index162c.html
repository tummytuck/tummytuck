Why do dog ears happen?
Tummy Tuck dogears
Sep 22, 2014
When the abdominal incision in a tummy tuck is kept short, it often leaves excess skin and fat at the ends of the incision, referred to as "dog ears". This can easily be revised in an office procedure with local anesthetic. Extending the incision at the time of the tummy tuck usually prevents this from occuring.
What determines if i will need a lift with my augmentation?
Breast Lift with Augmentation
Sep 22, 2014
Thank you for your question and inquiry into needing a breast lift with your augmentation. This would require an examination to determine is your nipple falls below your natural inframammary fold. If this is your anatomy, you would most likely require a breast lift. The augmentation with an implant will give you the upper pole fullness, giving you a fuller breast volume than your natural breast.
