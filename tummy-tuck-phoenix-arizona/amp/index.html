Tummy tuck Phoenix, Arizona

Phoenix tummy tuck

Tummy tuck Phoenix AZ is getting rid of excess subcutaneous fat and skin tissue of the anterior abdominal wall. Sometimes tummy tuck confused with liposuction, but they are two different surgeries, liposuction – a suction of excess fat from different parts of the body.

Tummy tuck in Phoenix AZ is a difficult surgery and it is resorted to only in the event if by any other ways (diet, exercise, liposuction) shape of abdomen can not be corrected.

With this surgery does not cure obesity, so there is no abrupt reduction in weight as a result. Conjunction with the tummy tuck can remove inguinal, umbilical and postoperative hernia. It should be noted that such multiple operations in Phoenix performed with great success.

Carrying out of tummy tuck in Phoenix shown in the following cases:

Tummy tuck in Phoenix

  • In the lower part of the abdomen is visible scars
  • There is a very sharp decline in the elasticity of the skin and if do liposuction, the belly will get the effect of “baked apple”
  • There is a discrepancy between direct and general stretching of the abdominal muscles
  • There is excess subcutaneous fat and skin tissue, which is difficult to remove by other methods
  • There is the omission of the abdomen, stretch marks.

Contraindications for a tummy tuck in Phoenix:

Tummy tuck Phoenix AZ

  • Pronounced obesity
  • Diabetes
  • Pulmonary and cardiac failure
  • Previously conducted surgery, which left a scar above your belly button.

Tummy tuck in Phoenix is of two kinds: the “big” and “small.”

With the “big” tummy tuck to remove excess skin and fatty tissue and suture the abdominal muscles, there is a separation of the skin of the abdominal wall of the abdomen. Also performed incision in the form of a “bow” along the perineal line, and navel is moved to a new umbilical hole.

Tummy tuck in Phoenix AZ

With “small” tummy tuck, fat tissue is removed and sutured the abdominal muscles. Excess skin excised in a small amount or not at all excised. Navel also remains in the same place, incision is also performed along the line  perineal, but its size is smaller than the “big” tummy tuck.

Tummy tuck surgery in Phoenix lasts 2-3 hours and is performed under general anesthesia. Patient is discharged through the day, but for two weeks every 2-3 days he should visit specialists for a bandage and inspection.

Within a month after the tummy tuck, the patient must wear a special compression garment.

Swelling in the operated area persist for months, scars do not become completely invisible, but surgeons are doing everything possible to subsequently scar could be hidden.
