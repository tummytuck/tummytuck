Is a Certification From ABCS the same as ABPS For a Mommy Makeover?
Board certification for plastic surgeons
Mar 6, 2012
Great question...unfortunately, someone boarded by the ABCS is generally an ear nose and throat surgeon not a plastic and reconstructive surgeon. Training for plastics is very specific and only surgeons with specific training in plastic and reconstructive surgery are boarded by the American Board of Plastic Surgeons.

I would strongly recommend you choose a surgeon with ABPS credentials for your Mommy Makeover surgery

What Order Are the Procedures Performed in Operating Room for Mommy Makeover?
Order of procedures for Mommy Makeover
Mar 6, 2012
Thanks for your question, generally order of procedures is dictated by sterility. In my room, what we do is prone first, which would be your liposuction. Then, while you are still asleep, turn you to the supine position, breasts followed by abdomen. Which allows a totally new set up for the breasts, and keeps you from having to lay on your tummy after a panniculectomy.

Best of luck with your surgery!!!

Is 22 Too Young for a Mommy Makeover?
Too young for Mommy Makeover

Switching Breast Implants from Subglandular to Submuscular, Is this Risky?
Going from subglandular to submuscular placement
Feb 22, 2012
Thank you for your question. If you are having rippling, going below the muscle will help, but in patients as thin as you are, you should still expect to feel your implant laterally. Going to a submuscular pocket with an intact implant is not a problem surgically. I recommend and do it often. Submuscular placement has so many benefits: easier to perform mammograms, lessens capsular contracture rate, not to mention look and feel.

Best of luck to you!
