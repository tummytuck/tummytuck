Tummy tuck in Portland, Oregon

Tummy tuck Portland

Tummy tuck in Portland – is a surgical procedure designed to correct sagging of the abdominal wall and removing excess skin and fat layer. Surgery is also designed to eliminate the scars of various origin and stretch marks in the abdominal area. Often, after several pregnancies or as a result of rapid weight loss abdominal skin loses its elasticity and sag, forming numerous folds.

Abdominoplasty in Portland used for the correction as a last resort, ie, when the weight loss due to diet or sports did not lead to the desired result.

The aim of surgery in Portland – removal of excess skin in the abdominal area and strengthening the abdominal wall by pulling and stapling the stomach muscles.

Surgery is recommended for women after childbirth who do not plan further new pregnancy. Also in Portland tummy tuck is performed in men and women who have stretched skin and folds as a result of a strong weight loss. It should be noted that tummy tuck is not an alternative to dieting and sports. In some cases, during abdominoplasty plastic surgeon can perform the removal of excess body fat. However, such a procedure should be carried out with great caution because of the high risk of complications associated with the simultaneous performance of these two procedures.

Before tummy tuck in Portland

Before abdominoplasty Portland

By the time of surgery performing, the patient is recommended to reduce weight to a minimum, so as to further reduction the weight after surgery may lead to the re-sagging abdomen.

Abdominoplasty in Portland can be done at almost any age. Nevertheless, the presence of chronic disease may be a serious risk factor. During the first conversation with the doctor, where the doctor will tell you about the process and the possible complications of surgery, tell him all the information about your existing chronic diseases, about surgical operations that have been carried out previously, about your medications and about allergic reactions. During the consultation, the doctor will take pictures of part of the body on which will make the surgery, in order to plan the course of surgery.

Tummy tuck procedure in Portland

Tummy tuck Portland, Oregon

Before starting the operation, the surgeon will mark the places of incisions on the skin . Anesthetist will set you an intravenous catheter through which will be entered drugs for anesthesia. As a rule, a tummy tuck in Portland performed under general anesthesia. However, the final decision about the type of anesthesia (local or general) will be accepted anesthesiologist during your pre-meeting with him.

During operation, the surgeon performs transverse incision of the abdomen and the incision around the navel. Skin flap from the surface of the abdomen rises up, the abdominal muscles are sewn together to strengthen the abdominal wall. In what follows the surgeon removes the excess skin and re has navel in the middle of the abdomen.

After performing the procedure in Portland, the surgeon sews the fabric with bioresorbable sutures and leaves two drainage in the groin (tube coming out of the abdominal cavity to the outside) that are used to drain fluid from the operated area. After surgery superimposed compression bandage on stomach. When your consciousness will be completely restored, you can gradually start to eat and drink. Nurses will help you get out of bed.
