Tummy tuck surgery pictures

With tummy tuck is possible to reduce belly size by tightening the abdominal muscles and getting rid of excess skin in its lower part. Most often, surgery is performed to correct the figure after childbirth or fast weight loss.

In this gallery are a tummy tuck pictures of operations, which were carried out by known surgeons. Judging by the tummy tuck pictures, you will appreciate the opportunities the operation and its consequences (in particular, the appearance of postoperative scar). For each series of images specified general notes about the nature of surgical intervention. Also on tummy tuck pictures you’ll see all the stages of surgery: tummy tuck pictures of the preoperative period, tummy tuck pictures during surgery, photos in the first days after surgery, tummy tuck pictures after several weeks, months and years.

By looking these tummy tuck pictures, you exactly decide whether you need surgery or not. In these photographs we tried to fully convey the atmosphere of the operation, surgeons care for patients, feelings of patients. Also after surgery, complications may occur. These complications you can also see on our tummy tuck pictures. People depicted in these photographs are absolutely real.
