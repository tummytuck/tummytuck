Breast augmentation Day 7 Post Op, 450 cc under muscle, is this normal? Have they dropped?
postop changes
Jul 22, 2017
You have to allow for postop changes to occur. The photo is at an angle but it looks like the implants are still riding high and need to drop, not that they have dropped too much. Smooth implants drop over the first few months. You also cannot determine the final scar resting place at this point but hopefully it will be in the fold. Of course, your surgeon is needed to provide a hands on evaluation and discussion.
6 weeks post-op breast aug and my nipples are super sore and sensitive, is this normal?
sore nipples after breast implants
Jul 22, 2017
There are two common reasons you would have sore nipples. One is that placing an implant stretches the intercostal nerves which provide sensation to the breast and nipple and these nerves have altered activity, either decreased or increased until they heal. And two, the implant has made the breasts more firm and added projection, so likely the nipples are suffering from more friction than they used to.
