Am I at risk for capsular contracture? If so, what should I do now?
Capsular Contracture Risk
Sep 4, 2018
Capsular contracture is scar tissue response of the breast around the implant. Anyone who has breast augmentation is at risk. We don't fully understand the causes but circulating bacteria or bacteria introduced at the time of surgery are possible culprits. Infection of the piercings around the time of surgery theoretically could affect this. What you describe would be a very low possible cause. Best wishes for soft , beautiful breasts!
How much recovery time do I need if I were to go under the muscle transaxillary incision for breast augm
Submuscular Breast Augmentation Recovery
Sep 4, 2018
The lower inner origin of the muscle is released during surgery in effect creating a controlled muscle strain or tear. You may resume low resistance motion in 3-5 days, limited lifting until 3 weeks then ease into heavier resistance . If it hurts back off. You need to protect the muscle by gradual increases in resistance. Best wishes for a beautiful outcome!
