When I perform an abdominal paniculectomy (tummy tuck) I will do suction of the abdominal area and usually the hip area and remove all the skin and remaining fat between the lower incision at the level of the pubis and the incision about the umbilicus (belly button).

This is only if the skin is loose enough to remove all this and still close the wound. Your plastic surgeon should be able to determine this pre operatively.
