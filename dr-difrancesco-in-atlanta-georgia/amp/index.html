Is Airsculpt safe and are results good?
Airsculpt
Mar 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. Yes airsculpt is safe and yields good results. Airsculpt is a gentler form of liposuction that removes just as much fat as traditional liposuction.

Liposuction
Mar 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. You would be a good candidate for liposuction. If you still had loose skin you could have a mini tummy tuck or use thermi to tighten the skin. It is best to go in for a consultation to understand your options.

Reshaping the Breasts
Mar 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. I am sorry to hear your breast augmentation did not work out. Your breast tissue and skin has been stretched and therefore you may need to reshape or tighten the skin when you remove your implants. This will help the shape of your breasts so they don't sag as much after implant removal. You should discuss your concerns with your surgeon.

If your surgeon is not available you should follow up with a board certified plastic surgeon in your area.

Post op Breast Implant Revision
Dec 27, 2018
Thank you for your question. One month after surgery your breasts are still healing and what you see is not your final result. In revisional surgery the surgeon will often over correct for the problem because we know that scar tissue will often redevelop in the same area and will be worse than the first surgery. Allow yourself time to heal.

Hernia After Liposuction
Dec 27, 2018
Thank you for your question. A hard knot after liposuction is usually scar tissue. If you are concerned about a hernia, you will need to have an exam to determine if a hernia is present. I would consider doing a tummy tuck next to tighten your skin instead of liposuction. Good luck.
Festoon/lower lid droop 4 weeks post lower bleph. Would lifting the right cheek or tear trough
Post op Festoon
Dec 13, 2018
Thank you for your question. In several of your photos, the right lower eyelid is starting to develop malposition. This is most likely the "festoons" or prolonged swelling. There are a number of treatments including massage and sometimes diuretics to address the issue. You need to follow up with your surgeon and get treated as soon as possible before the malposition becomes permanent. Good luck.
