Am I a candidate for eyelid surgery?
Rejuvenation of your eyes
May 31, 2017
You do have some excess skin of your upper lid and some amount of ptosis (drooping of your upper eyelid). Your eyebrow can also contribute to the excess skin of your upper eyelid - think of the brow as a curtain-rod to your eyelid skin. Your lateral brow looks a little low and complete rejuvenation may require some lift here as well. See a board-certified plastic surgeon for an evaluation. You may be a candidate even though you are only 33.
