Tummy tuck Manhattan

Tummy tuck in Manhattan before and after

Tummy tuck in Manhattan – is the removal of excess skin and fatty tissue in the abdomen, back and hips. During this surgery seams superimposed on the lower abdomen, sides, where going to the back. This is quite a complex technique that requires a high skill of the surgeon and the special feeling of symmetry. Such operations are not uncommon. To large number of patients suffering from overweight and disproportionate distribution of fat, need such surgery. In such situations is possible to remove the excess fat from the thighs (liposuction), both in the framework of abdominoplasty, as well as individual surgery.

Tummy tuck Manhattan – is a long and rather laborious action. It can last up to five hours. It is carried out under general anesthesia.

Tummy tuck in Manhattan

Before tummy tuck in Manhattan

Before the start of surgery the surgeon in Manhattan marks skin and only then does the first cut, which goes around the navel. After that, doctor cuts the skin and fatty tissue from the muscles. At the same time the excess fat is sucked gently using a special tube during the pulling of the skin.

The next step is the removal of excess skin on the preliminary marks and transfer the navel to the new location through made incision. If during standard reconstruction of the abdomen, this manipulation is not always performed, in expanded version of tummy tuck in Manhattan this step is required. Such extensive intervention is applied when the skinfold (apron) has impressive size, and after its excision is necessary to completely compensate for the tissue of the anterior abdominal wall. This will require the implementation of this manipulation, which is essential to ensure further the harmonious and natural appearance of the abdomen.

Fabrics of navel during and after tummy tuck in Manhattan are completely viable, because preserved feeding pedicle with blood vessels and nerve fibers. Next being reconstructed back and hips. At the end cosmetic seams are superimposed on the surgical wound for a period of 3-4 days and drains are installed.

Tummy tuck recovery

Abdominoplasty in Manhattan

After abdominoplasty in Manhattan patients require long rehabilitation period. Ligation performed two or three times a week until removing stitches on 14-15 days after surgery. Within six weeks, patients are advised to wear special compression underwear and not to resort to physical exercise and sports training. To prevent discrepancies of seams and wound infections, you should not be taking a bath a couple of weeks.

Abdomen, hips and lower back finally acquire the expected view after 3-4 months, when fully operational scars heal.
