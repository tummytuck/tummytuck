Tummy tuck Salt Lake City, Utah

Tummy tuck Salt Lake

Tummy tuck in Salt Lake City, is a cosmetic surgery to remove excess skin left after sharp weight loss in obese people.

Current task today to get rid of excess weight sometimes leads to a accompanying problems: the formation of excess skin, first of all – in the abdomen. Very often with this encounter patients who have had bariatric surgery and lost a large amount of weight at once. Leather, which by virtue of its flexibility has been stretched over the entire area of a huge belly, can not be canceled, and remains hanging in the surplus. It looks unsightly, and sometimes significantly hinders lead a normal life. Besides the problems with the appearance, the skin folds are the ideal place for the development of various fungal infections, ulceration, etc.

Solution to the problem of excess skin is surgery. Tummy tuck in Salt Lake City is held on an individual plan, based on objective indicators, and personal preferences of each patient. It is assigned only after weight loss and when body weight is stabilized.

How does tummy tuck work

Tummy tuck Salt Lake City

Tummy tuck Salt Lake City

Tummy tuck in Salt Lake City is performed under general anesthesia. The surgeon performs an incision in the lower abdomen, at the location of the skin fold. Second cut, circular, performed around the navel. The doctor removes excess skin and fat, separating the skin from the subcutaneous tissue of the muscles and strengthens the weakened abdominal muscles using sutures. The remaining skin is tightened and fixed in the lower abdomen, in the natural skin folds. Navel back into place. To discharge liquid in the postoperative period, the drains are entered into the abdominal cavity.

After tummy tuck in Salt Lake

Tummy tuck in Salt Lake City leaves two scars in the lower abdomen and around the umbilicus. At the same time, the result is a much more aesthetically pleasing as compared to the bulk overhang skinfold. Postoperative complications are rare and usually easily removed. Discharge from hospital can be on the next day after surgery. Several days later, the patient comes to the surgeon for a follow-up visit and retrieve drainages.

Implementation of tummy tuck in Salt Lake City allows you to quickly achieve an aesthetically good result, which is a significant aspect of the overall program of struggle against excess weight. The consultant will organize a reception by leading experts in the field of cosmetic surgery at a suitable time and with convenient payment terms.
