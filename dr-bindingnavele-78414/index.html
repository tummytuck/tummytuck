Lower back and upper buttocks lipo and skin excision?
lower back and upper buttock rejuvenation
Mar 8, 2018
Liposuction addresses excess tissue or bulges in and area while skin excision addresses loose skin. So if you have excess skin and would like to have your buttocks lifted, then a posterior body lift with an incision above the buttocks is the answer. If it is a bulge with love handles that is the problem, lipo will give you the results you are looking for. The lipo scars are in the sacral dimples and small, 1.5 cm.

Is it a health risk to fly back home 3 days after my surgery?
flying after a mommy makeover
Feb 23, 2018
In most cases it is not advisable to fly 3 days after surgery. This is because you will most likely have drains in place and you will have difficulty having appropriate follow up after the surgery. Most plastic surgeons prefer to take care of their patients and it will be difficult to do that if you are far away from your surgeon. An additional issue is problems with moving fast if required from the plane, or in the airport while traveling.

Hi! Is it safe to have the BBL and Breast augmentation (Breast lift or reduction no im
breast implants with bbl
Feb 23, 2018
While some do perform both surgeries at the same setting, most will stage them. This is because right after a BBL you want to avoid pressure on the buttocks and the fat transferred and at the same time avoid pressure on the breasts after a breast implant placement. It is best to very carefully consider this before proceeding with both procedure at the same time.

Feb 23, 2018
If you notice any significant changes after surgery such as increased swelling on one side, change in appearance on one side, or anything else unusual you should call your plastic surgeon immediately, because it may be related to bleeding or implant deflation. If it is not any of the above then the asymmetry may be due to something that was there before or that one implant is dropping faster than the other one which is often the case.
