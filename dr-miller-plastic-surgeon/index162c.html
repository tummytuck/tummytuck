Vertical Incision – Fluid Pocket – Packing wounds daily
Wound dehisence after tummy tuck
Oct 31, 2015
Unfortunately this a problem that can occur in that area likely due to a combination of tension and diminished blood supply. Local wound care managed by your surgeon is your best course of action at this point. This wound will most likely heal but may leave an unsightly scar which may need revision. I personally would not recommend a VAC at this point but that may be an option if the situation worsens.
Recent Breast Augmentation complete 3WKs..What Will Be My End Results?
Asymmetry
Mar 26, 2015
That is fairly significant asymmetry. It is early and your capsule is in its early formation stages so you do not have capsular contracure. There could be multiple issues at play here, ie, unnoticed preoperative asymmetry, asymmetric pocket dissection, etc. Definitely ask your surgeon for advice on maneuvers to help improve on the situation, massage, bandeau, etc. You do need to wait a few months as things will change during the first 3 months.
