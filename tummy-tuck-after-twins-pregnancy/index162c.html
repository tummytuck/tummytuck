Tummy Tuck After Twins Pregnancy?

Tummy tuck after twins pregnancy pictures

Although your frustration regarding the stretch marks is understandable, be careful that you do not waste a lot of time and/or resources on modalities such as laser surgery, thermage, cool sculpting (none of these modalities will be helpful for patients in your situation).

Also, in addition to the stretch marks, you are likely dealing with significant “spreading” of the abdominal wall muscles that can be especially troublesome after twin pregnancy. This can be a nuisance especially when you are flexed/bent at the waist.

Although I would not venture to give you advice based on the limited information provided, generally speaking, the “ideal” patient for tummy tuck surgery is one who has completed pregnancies, is psycho socially/emotionally/financially stable, has an excellent social support system surrounding him/her, is capable of arranging enough recovery time and who has reached a long-term stable weight.
