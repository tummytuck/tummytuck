Diet vs tummy Tuck?
Diet vs Tummy Tuck
Dec 16, 2013
Congratulations on your weight loss so far. Losing as much weight as possible will be better for you overall health and appearance of your tummy. Unfortunately, a tummy tuck will be needed to remove all of the excess skin after the weight loss. Even though your stomach will get smaller with the weight loss the excess skin can make it look worse.To have the flat appearance that all women want a tummy tuck will be necessary. Good luck continuing with your weight loss.

Scar Cream Recommended?
Scar Cream that works!!
Sep 12, 2013
Using a scar cream after any surgical procedure should be highly recommended. There are many over the counter products that work well. The product that I recommend to my patients cannot be found in local pharmacy or grocery store.
