Tummy tuck in New Orleans, Louisiana

Tummy tuck in New Orleans

Tummy tuck or correction of the form of the abdomen in New Orleans allows tighten saggy stomach, which is formed from the excess skin. This operation is not a method of weight loss, because does not involve the removal of adipose tissue.

A tummy tuck is recommended at:

  • excess skin or fat in the abdomen, when other methods (exercise, diet, liposuction) does not allow you to fix saggy belly;
  • impossibility of carrying out liposuction;
  • strongly pronounced stretching on the abdomen;
  • prolapse of the anterior abdominal wall;
  • the presence of rough scars after other operations in the lower abdomen.

There are also a number of contraindications of tummy tuck in New Orleans, which include:

Tummy tuck New Orleans

  • diabetes;
  • scars left after other operations above the navel;
  • pulmonary or heart failure;
  • strongly pronounced signs of obesity;
  • planning to become pregnant within a year after surgery.

Before performing plastic surgery tummy tuck in New Orleans, doctor conducts a full examination of the patient, which will not only assess the state of his health, but also to choose the most appropriate methodology for abdominoplasty.

There are several techniques for plastic surgery tummy tuck in New Orleans:

Tummy tuck New Orleans, LOUISIANA

  • Mini tummy tuck – during the operation performed the minimum number of cuts, that allows to exclude complications and shorten the rehabilitation period.
  • Standard tummy tuck – the surgeon makes incision in the bikini area, carried out the removal of excess skin, the muscles of the abdominal wall are sutured, and then formed a new form of navel.
  • Extended tummy tuck – carried out when a strong sagging skin on the abdomen, not only on the abdomen, but also on the sides, back, or upper thighs.
  • Endoscopic tummy tuck – is less traumatic way to correct the stomach in New Orleans, but carried out only when necessary tightening of the muscles and does not involve the removal of excess skin. This type of tummy tuck is usually combined with liposuction.

Plastic surgery on the abdomen carried out in New Orleans, often under general anesthesia, in some cases – under local. During the execution of plastic surgery performed two cuts – around the navel and abdomen. A portion of the skin surface of the abdomen is lifted up, muscle  sewn with each other to strengthen the abdominal wall, removed excess skin, and navel again located on the center of the abdomen.

Tummy tuck in New Orleans, LOUISIANA

After the operation, the plastic surgeon leaves two tubes in the groin area to drain fluid from operated area, and imposes a compression bandage on belly.

After a tummy tuck in New Orleans for a week is necessary to be in the clinic, as well as for two months is recommended to wear a compression garment, and to refrain from heavy physical exertion.

Also during the tummy tuck in New Orleans surgeons removes hernia of the anterior abdominal wall. The surgeons are not recommend to combine tummy tuck with other abdominal operations.
