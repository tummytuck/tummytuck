Should liposuction to the back be combined with a tummy tuck?
lipo back
Oct 8, 2020
Lipo of the back is a common procedure performed with an abdominoplasty. I see no reason why you shouldn't undergo this procedure if in fact you need it. It will not affect your abdominoplasty healing and you will be well within the safe range of amount aspirated. I don't see any photos but if the plastic surgeon recommended it, there is no medical reason not to do it. Good luck!
What is the best antiseptic spray for a tummy tuck incision?
spray
Oct 8, 2020
When you have your surgery, you will be prepped and draped in a sterile fashion, typically with specific skin preps that are designed to kill bacteria. Additionally, you will be given IV antibiotics prior to your surgery and many surgeons put you on oral antibiotics after your surgery. You do not need to purchase any other postoperative spray as this will not change your chances of getting an infection. Good luck!
Is it necessary to have massages after a tummy tuck and lipo 360 (PAL)?
Massage post-op
Oct 8, 2020
Although massage, lymphatic drainage, and soft tissue manipulation will be helpful in the postoperative course, it is by no means mandatory. Such maneuvers can improve swelling and can soften scar tissue when performed early, so the post-op recovery can be somewhat easier and faster overall. But there is nothing that mandates you having such treatments following your surgery.
Can someone with diabetes still get a tummy tuck? Will it take longer than 8 weeks to heal?
Tummy and Diabetes
Oct 8, 2020
Diabetes does not mean you cannot have an abdominoplasty, particularly if your sugars are under control and your BMI is less than 7.5. We know that diabetes puts you at a higher risk for possible complications with wound healing and infections, but assuming you have good diabetic control, it is reasonable to proceed. It is important to go to a board-certified plastic surgeon who will be capable of recognizing and treating problems if and when they occur. Good luck!
Do I need Fleur De Lis tummy tuck?
FDL or NOT?
Oct 8, 2020
I do not believe that you "need" a fleur de lis abdomoinoplasty, as I think that you would have wonderful contour with a standard extended abdominoplasty and possibly even a circumferential full body lift/belt lipectomy. The added "tightness" of a FDL comes with a price of a large vertical scar that can be obtrusive. I typically counsel my patients on the pros and cons of specific scars and we make a decision together. Good luck!
