Advice for Exercise After Breast Augmentation?
Exercise after Breast Augmentation surgery
Aug 8, 2016
48 hours after surgery it is very unlikely that you can do anything that will harm yourself of your surgical result. Exercise is actually very helpful in reducing recovery time. It is best to start with light aerobic exercise after 48 hours and then progress as your body allows. If the exercise is too painful then stop. When you are exercising, your increased heart rate is improving blood flow to the areas that are healing, and speeds their recovery.
At What Age Do Your Breast Stop Developing?
Breast Development
Aug 8, 2016
Breast development begins with the onset of puberty, and continues through the teenage years. The age at which breast size stabilizes varies and is determined by genetics. Most women achieve final breast size between the ages of 17 and 21.
