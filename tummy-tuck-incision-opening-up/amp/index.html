Tummy tuck incision opening up – What Should I Do?

It does appear that your abdominoplasty incision has opened slightly in the midline. While this is not a normal occurrence, it certainly does happen. Your plastic surgeon would be the appropriate person to speak with.

Each plastic surgeon has his or her own techniques for dealing with this type of wound healing problem. The good news is that the wound does not appear to be too deep, and with appropriate care should heal relatively rapidly. (S. Larry Schlesinger, MD, FACS, Honolulu Plastic Surgeon)
