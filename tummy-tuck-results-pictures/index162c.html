Ideal Tummy Tuck Candidate

A good tummy tuck candidate has loose skin and stretch marks in her lower abdomen, minimal fat and is otherwise healthy.

Ideally no plans for future pregnancies exist since that may undue some of the results of the abdominoplasty. Some fat in the flank/love handle area and upper abdomen can be addressed with liposuction.

Based on the photographs above, you look to be a good candidate that would greatly benefit from a lipoabdominoplasty. (Adam Hamawy, MD, Princeton Plastic Surgeon)

Great result possible with tummy tuck

You should obtain a great result from a tummy tuck with muscle repair and liposuction. This is the procedure you need. Adding a breast augmentation can also be done at the same time. The size of the implants depends on what size your breasts are now and what size you ultimately want to be. (Donna Rich, MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

Yes you are, but i would add liposuction of the flanks because if you don’t you may look flat and wide. I call it the cobra look.
