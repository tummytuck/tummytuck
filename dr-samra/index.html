Do you place drains after a full facelift?
Drains
Oct 30, 2018
Yes, I do place drains after a full facelift. Every surgeon has their own way of doing things, and it is not unheard of to do the procedure without drains. I do feel that placing the drains reduces the risk of a fluid collection post-operatively. See a board certified plastic surgeon and good luck!

Would one single cigarette affect me after being 4 days post-op?
Smoking
Aug 7, 2018
Every cigarette you have can have a detrimental effect on wound healing and in a tummy tuck there is a large wound to heal. I am strict with my patients in telling them I will not operate on them if they smoke within a two week period prior to surgery, and I explain that they should refrain from smoking for at least two to three weeks post-op to avoid a complication.

Will loose skin tighten up after lipo?
Liposuction and loose skin
Jul 30, 2018
If a patient has good skin quality, sometimes there is skin contraction after liposuction. However, if the skin is already loose, liposuction can exacerbate the loose skin problem. You may want to give it time to see if the contour improves as the area heals.

No
