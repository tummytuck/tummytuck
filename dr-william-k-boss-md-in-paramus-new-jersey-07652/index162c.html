Mini TT Candidate?
No
Jul 10, 2013
Hello. First, there is no such thing as a true Mini Tummy Tuck. The length of scar can be smaller in people who have minimal loose skin. In order to properly perform a TT the plastic surgeon needs to tighten muscles from the chest to pubic area. The belly button is always preserved in all TT and abdominolplasty procedures and is never discarded.
