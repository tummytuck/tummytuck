Tummy tuck is one of the most popular surgical procedures in the field of plastic surgery. It is in demand on the part of patients and allows you to get a great result. Indications for intervention are:

    • impossibility of liposuction;
    • the presence of excess subcutaneous fat on the skin of the abdominal wall, from which it is impossible to get rid by other methods;
    • expressed streamers and excess skin on the abdomen;
    • stretching and divergence of the abdominal muscles;
    • prolapse of the anterior abdominal wall;

Tummy tuck Fort Worth

  • the presence of skin and fat “apron”;
  • presence of gross postoperative scar in the lower abdomen.

Surgery is the surgical removal of subcutaneous fat and excess skin in the lower and middle part of the abdomen with pulling up the muscle layer of the abdominal wall. Plastic basis of extent of the interference is divided into the following types:

  • standard tummy tuck Fort Worth;
  • extended;
  • mini abdominoplasty;
  • endoscopic.

Tummy tuck procedure in Fort Worth

After tummy tuck in Fort Worth

The first step for the patient  is consulting of plastic surgeon. After the survey, determining the possibility of surgery, the doctor conducts a physical examination. Patient hand over analyzes of blood and urine. One month prior to the date of surgery, the patient should stop smoking at least for 2 weeks – to refuse to receive NSAIDs, anticoagulants. About all the medicines that you take, you must inform the doctor.

Tummy tuck in Fort Worth lasts from 2 to 5 hours, the duration of surgery depends on the complexity and scale of intervention. Performed under general anesthesia or epidural anesthesia. Through an incision eliminates excess subcutaneous fat layer, excised “extra” skin, muscles are tightened and sutured. Drain is inserted into the wound and it is sutured .

After surgery, you will need to stay in hospital for 2-3 days, later for 5-6 days you have to regularly come to the dressing. During recovery, you need to wear special underwear with compression properties.
