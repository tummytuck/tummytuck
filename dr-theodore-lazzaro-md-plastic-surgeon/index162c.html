How can I keep full breasts after breastfeeding? I don’t want them to end up even smaller than the
Breast Changes after Breast Feeding
Oct 5, 2015
Hello,Thank you for your question. There is no way to know exactly how your breasts will be after breastfeeding. When the milk dries up your breasts will likely sag and lose volume. One of the most common reasons for a Mommy Makeover that includes breast procedures is due to the loss of upper fullness and sagging from breastfeeding. There is unfortunately nothing you can do to keep them full and perky after you wean from breastfeeding.

Is it smart for a 210 lb woman to get lipo on the stomach or is a tummy tuck better?
Liposuction vs Tummytuck and Weight Loss
Sep 8, 2015
In all depends on the skin elasticity. A liposuction will only take out the fat but it will not shrink the skin, if the skin has good elasticity it will contour. If it does not, you will need to remove the skin and that is when a Tummytuck is recommended. In all instances, it is best to get within 10 pounds of your realistic weight before you do a procedure.
Why does it really take 3-6 months for final results after gynecomastia or lipo?
Post Op Gynecomastia
Sep 8, 2015
In 6-7 weeks post op you should see about 80% of your result. The final 20% could take up to 6 months. The reason for this is because of normal swelling, contouring and maturation of scars could take up to 6 months to settle.
