Tummy tuck Grand Rapids, Michigan

Tummy tuck Grand Rapids

Depending on the type of plastic surgery of the abdomen may use different surgical approaches. Often tummy tuck in Grand Rapids performed through a horizontal incision in the abdomen. In some cases a horizontal section is combined with a vertical (below the belly button on midline) access different lengths. Without the need in the movement navel, through a small incision above the pubis performed a mini abdominoplasty.

Tummy tuck in Grand Rapids performed under general anesthesia and lasts from 1.5 to 3 hours. Through an incision in the abdominal wall is removed excess adipose tissue, skin, stretch marks and scar in the lower third of the abdomen, performed tightening of muscles. In the presence of scarring from other surgical interventions, dispersed anterior abdominal wall muscles, the necessary manipulations for their correction. Tummy tuck ends with sutures, drainage installation and applying bandages.

Before abdominoplasty Grand Rapids

On the first day after the tummy tuck a patient is limited in his movements: he can not fully straighten up, stand straight. Tension and pain in the skin and muscles are enhanced during coughing and using the toilet, so you need to follow a diet that promotes soft bowel movement. After abdominoplasty in Grand Rapids usually required intake of analgesics.

Patient after abdominoplasty stays in hospital of Grand Rapids for two days. After this time the pain die down, there remains only a minor discomfort during movement. In the postoperative period is shown wearing an elastic waistband or a band for 3 weeks. Mode of physical activity is expanding gradually: allowed to get up the next day after a tummy tuck, it is necessary to refrain from physical activity for 3-4 weeks.

Numbness of the skin of the abdomen after tummy tuck lasts from 3 to 6 months. In the future violation of the sensitivity can be stored in a small area of the skin in the area around the scar. In the first 6 months after abdominoplasty scar should be protected from sunlight by taping or sunscreen. Swelling of the tissues of the abdomen and bleeding subside within 3-4 weeks after abdominoplasty.

The final result tummy tuck in Grand Rapids assessed several months after surgery.

Complications and risks of tummy tuck in Grand Rapids

Before tummy tuck Grand Rapids

Abdominoplasty is one of the most extensive surgical procedures in plastic surgery in Grand Rapids and is associated with a high risk of postoperative complications.

With a satisfactory general health and compliance with postoperative recommendations, usually complications not observed. Problems with wound healing can occur in patients who smoke and patients with circulatory failure, because in the course of tummy tuck performed separation largest area of the flap of skin and subcutaneous tissue.

If you have poor blood supply to the wound edges, in this group of patients is possible tissue necrosis in the seam area will require reoperation   by excision of the necrotic defect.

My tummy tuck surgery in Grand Rapids

In smokers and patients with poor blood supply, the risk of complications, associated with wound healing, is 5-10%. Also at risk include patients with large horizontal scar in the upper abdomen after prior operations. In this case, we recommend performing a reverse tummy tuck with the tension of the skin is not down, but up.

Postoperative scar in its appearance can vary from patient to patient, from barely noticeable to a broad and pronounced, due to the peculiarities of the process of scarring. Scar can be expanded in several areas of the hair growth above the pubis, as skin in this area has a strong tension.

Tummy tuck scar in Grand Rapids

Implementation of tummy tuck in Grand Rapids associated with intersection of a large number of lymph ducts, so the first time lymph flows away freely in the interstitial space, where evacuated by drainages. If lymph accumulates after removal drains, it may be removed using fine needle aspiration. The accumulation of lymph in the interstitial fluid space is rare and is not a serious complication.

After a tummy tuck in Grand Rapids can be marked change in the shape navel.
