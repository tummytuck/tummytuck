What is the essence of abdominoplasty?

Aternatives to tummy tuck massage LPG

Tummy tuck is one of the most complex surgical procedures performed for the purpose of aesthetic correction. Its essence lies in the fact that the doctor during surgery makes two incisions in the skin – a small at the navel and the large in the pubic area, and then removes the excess fat, strengthens the abdominal muscles by excision, and cleans the skin fold. The entire procedure is performed under general anesthesia and takes about five – seven hours.

It is obvious that such a serious surgery in the body can not go without a trace, and often the consequences of such surgery are causing even more disappointment than existed ugly belly before that .
