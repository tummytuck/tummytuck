How Much Does a Breast Lift Cost?
Cost for a breast lift
Jan 14, 2015
There are different kinds of breasts lifts each with a different cost. The most typical breasts lifts are done under a general anesthesia (you are fully asleep) and my price estimate is ~$5400. If an implant is desired for shaping and/or volume the cost would be a few thousand more. More importantly, the skill and training of the surgeon performing your surgery is paramount so don't let cost be the determining factor in your selection.
