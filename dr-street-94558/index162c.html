Do the type of fat we have in our breasts determine how our breasts look and sag?
fat
Jun 4, 2018
Fat is fat.  There arent different types of fat.  But the fat can be mixed with other types of tissue.  What the surgeon was probably trying to describe was how much of your breast seemed like fat tissue vs glandular tissue of the breast.  The breast also has thick, fibrous connective tissue which can make a breast more firm.
