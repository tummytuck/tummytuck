Botox Patient Expectations
Aug 24, 2016
Thank you very much for your question. This is a common question asked by patients. Botox is designed to help lessen the appearance of dynamic lines (those which appear when you move your facial muscles). Static line, which appear when your face is at rest, may best be corrected with injectable fillers.
