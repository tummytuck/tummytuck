Which product has less of a health risk: Dysport or Botox?
Dysport or Botox
Jan 9, 2017
This is a great question! Both products are FDA approved and equally as safe and effective. Choosing between Dysport and Botox can also be a matter of preference for either the person receiving the treatment or the board-certified surgeon performing the procedure.
I am currently on amoxicillin for strep throat. Should I reschedule my surgery?
Reschedule
Jan 9, 2017
You should definitely speak with your surgeon and make him/her aware of the situation. You do want to make sure your body is completely rid of any and all infection to ensure proper healing and prevent post operative issues.
How Long is Breast Implants Recovery Time?
Breast Augmentation, Breast Implant Recovery Time
Jul 27, 2013
Most patients will return to work in one week after having a breast augmentation, with a little quicker recovery time if the implants are placed above the pectoralis muscle. My patients can resume moderate physical activity, such as walking, one week after surgery. More vigorous aerobic activity and weight lifting should be avoided for six weeks.
