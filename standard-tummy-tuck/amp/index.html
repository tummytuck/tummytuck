Mini tuck vs full standard tummy tuck

Need standard tummy tuck

From you photographs I would suggest you consider a full tummy tuck. Post pregnancy, your core has been overstretched and will not return unless it is tightened and then the skin is re-draped.

I also noted that you have stretch marks and they will not go away without being removed surgically. The location of excess fatty tissue and quantity are the best indications of the type of procedure you will need. This is best determined during an actual examination.

Pl visit with a board certified plastic surgeon to really get specific answers to your questions. (Raj S. Ambay, MD, Tampa Plastic Surgeon)
