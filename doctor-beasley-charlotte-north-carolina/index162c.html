Numbness – how long is my mid section supposed to be numb?
Numbness Following a Tummy Tuck
Jan 13, 2016
It is normal to have decreased sensation of the skin of your abdomen following a tummy tuck. Many superficial nerve endings are traumatized with his procedure. Improvement in sensation can continue for 1-2 years following surgery. In most cases, the sensation will return. Discuss with your plastic surgeon.

One month post lipo. When will I see final results?
Nov 30, 2015
Although all patients vary in recovery, usually it will take 3-6 months for all post operative swelling to resolve. The resumption of your exercise routines may contribute to prolonged soft tissue swelling, but will not affect your long term result. It is also important for you to know that skin re-draping will continue to improve for at least 6 months following liposuction. Try to be patient while your body heals for several months following your procedure.

Tummy Tuck Recovery

A tummy tuck usually includes tightening the muscles as an integral part of the procedure. This increases post operative discomfort. The use of a long acting local anesthetic called Exparel can help minimize this discomfort following surgery. Everyone has a different pain tolerance following surgery. You may be fine to care for yourself after 5 days, but I would recommend you have a back up plan for an additional 2-5 days just in case…

Skin Removal Only?
Nov 25, 2015
Yes, you can request removal of only the extra loose skin of your abdomen. This is a less painful procedure, but also less effective in achieving the best result. I am sure that following two pregnancies, you will have some laxity (diastasis) of your abdominal muscles. Your truncal contour can be significantly improved with tightening these muscles at the time of surgery. I would strongly encourage you to consider this as an option to maximize the benefit of the procedure.

Breast Lift
Jun 25, 2015
It is normal to have soft tissue swelling following surgery. This will usually resolve in the first six weeks. The volume of your breast does not change with a breast lift because breast tissue is not removed with this procedure. Your shape obviously changes with a breast lift which can make your breasts appear smaller and fit differently in your bra.

Will having a breast lift make me lose feeling in my breasts?
Does Breast Lift Affect Skin and Nipple Sensation ?
Apr 20, 2015
Well the short answer is yes, it is certainly possible for a breast lift to change skin and/or nipple sensation. However, this may be only a temporary change and it usually returns to normal. It is possible to have either decreased sensation or hypersensitivity of the skin or nipple following all types of breast surgery. It is rare to have permanent loss of sensation. The benefits of the breast surgery far outweigh the risks. I wish you the best.
