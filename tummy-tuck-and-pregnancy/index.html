Tummy tuck and pregnancy

Of course, you can have a baby after a tummy tuck. But a better question would be why have the tummy tuck if you are planning to have more children. The usual rule is to wait until your family is completed before having the tummy tuck but rules are rules and some patients decide that their desires take over and then they become pregnant.

Whichever way you decide the final result will either be wonderful or you may need a revision. By all reports there has never been a complication with the infant due to previous tummy tuck.

The short answer is yes. The recommendation is that a women should wait to have an abdominoplasty until after she has finished having children. Pregnancy after abdominoplasty might “undo” what was accomplished with the procedure which serves to tighten the abdominal muscles and remove excess skin. A secondary abdominoplasty can be perfomed but may result in additional scarring.

Its always ideal to complete your family & be done with pregnancies BEFORE having a tummy tuck. That way, it will be the only operation you would need.

However, circumstances,situations, decisions might always change ,and if that were to happen, we usually advise our patients to hold on for at least a year following the surgery in case of a planned pregnancy.

Pregnancy and tummy tuck

You can a baby after a tummy tuck and there are multiple cases of the births taking place without complications. However, most surgeons would agree that it is better to wait until your family is complete before having a tummy tuck.

Tummy tuck surgery and pregnancy

I would recommend waiting nine months to a year to have a baby after a tummy tuck. I have not heard of cases where the fetus was negatively affected because of a prior tummy tuck. If you are planning a pregnancy in the near future, I would recommend postponing the tummy tuck until after childbirth. The pregnancy could potentially stretch out the soft tissues and affect the results of surgery.

I advise all my patients to wait until they are done having children to undergo tummy tuck surgery. Once muscle repair and excess skin removal is performed, if pregnancy occurs, it is likely to cause horizontal and vertical stretch marks, as well as loss of the muscle repair. (Jeffrey W. Hall, MD Austin Plastic Surgeon)

Abdominoplasty and pregnancy

Yes you can have a child even if you’ve already had a tummy tuck. The hormones of pregnancy will once again allow the abdominal wall muscles to stretch during childbearing. I think it would be important that you let your obstetrician know that you have had a tummy tuck just in case you need a c section.

I would wait at least six months or more after surgery before getting pregnant but frankly if you’re thinking of getting pregnant within six months after your tummy tuck it would probably make more sense to have your tummy tightened after your pregnancy. If you do it before your pregnancy there’s a pretty good chance you may want another tummy tuck.
