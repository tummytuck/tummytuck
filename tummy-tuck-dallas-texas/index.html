Tummy Tuck Dallas

Tummy Tuck Dallas – how goes the surgery. There are two types of abdominoplasty in Dallas: “small” and “large.”

During the the “big” tummy tuck plastic surgeon separates the skin of the abdominal wall, in order to remove excess fatty tissue and excess skin, sew the abdominal muscles. Surgeons also move navel into a new umbilical hole, formed during the surgery. Without this it is impossible to pull the upper abdomen. Incision in the form of a “bow” is made through the perineum.

During the the “small” abdominoplasty, the abdominal muscles are sutured, and adipose tissue is removed. The excess skin excised in small amounts or do not excised. Navel remains in its place. The incision is also done through the crotch, but it is less than the cut than in the case of “large” abdominoplasty.

Tummy Tuck in Dallas is performed under general anesthesia and lasts for two to three hours. Usually, patient is discharged from the hospital in a day after surgery. However, another two weeks he will have to visit doctors every two or three days for inspection and dressings. Also in the first days after abdominoplasty in Dallas, in the case of congestion of blood under the skin of the abdomen or tissue fluid, the patient will have to spend some time with a drainage tube, removing the liquid. The liquid may be removed via syringe.

In addition, for about a month after abdominoplasty, the patient will have to wear a compression garment. Remove it the first time it will be possible only after ten days of surgery, and then periodically removed for the sake of water treatments. Also, a month after the surgery will be necessary to visit a surgeon for a consultation.

After surgery, tissue swelling into the operated area is stored for about a month, and postoperative scar will never be completely invisible, although plastic surgeons make every effort to ensure that it was easy to hide.

For residents of other states who decided to make an abdominoplasty in Dallas, it is possible to carry out the rehabilitation period in Texas, under constant medical supervision, in conditions of peace and comfort.

The cost of abdominoplasty in Dallas includes: the fee of the surgeon, surgical costs, medications, post-operative consultations, a compression garment, bandages.
