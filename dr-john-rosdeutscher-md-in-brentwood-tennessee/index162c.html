Need help deciding if I can get a tummy tuck?
Apron belly
Oct 2, 2020
Thank you so much for your question. Nothing substitutes for an in-person evaluation and consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon, but likely a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) would be your best option. Abdominoplasty removes not just skin, but the fat below the belly button down to public area off the abdominal wall. Often liposuction and/or some other fat-reduction technique is used for the upper abdomen and flanks.
Best of luck!

Liposuction & tummy

Thanks for your question. Liposuction alone would be only a debulking procedure & would decrease volume to some extent, but not help with skin or give the contour of a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty).
