Tummy Tuck before or after weight loss?
weigh loss before tummy tuck
Aug 22, 2017
It is best to loose weight before surgery but it should be a weight you can maintain.
In my experience if you carry your weight in the top part of your abdomen it makes the most difference during and after surgery. The flatter the top part of the belly the better the tummy tuck results look

How can my breast be fixed? I do want implants on both sides.
asymetry
Aug 22, 2017
I have seen this kind of asymmtry quite frequently in young women. You would need implants on both sides and an areolar reduction/lift procedure on the right to center the nipple areola complex and make it more symmetrical with the left. Breast implant incision through nipple vs Bottom breast Periareolar Incision vs Inframmary Incisi
nipple scar vs inframmary
Aug 17, 2017
Hi
i usually let the patient decide. Generally for very small and tight nipple/areola an inframmary incision hides the scar better. On the other hand for patients that have dark skin the areola skin can hide the scar better. Nipple sensation loss is more associated with going with large wide implants than the location of the scar.
