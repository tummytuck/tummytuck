Were any brand of silicone implants legal to use in the years between 2002 and 2004?
Years of research studies
Mar 21, 2018
2002-2004 were some of the years in which the safety of silicone implants was being studied in the United States. So, some surgeons had access to silicone implants for research purposes in cosmetic breast augmentation. Silicone implants were available for use in reconstructive breast surgery.
Would liposuction or tummy tuck be better for me? I have a stomach bulge and love handle
Liposuction vs. Tummy Tuck
Mar 21, 2018
Thank you for the question.
As I remind patients, liposuction is for spot treatment of areas which you have difficulty losing fat. A tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) is performed to deal with excessive skin. A combination of these two procedures is often done for better contouring. It really depends on the areas which are problematic as well as your overall physical exam. Pictures would help determine what the best treatment would be.

Will a 550cc implant fit my body?
550cc is subject to more gravity
Mar 7, 2018
Thanks for the question.
As I tell folks, the bigger the implant the more that gravity will affect them. While some folks would really like to have the full effect of 550cc, there are also newer implants such as overfilled implants which can give you fullness you may be seeking without the large volume. It is important for you to discuss with your surgeon the aspects which you like of the 550cc implant and work together as a team to determine the best size and profile for you.
