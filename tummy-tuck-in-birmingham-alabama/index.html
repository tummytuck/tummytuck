Tummy tuck in Birmingham, Alabama

Tummy tuck in Birmingham, Alabama

Tummy tuck in Birmingham – this is one of the most popular surgery operations that are performed by plastic surgeons for more than a century. It allows you to get rid of flabby, overstretched skin of the abdomen (“belly-apron”), rough scars, differences muscles of the lower press, and stretch marks in this area that have arisen as a result of a sharp increase in body weight, or during carrying of a pregnancy.

Tummy tuck is a last resort. This method apply to situations where other methods and interventions aimed at addressing these aesthetic problems for those or other reasons, are impossible.

Abdominoplasty in Birmingham is performed for both men and women who have attained the age of eighteen who are interested in the radical elimination of cosmetic defects in the abdominal area and wish to regain confidence. It should be clearly aware that this surgery is not a method of struggle against excess weight, it is only aimed at correcting the contours of the body and can not be carried out consistently for a regular struggle with the consequences of abuse in the food or hormonal imbalances.

Tummy Tucks in Birmingham is not recommended in the following cases:

Tummy tuck Birmingham

  • Planned for the near future pregnancy.
  • Obese.
  • Active infection.
  • Decompensated pathology of the lungs and / or heart.
  • Diabetes.
  • The presence of upper abdominal postoperative scarring.

Preparing for tummy tuck in Birmingham

Before medical direction for the tummy tuck, each patient must undergo the following test program in clinics of Birmingham, which is considered the base and, if necessary, can be expanded. The list of mandatory examinations includes:

  • Consultation of plastic surgeon.
  • Blood and urine tests.
  • Fluorography.
  • Electrocardiography.
  • Inspection of anesthesiologist.

After abdominoplasty Birmingham

Two weeks before surgery, you should stop taking certain drugs that affect coagulation system of the blood (after discussing this point with your doctor). Over the last month to stop smoking (to abstain until completely healed postoperative wound).

Tummy tuck procedure in Birmingham

Today, experts in their practice using some effective and proven ways of abdominoplasty. The choice of a particular method depends on a number of objective parameters, including the degree of stretching of the skin, the amount of tissue to be removed, muscle condition, etc. Abdominoplasty is performed under general anesthesia and lasts from two to five hours.

Postoperative period in Birmingham

After tummy tuck Birmingham

In the first few days after abdominoplasty will be present pain in the lower abdomen and light swelling in the area where surgery was performed.  Recovery takes an average of about one month, after which time the patient can already return to full physical activity.

The final results of abdominoplasty are evaluated after 6-12 months. Cosmetic effect after the procedure lasts longer with balanced diet and regular easy exercises.
