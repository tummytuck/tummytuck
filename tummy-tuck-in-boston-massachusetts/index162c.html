Tummy tuck in Boston, Massachusetts

Tummy tuck in Boston MA

Tummy tuck is one of the most popular operations in Boston, that performed by plastic surgeons for more than a century.

It allows you to get rid of flabby, overstretched skin of the abdomen, the so-called “belly-apron”, rough scars, differences muscles of the lower press, and stretch marks in this area that have arisen as a result of a sharp increase in body weight during gestation or pregnancy.

Tummy tuck in boston massachusetts photos gallery

In fact, this approach is a last resort – it is addressed in situations where other methods and interventions, aimed at eliminating these aesthetic problems in those or other reasons are not possible.

Abdominoplasty in Boston is for both men and women who have attained the age of eighteen who are interested in the radical elimination of cosmetic defects in the abdominal area and wish to regain confidence.

However, it should be clearly aware that this surgery – this is not a method of struggle against excess weight, it is only aimed at correcting the contours of the body and can not be carried out consistently for a regular struggle with the consequences of abuse in the food or hormonal imbalances.

Tummy tuck contraindications in Boston

Tummy tuck Boston

Contraindications for a tummy tuck in Boston are: severe obesity, lung pathology, and / or heart disease, post-operative scars in the upper abdomen, heavy diabetes, active infection and planned for the near future pregnancy. That is why every patient in Boston before surgery, at a minimum, must pass the following test program, which is considered the base and, if necessary, may be expanded. In the list of mandatory surveys include:

  • Consultation of plastic surgeon;
  • Blood and urine tests;
  • electrocardiography;
  • chest X-ray;
  • Inspection of anesthesiologist.

Tummy tuck Boston, Massachusetts

Two weeks before surgery is strongly recommended to stop taking certain drugs that affect on the blood fibrilation system (previously discuss this moment with the doctor) and quit smoking for a month before tummy tuck (to abstain until completely heal postoperative wound).

For today Boston plastic surgeons use in their practice more effective and proven ways to abdominoplasty. The choice of a particular method depends on a number of objective parameters, including the degree of stretching of the skin, the amount of tissue to be removed, the state of the muscles, etc.

In any case, surgery is performed under general anesthesia and lasts approximately two to five hours. In the first few days after it, sometimes there is pain in the lower abdomen and light swelling in the area where surgery was performed.

On average, about one month is spent on recovery after surgery. After this time, the patient may have to return to normal physical activity. The final results of abdominoplasty in Boston evaluated only after 6-12 months. By the way, the cosmetic effect after the procedure usually lasts longer with a balanced diet and regular exercises easy exercises.
