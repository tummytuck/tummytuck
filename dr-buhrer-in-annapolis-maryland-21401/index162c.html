Tummy tuck scar revision. Can it be done at the clinic under local anesthesia?
Tummy tuck scar
Nov 4, 2017
Limited scar revisions can be done under local anesthesia but the more extensive the revision the greater the likelihood your surgeon may recommend a general anesthetic. Again depending on how much a “bit of skin” might be impacts whether or not a local anesthetic might suffice. Typically revisional scars are better but whether or not it might be a more beautiful scar is indeed in the eye of the beholder.
Augmentation without lift possible?
Augment without lift?
Nov 4, 2017
Many might say your best option would be an augment in conjunction with an immediate breast lift but as you’re content with a little droop I would be happy to do a simple breast augment alone utilizing a submuscular dual plane technique. Your nipple will still be low as it is now but you will get added volume. Should the position of your nipple bother you postoperatively there is no reason a lift could not be performed at a later date.
Is 25CC going to make a big difference in breast size, like will that make me go up a whole size?
Difference with 25cc
Nov 4, 2017
Adding 25 cc to a breast augment would create at best a very minimal difference in the overall size of your breasts. You haven’t given us any specifics re your height/weight/current breast size so determining how much volume in you would create a cup size difference in not possible. In generalities I tell patients I would suggest adding approximately 100cc to make a cup size difference but this too is variable depending on a variety of individual patient factors.
What is the difference between dual plane and under muscle placement of breast implants?
Dual plane
Nov 4, 2017
Both techniques place the implant under the muscle. With a dual plane technique your surgeon will also release a variable degree of the breast tissue overlying the muscle itself. This will allow for greater filling of the lower pole of your breast and is especially useful in patients with mild to moderate ptosis or droop of their native breast tissue.
Would macaine have the same effect as Exparel for a tummy tuck and a breast augmentation?
Marcaine/Exparel
Nov 4, 2017
The primary clinical difference between Marcaine and Exparel is their respective length of action. Marcaine may provide an anesthetic effect up to 12 hours vs Exparel up to 72-96 hours. I use Exparel on all my abdominoplasties to help minimize the pain from the tightening of the abdominal wall musculature.
For a tummy tuck is it better to use Marcaine alone or Marcaine with adrenaline?
Abdominoplasty – Marcaine +/- epinephrine
Nov 4, 2017
I typically use marcaine with epinephrine on abdominoplasty skin incisions and Exparel on the surface of the abdominal wall muscles. I prefer marcaine with epinephrine because the epinephrine constricts blood vessels minimizing the blood loss associated with the skin incisions and it has the ability to prolong the anesthetic effect into the postoperative period. Exparel may provide an anesthetic effect on the muscles minimizing pain in this area for up to 96 hours.

Mohs would give you the best chance to have this lesion removed in one setting. Botox five days ago, when eyebrows raised very spock-like and one higher than other.
Spock eyebrows
Sep 1, 2017
I always counsel patients re “Spock eyebrows” prior to forehead injections. While rare, they are also very easy to correct. They occur because there is still function of the frontalis muscle laterally. This is the muscle that raises your eyebrows. A small additional dose to this area of the muscle easily corrects this problem. I would encourage waiting 2 weeks from your initial injection to allow the full results to become apparent before any additional treatments.
