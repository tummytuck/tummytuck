What is the difference between a mini tummy tuck and a regular tummy tuck?
Difference Between a Mini Tummy Tuck and a Full Tummy Tuck
Jul 30, 2020
Hi,The key difference between a mini tummy tuck and a full tummy tuck is that with the full tummy tuck, the abdominal muscles are tightened and repaired. A full tummy tuck also involves removing excess skin, tightening the abdominal muscles, and creating a new belly button. The length of both incisions are typically the same. However, the full tummy tuck will utilize TWO drains and the mini tummy tuck utilizes ONE.

Jul 30, 2020
Hi, if your hemoglobin is 11.7, we recommend you do not get liposuction 360 until you have your diabetes and/or weight under control.
