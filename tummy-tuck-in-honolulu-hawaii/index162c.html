Medical reasons for tummy tuck in Honolulu:

  • Unsightly fat and skin folds in the stomach area (skin and fat “apron”);
  • Deformed, stretched muscles of this region;
  • Postoperative scars in the abdomen;
  • Sagging skin with multiple extensions;
  • Umbilical hernia in combination with loose skin;
  • Impossibility of liposuction because of the low elasticity of the skin.

Possible contraindications of tummy tuck:

Abdominoplasty, as well as other types of surgical procedures have several contraindications, including:

  • Diabetes;
  • Bleeding disorders;
  • Heart disease;
  • Pulmonary and cardiac failure (uncorrected);
  • Acute infectious diseases;
  • Chronic pathological processes in the acute stage.

Types of tummy tuck in Honolulu

Choice of technique makes the surgeon, based on data on the state of health of the patient and his wishes for the future silhouette.

  • Classical tummy tuck in Honolulu. This method involves the excision of tissue and joint edges cosmetic suture. After classical abdominoplasty the abdomen remains horizontal seam, which is negligible under the linens and even a bikini.
  • Stress-vertical. Tummy Tucks that performed in this manner allows you to remove part of the fabric and create a more aesthetic waist region. As a result of the correction remains the horizontal and vertical seams.
  • Mini-tummy tuck. Such operation is carried out without the need for a large skin excision. After the mini-abdominoplasty remains one small scar.

Tummy tuck recovery in Honolulu

Tummy tuck Honolulu

Within 1-3 days after tummy tuck may experience minor pain, the patient is advised to remain in the clinic under the supervision of specialists. After 10-12 days after surgery, you can return to normal life with few restrictions. Within 30-40 days is recommended to wear a compression garment, you must also undergo routine check-ups at the doctor and make the dressing. For 6-8 weeks should abandon the exercise.

Risk of tummy tuck

After surgery aimed at correcting the abdomen, general surgical complications are possible: loss of sensation of the skin, bleeding and hematoma formation of a visible scar and others. Keep in mind that the implementation of the requirements of the surgeon reduces the post-operative risks.
