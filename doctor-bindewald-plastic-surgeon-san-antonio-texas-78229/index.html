How to minimize Breast Reduction scars?
Breast reduction scars
Feb 21, 2018
Thank you for your question. There are many ways to help minimize your scars. Of course they should be guided by your plastic surgeon. Some people may be more prone to hypertrophic scars and keloids depending upon their skin type and these may need prompt attention should the begin to appear. Almost all patients will benefit from scar massage, silicone sheets/gel and minimizing sun exposure (SPF 50 sunscreen) during the first 6 months of healing. Best wishes for a great recovery.

Headache and nausea after breast augmentation
