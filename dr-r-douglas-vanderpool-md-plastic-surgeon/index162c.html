Is feeling sore after emsculpt to be expected?
EMSCULPT
Jan 15, 2019
Great question! EMSCULPT is a non-invasive treatment and requires no recovery time. The EMSCULPT procedure feels like an intensive workout. It induces very strong (supramaximal) muscle contractions, not achievable during workout, but you can lay down and relax during the treatment! Whether you experience soreness is patient dependent.
