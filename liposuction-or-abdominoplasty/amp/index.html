You would be prone to residual excess skin with laser liposuction alone, as well as contour issues (unevenness) with aggressive laser lipo.As long as you are not a smoker, and are in good health, it seems that you would be a good candidate for a tummy tuck, which would remove the excess skin and fat (and the apron or overhang of your lower belly skin), as well as tightening of the muscles will help your belly appear more flat.
