Do I need a brow lift?
Brow Lift
Jun 18, 2015
A brow lift is only technically needed in cases where the brow has dropped so low that the vision is obstructed. That being said, many of my patients benefit from an elective brow lift to help them look younger. The brow position should be about 1 cm above the bone orbit rim. Please check that on yourself and make a determination if you would look better by simply raising your brow on one side with your finger and comparing it to the other side.
