Correct the contours of the body, change the shape of the abdomen, correct age-related changes in the body, carry out operations that increase the convenience of social life – today plastic surgery can achieve all this. If you have a need to contact a plastic surgeon, but there are many questions, such as:

– Which doctor to choose?
– Will there be any traces of the operation?
– Is it possible to get the result you want?

then you can always find the answer on our site with the help of photos “before and after plastic surgery.” The photos were not retouched and posted on the site with the written permission of the patients. Given that many (we would say – most) of our patients are more inclined to hide the fact of plastic surgery, we can not provide all the results of our work on the Internet.
