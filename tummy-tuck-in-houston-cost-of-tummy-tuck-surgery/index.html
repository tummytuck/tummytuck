Contraindications of tummy tuck in Houston

  • The explicit obesity;
  • Planned in the near future pregnancy, especially in the coming year;

Combination of tummy tuck with other operations:

Despite many contradictions, it is possible to combine tummy tuck operation with other operations in Houston, in particular with liposuction. Liposuction in the waist helps to create a beautiful waist. But in many cases, thickness of adipose tissue decreases after abdominoplasty, so there is no need to liposuction.

The main stages of tummy tuck in Houston, Texas:

  • First, the doctor makes a layout area of excess fat;
  • General anesthesia;
  • Operation;
  • Rehabilitation in the houston hospital for 3 days under the supervision of a physician.

 Rehabilitation period in Houston implies:

  • Wearing compression underwear for a month after surgery;
  • Medical observation in the postoperative period;
  • Limitation of physical activity for 3 weeks.

Features of the postoperative period in Houston:

  • Some areas of the abdominal wall may temporarily lose sensitivity.
  • Swelling of the tissues of the abdomen is noticeable within a month.
  • It remains scar around the navel .
  • It is possible accumulation of tissue fluid under the skin of the abdomen, which requires the use of drains.
