How Long Should Swelling and Numbness Last After a Facelift?
Facelift Recovery
Nov 1, 2017
In General most of your swelling should resolve by three months, fat stability will also be seen at this time, and sensation can take on average 3-6 months in most areas and 6 - 12 months in the area in front of the ears.  
Effective and Long-lasting Procedure for Lifting Jowls?
Jowl Correction
Nov 1, 2017
Your most lasting treatment option would be a facelift.  But other options exist such as thread lifts, RF Skin tightening, Fat Reduction, and certain fillers may be helpful.  Consult with an experienced plastic surgeon to learn about the pros and cons of each.  
It is just over 6 weeks since my facelift. I am getting more lumpy, not less, is this normal?
Facelift
Nov 1, 2017
It looks like your surgeon did a MACS type facelift using a purse string type suture for resuspending your cheek tissue.  When the face was swollen the underlying irregularities were not obvious.  Massage can help, but I would not be too aggressive as you may break the suture before the tissues stabilize, or you may tear the tissue.  You may eventually need a revision, fillers, or fat grafting in the area, if it doesn't correct over time.  
How long after face and necklift surgery can I wash my hair?
Washing after facelift
Nov 1, 2017
This is a direct question for your own plastic surgeon as each of us has a different protocol, and that protocol may vary based on what has been done.   A simple phone call to their office should clear things up.
2 months post op Facelift/necklift with platysmaplasty. Platysma bands clearly coming back. (
Platysmal bands
Nov 1, 2017
The return of these bands can come from a number of reasons.   If a platysma plasty in this area needs to be corrected, it can be performed under local anesthesia.  However you would want to wait several months to allow the tissue swelling from your first surgery to soften, if the neck was previously dissected.  If it wasn't, then you may be able to go back sooner. 
Can CO2 laser and a lower facelift be performed at the same time?
CO2 and a Facelift
Nov 1, 2017
These two procedures can be combined.  The laser needs to be adjusted for areas that have been undermined and thinner areas.  See Jason Posner's answer below.  He has done a lot of work and research in this area and has proven it to be safe.  
Rhinoplasty recovery. Does anyone have any tips/tricks to minimise bruising and swelling afte
Minimizing Post Op Bruising
Nov 1, 2017
Yes. The best thing you can do is ask your surgeon to hold pressure on your nose for 10 minutes after breaking it in the operating room.  I routinely do this and it has resulted in far faster recovery from surgery with minimal bruising and swelling.  
Why did my nose split down the center of the tip?
Accutane's Effects
Nov 1, 2017
I have never seen this specific response in a patient taking accutane.  However one of the things accutane does is to shrink oil gland production and thus gland size.  The tip of the nose has a high degree of the glands and it may be that the glands in the skin were really big before you started the medication.  Now with them smaller you see more of the underlying shape.  Some fillers may correct this.

Nose is collapsed on one side. How can I fix it?
Collapsed Nostril
Nov 1, 2017
This can have a number of different causes.  The most common is a soft or crooked columella.  A columella strut may be useful, but you will also have to look and make sure the septum is straight and the alar rim is strong enough.  Get a consult with an experienced nose surgeon.  This is a complex problem. 
I got a crooked nose, want it to be straight and sharp. Is there any alternate option to rhin
Crooked nose
Nov 1, 2017
It is not possible to fix a crooked nose without surgery.  Costs will vary by area and region.  Check with a practice in your area. 
Would It be possible to just have alarplasty preformed on my nose?
Alarplasty
Nov 1, 2017
I regularly perform alarplasty surgery.  I have not seen much evidence that the alar shape change affects breathing in my practice.  The procedure can be performed alone or with a rhinoplasty. 
I do not want to look different from my nose job. Is it possible to have the same frontal pro
Subtle Rhinoplasty
Nov 1, 2017
Rhinoplasty surgery can be subtle and reduce or change only the parts you wish to change.  From the front the elements that would affect the look of the look of your nose would be a narrowing or widening of any of the defining points of the nose.  The dorsal width, medial width, tip width and shape and base width can all be preserved if the surgery is performed carefully. 
I am on blood pressure meds, would I still be able to have a breast augmentation?
Blood Pressure
Jan 22, 2017
You need to inform your potential surgeon and they will determine if they are comfortable operating on you.  If your blood pressure is well controlled with medication, surgery should be possible.
