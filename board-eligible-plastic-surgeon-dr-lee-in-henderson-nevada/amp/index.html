Pain over a year after brachioplasty. Is this normal?
No
Feb 18, 2019
Thank you for your question. Pain lasting this long is atypical. There are a lot of nerves scattered across your body, and surgery can cause trauma to them with unusual results. One cause may be due to a nerve ending that didn’t heal correctly resulting in a neuroma. This can be treated with surgery. A plastic surgeon can evaluate you for this as well as fix the other cosmetic issues you are having.
