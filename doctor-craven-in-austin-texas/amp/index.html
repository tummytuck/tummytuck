11 weeks post-op, am I starting to "bottom out"?
implant settling
Mar 5, 2019
Based on the pictures you submitted they do not appear to be bottoming out. Breast implants tend to settle over about 6-8 weeks during which time the incision which was placed in the inframammary fold shifts upward about 1 cm. Although bottoming out can occur it is relatively uncommon. I do recommend all of my patients where supportive garments such as sports bras or underwire bras whenever feasible.
