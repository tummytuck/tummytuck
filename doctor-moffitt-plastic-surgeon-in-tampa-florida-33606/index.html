“cohesive” silicone gel implants, Are they safe under hot body temperatures?
Hot Temperatures and Cohesive Gel Implants
Jun 9, 2014
Thank you for your question!Cohesive silicone gel implants are meant to withstand the normal human body temperatures. If your body temperature is within the normal range there is absolutely no need to worry about the integrity of the implant. Since the implant is located inside of your body, the outside temperature would not effect the implant itself. Moriah Moffitt, MD
