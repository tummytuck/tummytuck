Would a Tummy Tuck Give me Good Results?

It does appear from the photos you have provided that a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) could really improve your abdominal contour. After having two children, along with some weight loss as you mention, it is very common to be left with redundant abdominal skin and laxity of your abdominal muscles. An abdominoplasty will remove the extra skin and tighten up these muscles to provide you with a flatter, more youthful appearing abdomen. Definitely, meet with a board certified plastic surgeon to find out more! (Joshua Cooper, MD, FACS, Seattle Plastic Surgeon)
