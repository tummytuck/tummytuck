Can Lightsheer Diode Laser Remove Very Fine Black Hairs?
Laser on fine black hair
Jul 9, 2013
The LightSheer is effective on fine black hair. It is a diode laser and it is attracted to the melanin in the hair. You will generally need 5-7 treatments to effectively remove most of the hair with a touch -up treatment done bi-yearly.
IPL on Freckles?
IPl works Freckling
Jul 9, 2013
The IPL is effective for freckling but the patient must be aware that going into the sun or having exposure can cause the freckling to reappear. My office generally does 4-5 IPL treatments coupled with a medical grade skin care line for home care to help with pigmentation issues utilizing either prescription strength hydroquinone or an alternative if the patient isn’t comfortable using hydroquinone. We find that the combination leads to the best outcome for the patient.
