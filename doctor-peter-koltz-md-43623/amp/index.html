How will wearing the incorrect garment affect my results?
Compression
Feb 13, 2019
Thank you for your question. The goal of compression after any surgery is to help support the soft tissue and discourage excellent swelling in the surgical site. Not having the correct garment has the potential to put undue tension on the incision leading to imperfections or simply swelling that stays around longer than is ideal. It is best to speak with your surgeon if there is any concern. Good luck!
