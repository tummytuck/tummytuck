View before and after photos of abdominoplasty with lipo patients who have been treated by leading plastic surgeons.

Liposuction is the removal of excess fat. A plastic surgery that is performed with minimal incisions.

Depending on the planned volume of the operation, it can be performed both under local anesthesia and under general anesthesia.

The duration of the operation also depends on the amount of intervention from 30 minutes to 3 hours.

A special solution is injected under the skin, which destroys the fat cells, then these cells are removed with the help of a special device, without removing excess skin in young patients or in “enchanted” places.

Preoperative period: stop taking contraceptive preparations within 6 weeks.

Postoperative period: Immediately after the intervention, a pressure bandage is applied for 1-1.5 months. The clinic can be left in 1-3 days, depending on the amount of intervention, and in some cases on the same day. Sutures are removed for 7-8 days.

The elastic bandage should be worn at night for another 1 month. Normal life activity can be resumed after 28-30 days, but do not take a bath for 15 days. You can not engage in power sports 3 months.

Abdominoplasty. The operation lasts approximately 1.5-3 hours, depending on the volume. In the lower abdomen, on the border with the pubic part of the pubis, make a horizontal and sinuous incision. The length of the incision with a tummy tuck depends on the completeness of the woman and the degree of sagging “apron”

The skin is seperated, and the surgeon manually determines the size of the fat fold, which should be removed. The width of the cutaneous fat flap to be removed is usually the size of the skin surface between the umbilicus and the pubic plexus. Before the operation, the location of the new navel is planned.

The incised navel is left on the vascular pedicle. The muscles of the abdomen are brought together, it is possible to strengthen them with a mesh implant. Then the skin is pulled down and excess is excised. The navel is sutured into a specially made incision and then a cosmetic suture is applied.
