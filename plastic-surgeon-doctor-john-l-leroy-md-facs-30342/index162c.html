Smoking Near Time of Surgical Procedure

I strongly insist that my cosmetic plastic surgery patients refrain from
smoking for a minimum of 2-4 weeks before surgery in addition to a 2-4 week
period following the procedure. Because of the nicotine in cigarettes, smoking
interrupts blood circulation which leads to issues with healing. This can lead
to pronounced post-surgical scarring that could tarnish cosmetic results.
