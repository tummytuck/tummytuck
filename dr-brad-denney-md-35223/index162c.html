Can a diabetic person get a Mommy Makeover?
diabetes and mommy makeover
Jun 20, 2018
Hello and thanks for you question. In my practice, I will offer mommy makeover to patients with diabetes. However, I require clearance from their primary care provider who manages their diabetes and I make sure that their diabetes is well controlled with glucose ranges normal. If your glucose control is abnormal, there is significant risk for wound healing and other significant complications.

Can a fat transfer or breast implants be done under local anesthesia?
breast augmentation under local anesthesia
Jun 20, 2018
Hello and thank you for your question. Yes, it is possible to perform a breast augmentation under local anesthesia. It has the benefit of avoiding the nausea associated with general anesthesia as well as any other side effects of general anesthesia. However, even in the most experienced hands, there is some discomfort experienced with augmentation under local. I recommend meeting with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss the different modalities possible for sedation.
