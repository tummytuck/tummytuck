When is Lipo used Vs TT?
Liposuction versus tummy tuck
Oct 5, 2015
Liposuction only remove the excess fat on the abdominal skin flap without removing excess skin. There is some skin tightening effect after liposuction but it is unpredictable. Tummy tuck removes excess fatty tissue, skin, and repair the laxity of the abdominal wall muscles. From your pics, it appears that you have all these three components. It is likely that you will need a tummy tuck to give the desired cosmetic result. Good luck in your journey.
