Tummy tuck in Austin, Texas

Tummy tuck in Austin

Correction of the abdomen in Austin with using a surgical method called a tummy tuck. With tummy tuck, you can solve the problem of saggy belly, that lost its shape as a result of distension of the muscle and excess skin. The first such surgery was performed in 1899 for young woman with a large, pendulous abdomen. The surgery was successful, the shape of the stomach is back to normal, and since then the popularity of tummy tuck is only growing.

A tummy tuck in Austin does not pursue its goal the removal of adipose tissue, unlike liposuction, so it should not be done in order to reduce weight, although surgeons of Austin are practiced variety of operations that can be combined with liposuction (endoscopic tummy tuck, mini tummy tuck)

The complexity and variations of tummy tuck in Austin.

All surgery on the abdomen are very complex, tummy tuck is no exception. From surgeon is required large skill and extreme accuracy in order to achieve the desired result without damaging vital organs, so in Austin to tummy tuck are allowed only qualified doctors who have been trained and have extensive practical experience. The patient is undergoing tests, which determine its state of health, then the surgeon chooses the procedure of tummy tuck based on the patient’s anatomy.

There are several variations of this surgery in Austin:

Tummy tuck in Austin, Texas

  • Mini abdominoplasty. It differs from the classical tummy tuck with a smaller size of cuts and interference, accordingly, fewer complications and shorter recovery. In its turn abdominoplasty in Austin divided into the following modifications: the umbilical tummy tuck; mini tummy tuck with a cut in the bikini area without affecting the navel; mini tummy tuck with excision of the skin. The operation in the clinics Austin is often combined with liposuction of the anterior abdominal wall.
  • Classical or standard tummy tuck. Incision in the bikini area, removes excess skin, sutured the muscles of the abdominal wall, the navel is formed again.
  • Extended tummy tuck. Applied with significant sagging skin not only on the abdomen, but also on the sides, upper thighs, and even the back. Cuts on the sides of the lower back.
  • Endoscopic abdominoplasty in Austin . It is the least traumatic of all, but is carried out only in cases where it is necessary to tighten muscles. There is no opportunity to remove excess skin with this surgery. Perhaps a liposuction during surgery.

Techniques of conducting, the terms and conditions in Austin.

Tummy tuck Austin, TX

Surgery in Austin Hospital takes between 2 and to 5 hours, depending on the level of complexity. Produced mainly under general anesthesia, in some cases under epidural (spinal) anesthesia. Through the incision is excised and removed excess tissue, the abdominal muscles are tightened and sutured. The wound was sutured, drain is inserted into the seam to drain the fluid. Time in the hospital – two or three days after surgery for monitoring recovery. Then the patient is discharged, and another 5-7 days, he goes to hospital for bandages. After abdominoplasty you should wear compression (squeezing) postoperative underwear, to the omission did not happen as a result of a temporary weakening of the abdominal wall.

As with any surgery, this one has the indications and contraindications. Indications are: the patient’s desire to correct figure flaws, the presence of umbilical or other types of hernias, which are sewn together with abdominoplasty. Contraindications: diabetes, decompensated chronic diseases, cancer, mental illness, planned in the coming year pregnancy.

Cosmetic effects of a tummy tuck

Tummy tuck Austin, Texas

A month after the surgery a person can completely return to normal activities, including exercise, but the results of all operations are seen at least half a year later, as required for a full recovery.

Austin cosmetic surgeons use a technique of incisions and stitches that make postoperative scar almost invisible. He thready, located in places that are either covered with linen or the hairy part of the pubis, and as a rule, after a  year becomes virtually invisible.

Usually, if doctor and the clinic are chosen responsibly , the result of surgery is very impressive. Ugly fat “aprons”, pendulous abdomen remain in the past, replaced with the slim waist and a nice flat tummy.
