How is a tummy tuck performed San Francisco

Depending on the condition of the patient, the planned duration of procedure and other related factors, abdominoplasty is performed day hospital care or in a short hospitalization. Usually surgery takes about three hours and is done under general anesthesia. At the first stage the surgeon frees abdominal wall, cuts it in the right places for access to the subcutaneous tissue and fat layers, then stitched the fascia of the abdomen and removes excess skin and subcutaneous tissue. As a result, the shape of the abdomen acquires flat contours, and the skin becomes firmer. At the final stage superimposed cosmetic seams, which will soon disappear.

The first few days the patient feels moderate pain on the outer wall of the abdomen. Pain should be present, because this as part of a successful tummy tuck. The healing process of cuts must take place by natural means, without the use of tools for the early scarring. In this case, all the scars gradually become invisible.

The medical personnel of clinic in San Francisco will help to pass the postoperative period in a comfortable environment, faster to get up and join the usual active life. Doctors will track blood supply of the treated area, and if necessary apply anticoagulants, prophylactic medications that prevent rapid blood clotting to prevent blood clots.

For six weeks after surgery is recommended constant wearing a bandage that will not let develop edema and contribute to the formation of abdominal fascia in the new parameters. The final results of the tummy tuck will be visible after three months when fully over the healing process.
