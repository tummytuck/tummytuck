How does tummy tuck work

Tummy tuck Wichita KS

In Wichita testimonials to a tummy tuck are considered the following situations:

  • The need to remove excess fat at impossibility of liposuction
  • The excess skin and subcutaneous fat on the abdomen
  • Expressed stretching
  • Severe scarring left after the surgery in the abdomen

One of the indications for abdominoplasty is to conduct additional manipulation after liposuction, If pumping of excess subcutaneous fat does not help, and there is a need tightening of stretched skin, as in severe cases the patient has omission of the stomach tissue – pronounced ptosis.

Technique of a tummy tuck in Wichita depends on several factors: the condition of the skin, the amount and thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer, the availability of so-called “stretch marks”, existing hernias and other factors.

Tummy tuck in Wichita

    • Most gentle surgery, which is held in the lower abdomen with minimal incisions, called a mini-abdominoplasty in Wichita. When this surgery removes excess skin below the navel. Simultaneously may be carried liposuction.
    • A more complex variant provides transverse incisions in the area of ​​the pubis and the navel. It is through them made ​​liposuction, further tightening of the skin, eliminates stretching of the abdominal muscles. Such surgery qualifies as a classic or standard abdominoplasty.
    • During extensive tummy tuck in Wichita removes excess fatty tissue and skin are not only on the abdomen, but also on the back or hips. For this is done two cuts – above the pubis and around the navel.
    • Surgery for lift the abdominal muscles without removing the skin is called endoscopic tummy tuck. It is performed through several incisions in the pubic area.

Before tummy tuck Wichita

 

  • For people who want to remove a large amount of skin and adipose tissue, used vertical tummy tuck. Thus, as in the mini-abdominoplasty is performed sectional view abdomen but this incision is very long, and sometimes in addition thereto also performs vertical section.
  • Tummy tuck surgery in Wichita performed under general anesthesia. Its duration, depending on the complexity of intervention may be 2-4 hours. Within 2-3 days after surgery, the patient is in hospital. In general, the recovery period after this surgery can last from 4 months to 1 year.
