Can all of this be done at the same time? What would you do?
Mommy makeover surgery
Jul 24, 2019
From the photos, you need breast lift on both sides with or without implants. This procedure includes areola reduction. Nipple reduction is a simple procedure that can be done under local anesthesia. Although combining all these procedures is possible, it does increase your risk of deep venous thrombosis (DVT) and pulmonary embolus (PE), a complication that can be deadly. You should consult with board-certified plastic surgeon regarding this and ways to lower the risks.

I had a anchor lift with implant removal. What would cause this?
Complication after anchor lift
Dec 3, 2016
Hello. Sorry to see that you had complication following your anchor lift and implant removal. It is difficult to make a diagnosis by photographs alone but it appears that you had necrosis of portion of the nipple-areola complex on that breast. This can be caused by infection or a lack of blood supply to the area or both. The photograph does not show all good tissues yet so healing will take a while. Please be sure to follow-up with your surgeon for proper wound care to facilitate healing….
After 3 large babies my stomach is stretched out very badly, what kind of Tummy Tuck will I need to get a
Flat tummy – Is it possible?
Nov 8, 2016
Thank you for your inquiry and photos. Assuming that you are in good health, you are a candidate for a full tummy tuck with fascial plication and liposuction. However, without examination, it is not possible to say whether the procedure will give you a completely flat tummy or not. You should consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon for a more accurate assessment. Best wishes and hope everything turns out well.
