Tummy Tuck after c-section

Hello and thank you for the question! A previous C-section will not complicate a tummy tuck and are routinely done. Please make an appointment with a board certified plastic surgeon in your area for a personalized and in-depth evaluation. Carefully review before and after photos and ask questions and share any concerns. Best of luck on your journey.
