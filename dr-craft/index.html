Unbearable Pain in my Right Breast, Which Has an Implant. Does It Need to Be Removed?
Painful Breast- what to do?
Sep 24, 2013
Sound like you have a severe capsular contracture which is a scar envelope surrounding the implant causing this pain. A capsule is normal following an implant but in 20% patients this capsule can get progressively tighter to the point that it causes pain. The treatment is releasing or removing the capsule and putting the implant back into the new released pocket and start aggressive post operative massage to help prevent capsule tightening from reoccurring.
