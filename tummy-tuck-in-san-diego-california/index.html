Tummy tuck in San diego, California

Tummy tuck in San Diego is an operation that is aimed at toning the abdominal wall, removing excess of hanging skin and fatty tissue. It allows you to get taut and flat tummy.

Any woman in San Diego dreams about a thin waist and flat stomach. Unfortunately, not so easy, and at times impossible to achieve the desired result, while working out and controlling your diet. In such cases, to the aid comes an abdominoplasty – cosmetic surgery that turns shapeless body in an attractive figure.

A modern abdominoplasty in San Diego is widely demanded and rather complicated procedure, which leads to a decrease in the volume of the stomach. Of course, abdominoplasty and liposuction are not fully eliminate the obesity. But as additional methods to combat obesity, they are perfect for the shape correction in certain places.

Before and after tummy tuck in San Diego

San Diego plastic surgeons recommend abdominoplasty in the following cases:

  • If significantly reduced muscle tone in the abdominal area (mainly observed in the post-partum period) ;
  • When you stretch the skin (postpartum striae) ;
  • In the case of formation of excess fat in the adipose tissue of the anterior abdominal wall;
  • If excess skin fold (“apron”) was formed in the lower abdomen;
  • These readings are not absolute. Depending on the situation, to the patient may be offered a more forgiving alternative to tummy tuck, for example, lipolysis, liposuction, hardware methods of correction.
