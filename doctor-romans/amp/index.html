Can I get a Tummy Tuck if I have had several surgeries on my stomach?
abdominoplasty abdominal scars
Aug 19, 2017
Scars that are below the umbilicus generally do not affect the ability to do an abdominoplasty since this tissue will be removed if you have enough extra tissue. Scars above the umbilicus can interfere with skin circulation and the elasticity of the tissues that are being stretched to tighten the skin.
Go see a qualified Plastic Surgeon for a consultation because only an in-person exam can tell you the affect your scars will have on an abdominoplasty.
