Can I do a collagen powder supplement after ARTAS?
Nutrtional supplements
Jul 18, 2019
Resuming your nutritional supplements should be fine after your ARTAS procedure. It's always good to go over your medications and supplements with your surgeon before and after your procedure to discuss the possibility of ant of them having an effect on your procedure both before and after.
Does microneedling/PRP plump the face or does it just build collagen that works on the skin quality?
Microneedling won't plump you up
Jul 17, 2019
Any micro needling treatment you get is more of a superficial treatment that promotes collagen production in your skin resulting in improved skin texture and often helps with acne scarring. Adding Radiofrequency like the Profound to the micro needling helps improve the effects even more. Any plumping of the face has to do with the amount of fatty tissue in your face that degrades with time resulting in a deflated appearance but that can be compensated for with fillers.
What kind of medical procedure can fix my acne scarring between my eyebrows?
Progressive treatments may be best
Jul 17, 2019
You may want to start with micro needling as a first option and progress to more aggressive treatments. Microneedling with PRP and even radio frequency micro needling can help improve the appearance of scars but having fillers done in this area is risky as it could cause necrosis of the soft tissue in the region and possibly even blindness. None of these treatments will 100% get rid of the scarring but they will help improve the appearance. Best of luck!
Do I need more filler in my lips to correct the unevenness?
Subtle asymmetry
Jul 17, 2019
The amount of asymmetry you have is subtle and I would not recommend any further filler to balance it out. If you would like you can discuss with your injector if you need more time, more massaging or if you would benefit form having some of it or all of it dissolved and starting over.
Is it possible for the nose to grow as a side effect of fillers and laser treatments?
Nose not growing
Jul 17, 2019
Based on the treatments that you have had, there should not be any concern that they have caused your nose to grow. As time progresses, our facial features change with deflation and sagging of tissue which may cause an appearance of changes to other structures but the nose does not continue to grow with age after a certain point.
Also keep in mind that different lighting can accentuate different features of your face that you may have not noticed before.

Possibly a Suture
Jul 17, 2019
Most likely it is a suture that is working its way to the surface which can happen after surgery. It would be best to discuss this with your plastic surgeon and ask if the sutures they used in this area were dissolvable. This may pass with time or the suture material may surface. Discuss with your plastic surgeon if they think you need antibiotics. Depending on what you had done and if there is an implant present they may decide to put you on antibiotics or not.
