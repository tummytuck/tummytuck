Labiaplasty

Is constipation normal after tummy tuck?
Constipation
Nov 28, 2015
This is almost always from the pain medication that you are taking and that you received during surgery. Definitely use a stool softener to get ahead of the constipation so you don’t have a problem going forward.
My tummy feels as hard as a brick since my tummy tuck with lip. How long will it last?
Tummy tuck hardness
Nov 28, 2015
At 8 days you are not even close to your final result. You will soften and the swelling will go down quite a bit over the next few weeks. You will be almost at your final result at 2 months, but sometimes it takes a bit longer. If you are experiencing this as new hardness, swelling, or redness then make sure you see your surgeon immediately. Otherwise, just give it time.

Is the risk of implant rotation or displacement limited to the first few weeks? Or can it happen even
Implant movement
Nov 28, 2015
Implants can move anytime, but after the first few months it is extremely unlikely as the implant has formed a capsule around it which holds it in place.If it looks like it has moved after that point, it is likely either a capsular contracture where the capsule get thick and displaces the implant…or the breast itself has changed.

I’m going to need a full areola incision with the called cookie cutter, is that going to kill the ner
Nipple sensation
May 28, 2015
The incision itself around the nipple does not increase risk for loss of sensation, but the breast augmentation is what puts the nipple at risk. Patients do often have a change in nipple sensation right after surgery for a couple months (either hypersensitive (too sensitive) or hyposensitive (loss of sensation)), but this goes away in the large majority of patients.
