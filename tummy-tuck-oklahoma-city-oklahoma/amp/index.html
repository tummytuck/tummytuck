Tummy tuck Oklahoma City, Oklahoma

Tummy tuck in Oklahoma City

Tummy tuck in Oklahoma City is a surgical procedure aimed at toning the abdominal muscles (the so-called “tightening of muscles”) and the removal of excess fat and skin. This surgery is often used by women after pregnancy, as well as people after a quick weight loss.

During pregnancy there is a stretching and loss of tone of the abdominal muscles, which leads to its sagging. The same occurs with rapid weight loss. Quite often, tummy tuck is combined with liposuction. If, within 6 months after giving birth abdominal skin was not reduced – hence, in the following, it is not reduced.

Using a cross incision, he removed the skin and fat flap of 7.5 kg. From this moment surgical technique has improved significantly.

Medical reasons for tummy tuck in Oklahoma City

Tummy tuck Oklahoma City

The most common reason for the formation of excess skin and fat layer of the abdomen is pregnancy, especially repeated. During pregnancy the abdominal skin is stretched more than its ability to decrease back. Also stretched musculo-aponeurotic layer. The result is a stretched thinned skin and fat flap and separated abdominal muscles (diastasis recti). Losing weight after childbirth also contribute to this process. If the skin for 6 months is not too short – so it subsequently has not reduced. Great weight loss as a result of different diets or due to operations also leads to an excess of skin and fat layer and the weakness of the anterior abdominal wall.

Thus, there are 2 main reasons for the “saggy” stomach:

  • excess skin and fat layer;
  • muscle weakness of the anterior abdominal wall.

Excess skin and fat layer is most noticeable in the area of ​​the navel.

Tummy tuck in Oklahoma City is divided into the following types:

Tummy tuck scar Oklahoma City

Full tummy tuck Oklahoma City

During this surgery, excess skin and excess fat removed from the upper and lower abdomen. After that your abdominal muscles are tightened and navel moves.

Mini tummy tuck

During this simple surgery excess fat (adipose tissue) and skin is removed below the navel. In this case, the muscles are stretched, but the navel is not displaced – so he can stay in unnatural positions

Abdominoplasty Oklahoma City

Endoscopic tummy tuck

During this procedure, small incisions are made with minimal intervention using a small camera. Muscles are not stretched, unlike full abdominoplasty.

Tummy tuck lipo

During this surgery, the fat is removed above and below the umbilicus, which is moved. Muscles are not stretched. Tummy tuck lipo suitable for those who have a very flabby skin.

Other methods include reverse tummy tuck tummy tuck and tummy tuck with vertical scars.
