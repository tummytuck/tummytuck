Tummy tuck in Fresno, California

Abdominoplasty Fresno

For surgical correction of excess skin and subcutaneous fat in the abdomen is performed tummy tuck. Such surgery is equally in demand in Fresno clinics in both men and women. At the same time patient, age ranges from 18 to 60 years.

Indications for abdominoplasty in Fresno

The main recommendations for surgery include:

  • the presence of skin and fat “apron” in the middle and lower abdomen;
  • severe sagging of the skin and stretch marks formed as a result of drastic weight loss / in the postpartum period among women;
  • the loss of the natural elasticity of the skin and muscle tone;
  • excess fat and skin in case of deviations of recti;
  • the need to remove the post-surgical scars and hernias.

Another reason for performing abdominoplasty in Fresno is the presence in the patient contraindications to liposuction.

It should be noted that a tummy tuck does not apply to methods of weight loss and is not aimed at the removal of irregular fat deposits. In most cases, in Fresno practiced simultaneous liposuction and abdominoplasty, that allows to achieve maximum aesthetic effect.

Contraindications to abdominoplasty in Fresno

Tummy tuck Fresno

Tummy tuck can not be performed when the patient has a number of diseases, including:

  • heart failure;
  • the last stages of obesity;
  • arterial hypertension;
  • psychiatric disorders;
  • a bleeding disorder;
  • presence of postoperative scar tissue in the upper abdomen.

Patients with diabetes undergo further examination on the basis of which a final decision on the tummy tuck. Women, that in the coming year planning to become pregnant, abdominoplasty is not recommended.

Tummy tuck procedures in Fresno

After conducting the necessary diagnostic measures and taking into account the wishes of the patient, the surgeon selects the technology of tummy tuck. Currently, doctors of Fresno actively using the following techniques tummy tuck:

Before tummy tuck in Fresno

  • Mini tummy tuck in Fresno. Excision of skin and fat apron, located in the lower abdomen, is made without moving the navel. Incision is made in the bikini area. Allows you to eliminate stretch marks, to simulate the anterior abdominal wall, remove fat deposits. Due to minimally invasive surgery, the recovery period is reduced as much as possible.
  • Full tummy tuck in Fresno. Provides a complete correction of the abdomen, hips and back. During surgery excess fat removed using the cannulae. The incision is held in the lower abdomen and ends at the waist. In accordance with the pre-applied labels, moving of navel is carried to a new place with full vitality and neural conductivity of its tissues. Carrying of extended tummy tuck in Fresno takes about 4,5-5 hours. In this case, the rehabilitation period lasts about 2 months.
  • Endoscopic abdominoplasty in Fresno. With the help of special equipment, trocars are introduced into abdominal wall, and with simultaneously computer control, the surgeon stitches the stretched muscles. The length of the incision in this case is 10-20 mm. If necessary, liposuction is performed at the same time. This technology is recommended in case of sagging and loss of tone of the abdominal muscles to maintain good skin elasticity.

Surgical procedures are performed under local anesthesia. The doctor takes photos of the operated stomach area before and after the removal of sutures, that patients can assess the high aesthetic effect of abdominoplasty in Fresno.

Tummy tuck recovery in Fresno.

Fresno tummy tuck

Depending on the type of abdominoplasty, the recovery period can take anywhere from a week to several months. All patients compulsorily must wear a special compression garment. During the first few days is mandatory installation of drainage tubes to drain the fluid from the wound. Postoperative swelling and redness disappear, usually on 7-20 days after a tummy tuck. Physical activity is limited to six months.

For performing abdominoplasty in Fresno is used only most advanced operating equipment. The highest level of skill of plastic surgeons can get rid of any aesthetic defects.
