Tummy Tuck or Lipo alone?

Lipo tummy tuck surgery

Your question is a very common one. The short answer is that Lipo alone removes fat deposits. It does nothing specifically to the abdominal wall musculature or skin and therefore typically will not produce results that a post-partum woman is satisfied with. ie. a flat stomach.

A tummy-tuck removes excess skin and fat, but more importantly, it addresses the underlying laxity of the abdominal wall musculature, restoring the abdominal wall tone and contour to your pre-pregnancy condition.

This is the real “magic” of a tummy-tuck operation. When you’re serious in pursuing your options, schedule a consultation with a Plastic Surgeon, Board Certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery.

Could I do lipo or do I need a tummy tuck?

From you photo you may be a candidate for either lipo or a tummy tuck. The tummy tuck will probably result in a tighter, smaller waist line. However, be aware that no surgeon or surgery can guarantee a specific waist measurement.

Seek out a board certified plastic surgeon with extensive tummy tuck experience. (Vincent D. Lepore, MD, San Jose Plastic Surgeon)
