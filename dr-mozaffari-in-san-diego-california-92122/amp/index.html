Can epicanthoplasty or blepharoplasty widen eyes horizontally?
Long term effects
May 14, 2020
I would not recommend such a procedure as risks are far greater than the possible benefits for such a surgery. Lower eyelid anatomy is very complex and changing the position of the eyelid in a manner that you are seeking can lead to multiple problems which are not easy to fix. Also you have to consider the long term effects of how your eyelid will look and function in 20-30 years from now as eyelids relax more and tear production goes down.
