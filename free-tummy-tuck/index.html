Free tummy tuck surgery

Free tummy tuck – a surgical procedure aimed at correcting excess skin, fatty tissue, stretched muscles of the anterior abdominal wall and fascia, in other words – a reduction of the abdomen.

Free tummy tuck contest

Causes of these changes are related to genetic characteristics, pregnancy, childbirth, obesity. During free tummy tuck along with excess skin below the navel is removed stretch marks caused by abrupt changes in weight or pregnancy.

Sometimes an imperfect view of the abdomen is compounded by the umbilical hernia and diastasis (divergence) of the rectus muscles, which is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also can lead to herniation of the white line of the abdomen. If such defects are present, they removed during free abdominoplasty. Accumulation of adipose tissue is most often occurs in the abdomen and usually can not be repaired with the help of exercise and dieting.

Its excess is also removed during this surgery, muscular corset stomach is reduced and the stomach becomes flat, acquiring attractive contour. Free plastic surgery tummy tuck is not recommended for women who plan to become pregnant in the future.

Tummy tuck is usually performed under general anesthesia and lasts 1.5-3 hours. The incision extends through the bikini. Free stomach reduction surgery involves detachment and subsequent excision of skin and subcutaneous tissue of the abdomen.

In cases of postpartum deformation, when stretched not only the skin, but also the muscles, the surgeon not only removes excess skin, but restores muscular corset abdomen. During the surgery usually occurs transfer and plastic of navel.

If necessary, free abdominoplasty supplemented by paid liposuction sides, back and other parts of the body. A distinctive result of such surgery is to achieve a lasting effect, minimal incision and aesthetic result.
