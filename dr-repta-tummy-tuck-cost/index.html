Has my tummy tuck failed? Why is my skin still so loose?
Loose skin appears present
Dec 5, 2018
Good question.  Its hard to know what occurred during your tummy tuck.  It does appear that you have some residual loose skin.  With time as welling subsides this may improve.  If it does not do so to your satisfaction a TT revision to remove more skin will be needed.Good luck with the remainder of your recovery.Dr. Remus Repta
Am I a good candidate for a BBL and other surgeries?
All depends on how much intra-abdominal fat you have
Nov 6, 2018
Great question.  Fat under the skin can be liposuctioned, skin can be removed, and muscles can be tightened.  Fat inside your abdomen called intra-abdominal fat or visceral fat cannot be changed by surgery and requires a chance in diet primarily.  Overall, I think liposuction, tummy tuck, and buttock fat transfer would be the ideal procedures for you (not considering the intra-abdominal fat part).Good luck with your surgery.Dr. Remus Repta
