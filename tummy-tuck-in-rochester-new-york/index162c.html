Tummy tuck in Rochester, New York

Tummy tuck Rochester NY

A tummy tuck is designed for patients in Rochester, which in a short time got rid of a significant weight loss, as a result of the surgical treatment of obesity or for other reasons. Says William Koenig, an expert in the field of aesthetic and reconstructive surgery, a leading plastic surgeon plastic surgery department of the medical center in Rochester.

In most cases, tummy tuck is required for patients who have undergone bariatric surgery. In such cases, after weight loss are often left excess skin, disturbing live normal life. These surpluses are formed due to such property as the elasticity of the skin. Skin gradually stretched with weight gain, but with a sharp decrease weight, it is not reduced to its initial dimensions, and remains “hang” on the abdomen, upper arms, buttocks and other parts of the body.

Rochester tummy tuck

Before tummy tuck Rochester

Apart from unsightly appearance, these folds are the ideal place for the development of fungal infections, wounds and ulcers. In some cases, when the skin was stretched “to the limit” there is no other way to rectify the situation as soon as surgically. This surgery is performed only after the process of weight loss,
when achieved body weight remains stable for at least six months. Plan of operations in Rochester made ​​individually, taking into account the initial data and the patient’s wishes.

Abdominoplasty is performed under general anesthesia. The surgeon performs a cross-section in the lower abdomen, in the course of skin folds. Then he separates the skin and subcutaneous tissue from the muscles to the level of the chest, and then it performs a circular incision around the navel. On next stage the doctor using sutures strengthens weakened abdominal muscles and removes excess skin and fat and tightens the remaining skin from top to bottom, where it establishes in the skin folds. At the end of surgery moves a navel at its new location and installs drainage, through which, in the first days after surgery will be to move away excess fluid from the abdominal cavity.

Abdominoplasty Rochester

After classical abdominoplasty in Rochester left two scars: in lower abdomen and around the umbilicus.

Like any other surgery, abdominoplasty is not devoid of a certain operational risk. Complications of this type of surgery are relatively rare and include bleeding, formation of seroma (fluid accumulation), problems with the healing of the wound, disruption of the wound, damage of nervous structures, unsightly view navel or suboptimal results of surgery.

All this and much more is discussed by surgeon and the patient in the pre-meeting, during which the survey is conducted and gives instructions in preparation for surgery. In the postoperative period there may be some pain in the abdomen, which is controlled by conventional painkillers. Typically, patients are discharged from the hospital the next day. Drains are removed within a few days after discharge.

In most cases, patients expressed lot of satisfaction the results of the operation, because it not only improves their appearance, but also allows you to fully return to public life, saving them from embarrassment and awkwardness due to skin folds.
