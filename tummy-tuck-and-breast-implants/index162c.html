Tummy tuck and breast implants

You should wait at least 4-6 months after having your baby before considering any mommy makeover procedures.

You need to lose that baby weight and allow your breasts and tummy to settle down before considering any procedures. (C. Bob Basu, MD, FACS, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

Abdominoplasty and breast implants after preganancy – wait 6 months

It is probably safe to consider these operations 6 months after your pregnancy. There are many hormonal changes that occur in a woman’s body during and after pregnancy and you want to wait until your body is back to its normal state. (Jeffrey Zwiren, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)

Tummy Tuck and Breast implants after Having a Baby

The tummy tuck and breast implants

These procedures are reasonable to do together and are safe but not “done right away.” You need to wait at least six months after you deliver or stop breast feeding.

This lets your hormones, the breasts and your abdomen return to normal. Otherwise you are at higher risk for blood clots or having recurrent deformities. (Robert T. Buchanan, MD, Highlands Plastic Surgeon)

It’s relevant to know if you’re having another baby. Once you’re done, go find a board certified plastic surgeon and get your mommie makeover. (Ricardo A. Meade, MD, Dallas Plastic Surgeon)

Tummy tuck and breast augmentation after a baby

Tummy tuck and breast implants photo

If you are pregnant and then deliver, a tummy tuck and breast augmentation can not be done at the same time. You will have to wait at least 6 months or longer for things to go back to “normal.” (Steven Wallach, MD, Manhattan Plastic Surgeon)

For Mommy Makeovers in San Antonio, I generally recommend patients wait for at least 6 months after delivering and breast feeding before surgery. Operating too soon can lead to complications and an unsatisfactory cosmetic result. I would recommend you visit several Plastic Surgeons after you deliver and then choose the one you are most comfortable with. (Suresh Koneru, MD, San Antonio Plastic Surgeon)

Combined Breast Implants and Tummy Tuck After Having A Baby

It is very common to do a combined procedure of breast implants and a tummy tuck, also known as a Mommy Makeover.

Tummy tuck surgery and breast implants

But one will need to wait a minimum of three, and preferably six, months after delivering before having ir done to make sure you have lost all the baby weight. (Barry L. Eppley, MD, DMD, Indianapolis Plastic Surgeon)

It is best to wait at least 6 months after birth of baby to have a Tummy Tuck and Breast Augmentation

Once you have delivered your baby and have stopped producing breast milk, the tummy tuck and breast augmentation can be performed. It is often helpful to reach a stable or goal weight after pregnancy as well prior to surgery. Find a plastic surgeon with ELITE credentials who performs hundreds of breast augmentations and tummy tucks each year. Then look at the plastic surgeon’s website before and after photo galleries to get a sense of who can deliver the results. (Kenneth B. Hughes, MD, Los Angeles Plastic Surgeon)
