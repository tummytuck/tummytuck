Indications for abdominoplasty in New York:

  • The inability to carry out liposuction;
  • Inability to correct excess skin or fat in the abdominal area by other methods (diet, exercise, etc..)

  • Strongly expressed streamers;

Contraindications of tummy tuck in New York:

Abdominoplasty in New York is not carried out if the patient have

  • Postoperative scars which are located above the navel;
  • Diabetes mellitus;
  • Cardiac or pulmonary insufficiency;
  • Clear signs of obesity.

Also, tummy tuck is not conducted to patients who are planning to become pregnant within one year after the surgery.
