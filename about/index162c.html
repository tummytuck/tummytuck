About Andrew J. Kaczynski, MD

Slender waist plays an important role in the overall attractiveness of the figure. Taut belly and waist increases self-confidence and well-being of both women and men.

The desire to look attractive, to have natural outlines and the shape of the body is conditioned by the modern way of life.

At the same time, many women and men have a deformity of the abdomen, caused by various causes.

Defects of appearance often contribute to the emergence of a complex of emotional and social inferiority. In addition, excessive deposition of subcutaneous fat on the anterior abdominal wall and, as a rule, concomitant obesity of various degrees, have a significant negative impact on the functional state of internal organs and systems.

Andrew Kaczynski, MD

In addition, the relaxation of the musculo-aponeurotic layer, the increase in the volume of the abdomen is accompanied by the descent of the internal organs and the diaphragm, a decrease in intra-abdominal pressure. Often this leads to various diseases, such as atony of the stomach and intestines, dyspeptic disorders, spontaneous abdominal pains, constipation, urinary incontinence.

Abdominoplasty is a plastic surgery to remove excess fat and sagging skin of the anterior abdomen, during which the muscles of the abdominal wall are also tightened.

Tummy tucks significantly improves appearance of the sagging abdomen. The best results can be achieved by patients who are in good physical shape, but whose “skin-fat apron” does not respond to physical exercises or to a diet.

Andrew Kaczynski, MD with Office Manager

Changing the skin and subcutaneous tissues over the abdominal muscles with the help of workout, massage and other similar methods is possible only to a certain extent, because in many cases we are talking about ruptures of supporting structures (for example, pregnancy scars). A slender waist can be the main dream and after positive changes in the way of life, when the patient loses excess weight and regularly exercises properly.In such cases, abdominal plastic surgery is a good opportunity to improve the appearance of the unaesthetic abdominal area.

Plastic surgeons are called radical psychologists, because they eliminate psychological complexes operatively, using innovations of modern medicine.
