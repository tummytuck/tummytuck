This section is intended for patients who have had plastic surgery or are going to do it.

Abdominoplasty is a surgical procedure aimed at toning of abdominal muscles and the removal of excess fat and skin.

This operation is often used by women after pregnancy, as well as people after a quick weight loss.

Tummy tuck reviews of patients

There is a tension and loss of tone of the abdominal muscles in pregnancy, which leads to its sagging.

The same occurs with rapid weight loss. Abdominoplasty is often combined with liposuction procedure.

Tummy tuck reviews will help you understand whether you need a tummy tuck or not, point to the possible complications after surgery, show experience real people, warn against wrong actions.
