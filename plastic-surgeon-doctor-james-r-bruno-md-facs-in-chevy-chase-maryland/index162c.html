Fat grafting after breast implant removal
Aug 7, 2017
It is possible to fat graft the breast at the time of implant removal. There are many factors as to how much fat can be placed successfully in each breast based on fat availability and thickness of the residual breast skin after implant removal. From your picture it is likely a lift will be needed to remove exceed skin and reshape the breast.
Please discuss all of these questions and concerns with you Board Certified Plastic Siregeon. All the best.....
