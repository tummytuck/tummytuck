Fat Injection Depth

Can something be done about my nose becoming more symmetrical and small without rhinoplasty? (Photo
Improving nasal appearance and size
Feb 17, 2020
Hi, thanks for the question. The short answer is no, your nose cannot be shaped and resized in this area without rhinoplasty. Your nasal tip is made up of a complex 3D framework of cartilage and specialized soft tissue that must be surgically altered to become smaller and more symmetric. After these cartilages have completed growing, there is no taping or external adjustments that can change the form of your nose.

How can I treat the wrinkles in my labia majora?
Labia wrinkles
Feb 14, 2020
Often wrinkles of the labia majora are caused by atrophy (thinning) of the tissue under the skin. Injection of a small amount of your own fat into the area is my usual recommended treatment for this concern. I highly recommend consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss treatment.

Is it possible getting empty nose syndrome symptoms just by getting nasal spreaders grafts?
Empty Nose Syndrome
Feb 5, 2020
Thanks for the question. Since spreader grafts by definition decrease the size of the airway (in addition to preventing loss of midvault support), they cannot by themselves cause "empty nose syndrome." This syndrome is typically the result of overreducing the turbinates. A similar sensation could be achieved by a very large septal perforation.
